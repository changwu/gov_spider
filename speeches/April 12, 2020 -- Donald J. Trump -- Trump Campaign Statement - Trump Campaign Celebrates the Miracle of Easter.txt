
    In observance of Easter Sunday, Lara Trump, Senior Advisor to Donald J. Trump for President, Inc., issued the following statement.
"The celebration of Christ's Resurrection at Easter brings joy and hope to everyone in this season of renewal. As our nation begins to emerge from a crisis unlike any we have ever experienced, President Trump remains dedicated to leading us, safely and swiftly, from a time of great hardship to a future of health, opportunity, and prosperity for America and the world. We wish heartfelt blessings and peace upon Christians everywhere as we celebrate the miracle of the Resurrection on this very special Easter Sunday."
