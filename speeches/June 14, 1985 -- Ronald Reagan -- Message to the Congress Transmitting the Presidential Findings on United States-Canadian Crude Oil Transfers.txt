
    To the Congress of the United States:
I hereby transmit the requisite findings and determinations to permit the export of non-Alaskan North Slope crude oil to Canada. These findings and determinations are made pursuant to section 28 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act (43 U.S.C. 1354), section 28(u) of the Mineral Lands Leasing Act (30 U.S.C. 185(u)), and section 103 of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act (42 U.S.C. 6212).
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
June 14, 1985.
