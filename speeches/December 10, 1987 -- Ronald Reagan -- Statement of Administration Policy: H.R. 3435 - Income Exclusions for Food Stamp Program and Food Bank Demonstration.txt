
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Panetta (D) CA and Emerson (R) MO)
The administration has no objection to House passage of H.R. 3435, which would: (1) provide that certain cash charitable donations and payments received for blood donations would not be counted as income for purposes of the Food Stamp program; and (2) provide for the Department of Agriculture to conduct a food bank demonstration project.
