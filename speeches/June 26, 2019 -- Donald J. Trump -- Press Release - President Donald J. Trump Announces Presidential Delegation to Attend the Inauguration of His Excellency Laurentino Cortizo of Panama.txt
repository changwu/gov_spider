
    Today, President Donald J. Trump announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Panama City, Panama, to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Laurentino Cortizo on July 1, 2019.
The Honorable Wilbur Ross, Secretary of Commerce, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
Mr. T. Ulrich Brechbühl, Counselor of the Department, U.S. Department of State
Ms. Roxanne Cabral, Chargé d'Affaires, U.S. Embassy Panama
The Honorable Marshall Billingslea, Assistant Secretary of the Treasury for Terrorist Financing, U.S. Department of the Treasury
The Honorable Mauricio Claver-Carone, Special Assistant to the President and Senior Director for Western Hemisphere Affairs, National Security Council
Mrs. Julie Chung, Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary of State for Western Hemisphere Affairs, U.S. Department of State
Mr. Tim Smucker, Member, Advisory Council for Trade Policy Negotiations; Chairman Emeritus, The J.M. Smucker Company
Mr. Jay Collins, Jr., Vice Chairman of Banking, Capital Markets & Advisory, Citigroup
