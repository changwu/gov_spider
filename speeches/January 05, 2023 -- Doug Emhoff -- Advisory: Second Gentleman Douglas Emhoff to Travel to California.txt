
    On Monday, January 9th, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will travel to Los Angeles, California to join Secretary Miguel Cardona for a tour of Roybal Film and Television Production Magnet, located on the Edward R. Roybal Learning Complex in the Los Angeles Unified School District (LAUSD). The Second Gentleman and Secretary Cardona will also host a roundtable with students and the school's advisory board members including George Clooney, Mindy Kaling, and Don Cheadle to learn more about teacher support programs and their career and technical programs (CTE).
