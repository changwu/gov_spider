
    On Monday, June 25, 2018, the President signed into law:
S. 1869, the "Whistleblower Protection Coordination Act," which reauthorizes and renames the position of the Whistleblower Ombudsman to be the Whistleblower Protection Coordinator; and
S. 2246, which designates the health care center of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Tallahassee, Florida, as the Sergeant Ernest I. "Boots" Thomas VA Clinic.
