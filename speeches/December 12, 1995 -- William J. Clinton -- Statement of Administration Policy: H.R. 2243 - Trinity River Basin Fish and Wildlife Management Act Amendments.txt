
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Reps. Riggs (R) CA and Herger (R) GA)
The Administration supports House passage of H.R. 2243. During Senate consideration of this legislation, the Administration will seek amendments to: (1) authorize additional appropriations for the Trinity River Basin Fish and Wildlife Management Program, and (2) reauthorize the Program and its Task Force for seven years.
