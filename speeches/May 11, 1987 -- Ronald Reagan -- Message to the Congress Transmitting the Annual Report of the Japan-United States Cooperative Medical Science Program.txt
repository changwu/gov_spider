
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with Section 5(h) of the International Health Research Act of 1960 (Public Law 86-610; 22 U.S.C. 2103(h)), I transmit herewith the Twentieth Annual Report of the U.S.-Japan Cooperative Medical Science Program for the period of July 1985 to July 1986.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
May 11, 1987.
