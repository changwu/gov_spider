
    The President today announced that he will nominate Marion Edey, of Washington, D.C., to be a member of the Council on Environmental Quality. Ms. Edey is volunteer chairman of the League of Conservation Voters.
Edey was born May 17, 1945, in Washington, D.C. She received a B.A. in political science from Reed College in 1968.
In 1969 Edey worked as legislative assistant to Representative Lester Wolff. In 1970 she became chairman of the League of Conservation Voters.
