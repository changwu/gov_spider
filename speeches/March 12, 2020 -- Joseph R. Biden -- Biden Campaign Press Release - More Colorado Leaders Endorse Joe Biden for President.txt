
    Following resounding victories on March 3 and March 10, more leaders across Colorado are endorsing Joe Biden for President.
"Leaders all across the Centennial State know that Joe Biden is the best candidate to unite our country and deliver for families all across the state," said State Senator Leroy Garcia, President of the Colorado Senate. "He will expand access to quality and affordable health care, he will invest in students and teachers, and he'll fight tooth and nail to protect our environment and public lands."
