
    On Friday, September 9th at 6:30 PM ET, as part of the Biden administration's Cancer Moonshot, First Lady Jill Biden will participate in the Philadelphia Phillies' sixth annual "Childhood Cancer Awareness Night" game at Citizens Bank Park in Philadelphia, PA. The First Lady will join the Phillies and Major League Baseball in honoring pediatric cancer patients and survivors and their families at the game. The First Lady's visit will be covered by an expanded pool. For media interested in joining the pool, please RSVP HERE by Thursday 9/8 at 6:00 PM ET.
