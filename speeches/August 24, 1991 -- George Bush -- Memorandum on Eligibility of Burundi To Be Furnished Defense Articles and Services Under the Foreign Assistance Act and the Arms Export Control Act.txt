
    Presidential Determination No. 91-50
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to the authority vested in me by Section 503 of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended (22 U.S.C. 2311), and Section 3(a)(1) of the Arms Export Control Act, as amended (22 U.S.C. 2753(a)(1)), I hereby find that the furnishing, sale, and/or lease of defense articles and services to the Government of Burundi will strengthen the security of the United States and promote world peace.
You are hereby authorized and directed to transmit this determination to the Congress and to publish it in the Federal Register.
GEORGE BUSH
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, August 24, 1991.
