Boston, MA – Today, Governor Mitt Romney announced the members of the New Hampshire Attorneys for Romney Leadership Team. These New Hampshire attorneys will work to make sure Granite State voters know Governor Romney is the candidate best prepared to lead our nation and who will fight attempts to re-write our nation's laws from the bench.
"I am honored that these talented attorneys have agreed to serve as part of my New Hampshire Attorneys Leadership Team. They will help advise our efforts on the issues that affect the legal community and the impact of activist courts, while helping to expand our support and spread my message throughout the Granite State," Governor Romney said.
New Hampshire State Director, Jim Merrill added, "Governor Romney's vision of strengthening America's future and preventing activist courts from re-writing our Constitution is something that we can all support. We look forward to helping Governor Romney continue to build his support throughout the Granite State through Election Day."
