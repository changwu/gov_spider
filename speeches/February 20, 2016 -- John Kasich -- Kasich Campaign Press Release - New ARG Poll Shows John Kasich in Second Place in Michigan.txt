
    A new American Research Group poll shows that John Kasich is in second place among likely Republican primary voters in Michigan.
The poll, which was conducted from Feb. 19-20, 2016, shows John Kasich in second place with 17 percent of the vote, 5 points ahead of Marco Rubio and Ted Cruz and 13 points ahead of Jeb Bush.
