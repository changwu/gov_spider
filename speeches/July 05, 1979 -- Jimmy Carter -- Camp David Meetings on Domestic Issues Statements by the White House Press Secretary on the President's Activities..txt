
    The President has authorized me to state that, pursuant to the agreements reached at the Tokyo summit, he intends to propose at an early date a series of strong measures to restrain United States demand for imported oil.
The President is in the process of assessing major domestic issues which he believes are important to the country and which include, but go beyond, the question of energy.
He will be consulting with a number of individuals whose judgment he respects, both in and outside of government.
