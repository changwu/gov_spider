
    The President today announced that he will nominate Andrew E. Manatos, of Bethesda, Md., to be an Assistant Secretary of Commerce. This is a new position which was created by the signing of S. 1019, the authorization bill for marine programs in the Commerce Department.
Manatos was born December 7, 1944. He received a B.A. and an M.A. from American University. From 1969 to 1973, he was on the staff of the Senate Committee on the Post Office and Civil Service. From 1973 to 1976, he was associate staff director of the Senate Committee on the District of Columbia and legislative assistant to Senator Thomas Eagleton. Since earlier this year, Manatos has been Counselor to the Secretary of Commerce for Congressional Liaison.
