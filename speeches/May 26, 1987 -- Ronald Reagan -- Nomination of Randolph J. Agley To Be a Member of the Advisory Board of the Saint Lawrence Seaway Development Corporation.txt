
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Randolph J. Agley to be a member of the Advisory Board of the Saint Lawrence Seaway Development Corporation, Department of Transportation. He would succeed John R. Wall.
Mr. Agley is presently the chairman of the board and the chief executive officer of Talon, Inc., in Roseville, MI. Prior to this he served as the manager of the small business division of Arthur Andersen & Co., in Detroit, MI.
Mr. Agley graduated from the University of Michigan (B.B.A., 1964; M.B.A., 1965). He was born December 2, 1942, in Brooklyn, NY. Mr. Agley is married, has two children, and currently resides in Grosse Pointe Farms, MI.
