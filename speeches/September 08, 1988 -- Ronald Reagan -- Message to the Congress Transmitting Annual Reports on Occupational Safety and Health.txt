
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with Section 26 of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (Public Law 91-596; 29 U.S.C. 675), I transmit herewith the 1987 annual reports on activities under that law of the Department of Labor, of the Department of Health and Human Services, and of the Occupational Safety and Health Review Commission.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
September 8, 1988.
