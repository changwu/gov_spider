
    The President today announced the appointment of two persons as members of the President's Commission on White House Fellowships. They are:
PHYLLIS R. BLEIWEIS, of Gainesville, Fla., owner of PRB Consulting, a public relations firm. She is active in community and civic affairs in Gainesville; and
ARTHUR H. HOUSE, administrative assistant to Senator Abraham Ribicoff. House was a White House Fellow in 1975-76.
