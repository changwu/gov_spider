
    Today Republican presidential nominee Bob Dole released the following statement on Rosh Hashana:
"On the occasion of Rosh Hashana which begins the Jewish New Year of 5737, Elizabeth and I would like to extend our warmest wishes for peace and prosperity to Jewish Americans across the country who have contributed so much to the greatness of our nation May it be a year of happiness for American Jews, a year of peace throughout the world -- especially for Israel and all the peoples of the Middle East -and a year that brings joy and fulfillment to Americans of all faiths "
Bob Dole
