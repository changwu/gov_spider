
    President Donald J. Trump spoke today with President Enrique Peña Nieto of Mexico to offer condolences to the families of the victims of the helicopter accident in Oaxaca.  President Peña Nieto returned the sentiment and expressed Mexico's solidarity with the United States following the high school shooting in Parkland, Florida.  President Trump underscored his commitment to expanding cooperation between the United States and Mexico on security, trade, and immigration.
