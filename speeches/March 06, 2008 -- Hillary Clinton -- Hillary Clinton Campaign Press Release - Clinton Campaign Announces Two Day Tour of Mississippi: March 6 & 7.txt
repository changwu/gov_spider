
    Hillary Clinton to Visit Canton, Hattiesburg. President Clinton to Visit Tupelo
The Clinton campaign today announced Hillary Clinton will visit Mississippi tonight, Thursday, March 6, and both Hillary and President Bill Clinton will visit tomorrow, Friday, March 7, criss-crossing the state and sharing with voters why Hillary is the most qualified candidate to jumpstart Mississippi's economy, rebuild the middle class, and be Commander-in-Chief on day one.
This evening, Hillary Clinton addresses the 26th Annual Jefferson-Jackson-Hamer Day Dinner in Canton. Tomorrow, March 7, she will host a "Solutions for America" town hall in Hattiesburg.
President Clinton will attend a fish fry in Tupelo, tomorrow, Friday, March 7.
