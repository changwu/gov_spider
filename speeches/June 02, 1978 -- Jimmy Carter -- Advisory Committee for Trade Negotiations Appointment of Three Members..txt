
    The President today announced the appointment of three persons as members of the Advisory Committee for Trade Negotiations for 2 year terms. They are:
FRANK T. CARY chairman of the board and chief executive officer of International Business Machines Corp. in Armonk, N.Y.;
ABRAHAM FEINBERG, who was chairman of Kayser-Roth Corp. until his retirement in 1964;
BROOKS McCORMICK, chairman of the board of International Harvester.
