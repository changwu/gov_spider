
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Bishop L. Robinson to be a member of the National Institute of Justice Advisory Board, Department of Justice, for a term expiring January 11, 1986. This is a reappointment.
Mr. Robinson is deputy commissioner of the Operations Bureau of the Baltimore Police Department. He is married, has one child, and resides in Baltimore, Md. He was born January 16, 1927.
