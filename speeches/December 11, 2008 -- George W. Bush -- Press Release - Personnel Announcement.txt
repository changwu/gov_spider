
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint five individuals to serve in his Administration.
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the President's Council on Service and Civic Participation, for a term of two years:
Richard D. Fairbank of Virginia;
Arthur G. Linkletter of California;
Sonya E. Medina of Texas;
Stephen Odland of Florida;
Jose R. Ramos of Texas.
