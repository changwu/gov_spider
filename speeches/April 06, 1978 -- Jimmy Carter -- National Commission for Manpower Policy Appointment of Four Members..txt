
    The President today announced the appointment .of four persons as members of the National Commission for Manpower Policy. They are:
GEORGE S. DILLON, 60, of Saddle River, N.J., chairman of the board of Airco, Inc.
ROY R. ESCARCEGA, 40, of Hacienda Heights, Calif., vice president of the Social Services Division of the East Los Angeles Community Union.
GERTRUDE G. MICHELSON, 52, of New York City, senior vice president for personnel, labor, and consumer relations for Macy's.
LESTER G. THUROW, 39, of Lincoln, Mass., professor of economics and management at Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
