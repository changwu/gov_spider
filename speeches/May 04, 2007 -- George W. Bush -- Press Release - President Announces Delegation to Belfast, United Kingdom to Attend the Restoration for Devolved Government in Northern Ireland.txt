
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Belfast, United Kingdom to attend the Restoration for Devolved Government in Northern Ireland.
The Honorable Paula J. Dobriansky, Special Envoy to Northern Ireland, who also serves as the Under Secretary of State for Democracy and Global Affairs, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Thomas C. Foley, United States Ambassador to Ireland
The Honorable Edward M. Kennedy, United States Senator from Massachusetts
Howard Dean Pittman, United States Consul General in Belfast
Richard F. Powers, III, Former President of the Morgan Stanley Investment Management Client Group
