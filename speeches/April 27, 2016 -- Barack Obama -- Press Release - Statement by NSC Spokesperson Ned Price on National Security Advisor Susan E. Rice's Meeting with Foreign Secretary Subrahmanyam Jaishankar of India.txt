
    National Security Advisor Susan E. Rice met today with Indian Foreign Secretary Subrahmanyam Jaishankar at the White House. Ambassador Rice and Foreign Secretary Jaishankar affirmed their commitment to deepening bilateral cooperation on climate change, trade, and defense. They also discussed critical follow-on to the Paris Agreement, progress in our civil-nuclear cooperation, our ongoing support for India's membership in the multilateral export control regimes, next steps in the Global Health Security Agenda, and opportunities for expanded security and counterterrorism cooperation.
