
    In recognition of the 50th anniversary of the nation's All-Volunteer Force, and as part of the First Lady's Joining Forces initiative, Dr. Biden will visit U.S. Marine Corps Recruit Depot, Parris Island, South Carolina the morning of Friday, June 30th to celebrate and thank Marine graduates and their families. The First Lady will attend and deliver remarks at the Marine Corps graduation ceremony. This event will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Thursday, June 29th at 10:00 AM.
