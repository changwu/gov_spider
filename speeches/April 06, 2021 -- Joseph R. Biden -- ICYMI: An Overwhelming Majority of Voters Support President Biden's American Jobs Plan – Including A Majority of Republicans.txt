73% of respondents, including 67% of Independents and 57% of Republicans, support the American Jobs Plan

President Biden has outlined a bold program to build back better. The American Jobs Plan is a transformational package that will reimagine and rebuild a new American economy. The President is proposing a plan that America needs, and this poll proves his plan is what Americans want.
