
    On Thursday, November 7th, the Vice President will travel to Manchester, NH. There, the Vice President will formally file the ticket of President Donald Trump and Vice President Pence to be on the ballot in New Hampshire. Following, the Vice President will visit New Hampshire Institute of Politics & Political Library at Saint Anselm College to deliver remarks at New England Council's Politics & Eggs series.
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
