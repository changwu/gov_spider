
    On Wednesday, July 26, 2023, the President signed into law:
H.R. 1096, the "250th Anniversary of the United States Marine Corps Commemorative Coin Act," which directs the Department of the Treasury to mint coins in commemoration of the 250th Anniversary of the United States Marine Corps.
Thank you to Representatives Moulton, Bergman, Gallego, and Pence, and Senators Blumenthal, Sullivan, Rick Scott, Rounds, Young, Shaheen, Warner, Heinrich, and Duckworth for their leadership.
