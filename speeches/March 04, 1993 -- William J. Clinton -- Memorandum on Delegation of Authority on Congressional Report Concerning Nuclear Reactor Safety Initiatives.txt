
    Memorandum for the Secretary of State
By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Constitution and laws of the United States of America, including Section 301 of Title 3 of the United States Code, I hereby delegate to the Secretary of State all functions vested in me by Section 3202(c) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1993 ( P.L. 102-484). These functions shall be exercised in consultation with appropriate departments and agencies.
The Secretary of State is authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, March 4, 1993.
