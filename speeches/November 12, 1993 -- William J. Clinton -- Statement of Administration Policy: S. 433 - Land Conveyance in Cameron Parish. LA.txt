
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Johnston (D) LA and Breaux (D) LA)
The Administration supports S. 433.
Pay-As-You-Go Scoring
S. 433 affects receipts and direct spending; therefore, it is subject to the pay-as-you-go (PAYGO) requirement of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act (OBRA) of 1990. OMB's preliminary PAYGO estimate is zero.
