
    PRESTON, IA – Iowa State Senator Roger Stewart, District 13 – Clinton, Dubuque, and Jackson Counties, released the following statement following today's NPR debate in Des Moines.
"Hillary's performance at this afternoon's debate shows once again her strong command of issues important to Iowa voters, from Iraq and Iran to consumer safety, the military and international trade.
"We need a President who understands the magnitude and complexity of the challenges we face – and has the strength and experience to address them from day one. Listening to her today, I am reminded why she has my support."
