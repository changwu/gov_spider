
    The President today announced the nomination of the following three individuals to the District of Columbia Superior Court: Ronna Lee Beck, Linda Kay Davis, and Eric T. Washington. The President is authorized by statute to nominate the District of Columbia Superior Court judges from a list of individuals recommended by the District of Columbia Judicial Nomination Commission.
"I am confident that these individuals will serve with distinction," the President said. "Their legal skill and dedication to the community will be strong assets to the District of Columbia and to this Court."
