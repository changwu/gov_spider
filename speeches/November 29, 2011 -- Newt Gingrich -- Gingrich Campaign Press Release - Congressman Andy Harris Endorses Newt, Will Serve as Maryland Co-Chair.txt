
    Conservative Freshman Congressman Andy Harris endorsed Newt today and will serve as co-chair of our Maryland team.
Rep. Andy Harris has announced that he is endorsing Former Speaker Newt Gingrich for President of the United States. Harris also announced that he is co-chairing Maryland's Gingrich team.
"I am supporting Newt Gingrich because I believe he is the best candidate with the critical experience to turn this economy around and resolve our nation's debt crisis," said Harris.
Rep. Andy Harris represents Maryland's 1st Congressional District which includes Maryland's entire Eastern Shore and parts of Anne Arundel, Baltimore and Harford Counties.
