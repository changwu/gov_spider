
    Memorandum for the Secretary of StateSubject: Delegation of Authority for Report Containing a Determination Concerning El Salvador
By authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and statutes of the United States of America, including Section 621 of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended, and Section 301 of Title 3 of the United States Code, I hereby delegate to you the functions conferred upon me by Public Law 98-396 (Second Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1984) in the "General Provisions" of Chapter XII, insofar as they relate to El Salvador.
This memorandum shall be published in the Federal Register.
RONALD REAGAN[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 4:01 p.m., September 18, 1984]
