
    The President today announced his intention to designate David M. Sablan to be a member of the Northern Mariana Islands Commission on Federal Laws. He would succeed Jesus Camacho Borja.
Mr. Sablan is currently chairman of the board of the Microl Corp., Saipan, Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands. Previously he was president of the Microl Corp. In 1982 Mr. Sablan was special assistant to the Governor for planning and budget, Commonwealth government. From 1979 to 1981, he was an economic consultant to the Northern Marianas Commonwealth Legislature.
Mr. Sablan is married, has one child, and resides in Saipan, Northern Mariana Islands. He was born April 2, 1932, in Garapan, Saipan.
