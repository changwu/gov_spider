
    Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
I hereby submit the second report on the Operation of the Andean Trade Preference Act. This report is prepared pursuant to the requirements of section 203 of the Andean Trade Preference Act of 1991. The report concludes that the Andean Trade Preference Act continues to advance U.S. counternarcotics goals in the Andean region.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
