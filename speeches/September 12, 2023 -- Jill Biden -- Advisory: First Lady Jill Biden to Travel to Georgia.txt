
    From Thursday, September 14th to Friday, September 15th, First Lady Jill Biden will travel to Atlanta, Georgia.
On Thursday, September 14th, in the evening, the First Lady will arrive at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. This arrival will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Wednesday, September 13th at 5:00 PM ET.
In the evening, the First Lady will deliver remarks at a political event for the Biden Victory Fund in Atlanta, GA. This event will be covered by an editorial pool only.
On Friday, September 15th, as part of the Biden Cancer Moonshot, the First Lady will visit Emory University to highlight the launch of the first project funded through the Advanced Research Projects Agency for Health (ARPA-H), which aims to use mRNA technology to train immune systems to fight cancer and other diseases more effectively. Portions of this will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Wednesday, September 13th at 5:00 PM ET.
