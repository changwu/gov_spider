
    President Biden's economic plan is working. This morning's unemployment insurance claims mark another low since the pandemic began over a year ago. The four-week moving average has gone down by around 50% since President Biden took office. The headlines of the past few weeks highlight this trend.

June 3rd – AP: US jobless claims drop to 385,000, another pandemic low
May 27th – WSJ: U.S. Jobless Claims Extend Decline to New Pandemic Low
May 20th – Bloomberg: Initial Jobless Claims in U.S. Decline to Fresh Pandemic Low
May 13th – Politico: U.S. unemployment claims drop to 473,000, a new pandemic low
May 6th – AP: US unemployment claims fall to a pandemic low of 498,000

Thanks to a robust vaccination program, Americans are getting back to work, and President Biden's economic plan – building the economy from the bottom up, and the middle out – is working.
