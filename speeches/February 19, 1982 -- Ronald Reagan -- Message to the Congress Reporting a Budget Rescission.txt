
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report a revision to a rescission proposal previously reported decreasing the amount proposed for rescission by $2.0 million.
The amended rescission proposal affects the Mine Safety and Health Administration of the Department of Labor.
The details of the revised rescission proposal are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
February 19, 1982.
