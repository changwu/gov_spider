
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint the following three individuals to serve as Members of the Christopher Columbus Fellowship Foundation:
President Pro Tempore of the Senate Representative --
Audy Michael Perry of West Virginia, for the remainder of a six-year term expiring October 19, 2002
Speaker of the House Representative --
Robert Jeffrey Glovitz of Arizona, for the remainder of a six-year term expiring October 2, 2006
Minority Leader of the House Representative --
Lynda Hare Scribante of Colorado, for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 23, 2007
