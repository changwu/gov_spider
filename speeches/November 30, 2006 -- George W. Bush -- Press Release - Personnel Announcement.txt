
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint two people and designate two people to serve in his Administration.
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the President's National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee:
Daniel J. Carroll, Jr. of New Jersey
Howard L. Lance of Florida
The President intends to designate Gary D. Forsee, of Missouri, to be Chairman of the President's National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee.
The President intends to designate Randall L. Stephenson of Texas, to be Vice-Chairman of the President's National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee.
