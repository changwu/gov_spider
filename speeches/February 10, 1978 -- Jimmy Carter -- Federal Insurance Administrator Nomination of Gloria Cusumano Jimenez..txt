
    The President today announced that he will nominate Gloria Cusumano Jimenez, of Durham, N.C., to be Federal Insurance Administrator.
Jimenez was born July 1, 1932, in New York City. She received an LL.B. from Brooklyn Law School in 1954.
From 1955 to 1966, she practiced law in New York, and in 1965 and 1966, she also served as district aide to Congressman Richard Ottinger.
From 1968 to 1970, Jimenez was director of housing and urban programs for the North Carolina Department of Local Affairs. From 1971 to 1974, she was a housing consultant and assistant director of the Low Income Housing Development Corporation in Durham. Since 1975 she has been deputy commissioner and general counsel of the North Carolina Department of Insurance.
