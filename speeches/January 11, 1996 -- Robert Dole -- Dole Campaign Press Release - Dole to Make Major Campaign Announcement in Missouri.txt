
    GOP Presidential frontrunner Senator Bob Dole will visit St. Louis, Missouri on Friday, January 12, 1996. He will attend a news conference with Senator Christopher "Kit" Bond, Congressman William Emerson and other Missouri political leaders for a major campaign announcement from 5:40pm to 6:00pm CST at the Holiday Inn Clayton Plaza. Media attending should arrive thirty minutes early for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials.
