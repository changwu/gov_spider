
    [Released October 22, 1929. Dated October 21, 1929]
I WISH to express the condolence of the people of the United States and my own sympathy in the loss which Uruguay has suffered through the death of the distinguished citizen and ex-President Jose Battle y Ordonez.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Juan Campisteguy, President of Uruguay, Montevideo]
