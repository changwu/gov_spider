
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Guatemala to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Jimmy Morales, President-elect of the Republic of Guatemala, on January 14, 2016.
The Honorable Joseph R. Biden, Jr., Vice President of the United States, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation: 
The Honorable Todd Robinson, U.S. Ambassador to Guatemala, Department of State
The Honorable Jeh Johnson, U.S. Secretary of Homeland Security
The Honorable Thomas Carper, United States Senator, Delaware
The Honorable Norma Torres, Member of the United States House of Representatives (CA-35)
The Honorable Thomas A. Shannon, Counselor of the Department of State
The Honorable Mark Feierstein, Senior Director for Western Hemisphere Affairs, National Security Council
