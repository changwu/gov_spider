
    The President today announced his intention to nominate John F.W. Rogers, of New York, to be Under Secretary of State for Management. He would succeed Ivan Selin.
Currently Mr. Rogers is executive vice president for operations of The Oliver Carr Company, 1988 to present. Prior to this, Mr. Rogers served as Assistant Secretary of Treasury for Management (1985 - 1987) and Assistant to the President for Management (1981 - 1985).
Mr. Rogers graduated from George Washington University (B.A., 1978). Mr. Rogers was born April 15, 1956, in Seneca Falls, NY, and resides in Washington, DC.
