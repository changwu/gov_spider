
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia to attend the 800th Anniversary of the Great Mongolian State from July 12-13, 2006.
The Honorable Mike Johanns, Secretary of Agriculture, will lead the delegation.
Accompanying member of the Presidential Delegation:
Mr. Robert M. Peck, Senior Fellow of the Academy of Natural Sciences of Philadelphia
