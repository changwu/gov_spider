
    On Friday, July 27, 2018, the President signed into law:
S. 490, which allows for the extension of the construction license for the Gibson Dam Hydroelectric Project in Montana;
S. 931, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4910 Brighton Boulevard in Denver, Colorado, as the George Sakato Post Office; and
S. 2734, which designates the Federal building and United States courthouse located at 1300 Victoria Street in Laredo, Texas, as the George P. Kazen Federal Building and United States Courthouse.
