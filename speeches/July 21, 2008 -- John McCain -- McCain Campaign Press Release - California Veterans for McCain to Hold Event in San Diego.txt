
    TORRANCE, CA -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Admiral Ed Martin, Nathan Fletcher, Gabe Groves and Alexis Haftvani will participate in the announcement of California Veterans for John McCain on Tuesday, July 22nd at 10:00 a.m. PDT. This event occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across the country.
Tuesday, July 22, 2008
SAN DIEGO, CALIFORNIA
WHO: California Veterans for McCain
Robert Rubin, Chairman, Southern California Region, McCain '08
Admiral Ed Martin, USMC (Ret.), former POW, Vietnam War
Gabe Groves, USAF, U.S. Navy
Nathan Fletcher, Former USMC SSgt.; Iraq combat veteran
Alexis Haftvani, Naval Intelligence Officer, Former Lt., U.S. Navy
WHAT: Remarks at the Veterans Memorial Center
WHEN: Tuesday, July 22, 2008 at 10:00 a.m. PDT
WHERE: Veterans Memorial Center
2115 Memorial Center Blvd.
San Diego, CA
