
    Des Moines, IA -- The Obama Campaign today announced that U.S. Senator Barack Obama will return to Iowa today, Thursday, December 13, to campaign throughout the state.
Today, Senator Obama will attend the Des Moines Register Democratic Presidential Debate before attending a town hall meeting in Dubuque moderated by Gayle King. On Friday, Obama will host a roundtable in Cedar Rapids and make stops in Monticello, Manchester, Guttenberg and Oelwein.
On Saturday, Obama will host events in Independence, Waterloo, New Hampton, Charles City and Mason City. On Sunday, Obama will host events in Algona, Emmetsburg, Estherville and Spirit Lake and on Monday Obama will continue to campaign through communities in northwestern Iowa. On Tuesday, Obama will wrap up his swing through Iowa with a public event in Des Moines.
