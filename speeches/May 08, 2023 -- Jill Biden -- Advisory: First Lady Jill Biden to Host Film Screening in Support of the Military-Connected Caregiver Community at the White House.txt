
    On Wednesday, May 10th at 4:30 PM ET, as part of the First Lady's Joining Forces initiative, Dr. Biden will host a documentary screening and reception with the Elizabeth Dole Foundation at the White House of "Unconditional: When Minds Hurt, Love Heals" to highlight caregivers of wounded, ill, or injured service members or veterans. The First Lady will deliver remarks and will be joined by caregivers in the military-connected community, including some of the family members from the film, and filmmakers of the documentary. The opening remarks program will be open to pre-credentialed media. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Tuesday, May 9th at 6:00 PM.
