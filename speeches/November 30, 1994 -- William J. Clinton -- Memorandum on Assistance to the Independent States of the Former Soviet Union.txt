
    Presidential Determination No. 95–6
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject: Assistance Program for Independent States of the Former Soviet Union
Pursuant to subsection (d) under the heading "Assistance for the New Independent States of the Former Soviet Union" in title II of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1995 (titles I–V of Public Law 103–306), I hereby determine that it is in the national interest of the United States to make available funds appropriated under that heading without regard to the restriction in that subsection.
You are authorized and directed to notify the Congress of this determination and to publish it in the Federal Register.
William J. Clinton
