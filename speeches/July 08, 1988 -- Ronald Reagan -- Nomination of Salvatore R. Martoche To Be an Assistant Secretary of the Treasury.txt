
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Salvatore R. Martoche to be an Assistant Secretary of the Treasury (Enforcement). He would succeed Francis Anthony Keating II.
Since May 1986 Mr. Martoche has been Assistant Secretary of Labor (Enforcement). Prior to this he was United States Attorney for the Western District of New York, 1982-1986, and an attorney in the private practice of law, 1969-1982.
Mr. Martoche graduated from Canisius College (B.S., 1962) and the University of North Dakota School of Law (J.D., 1967). He was born October 12, 1940, in Buffalo, NY. Mr. Marroche is married, has three children, and resides in Buffalo, NY.
