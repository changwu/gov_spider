
    **All times are local.**
Over the next two weeks, First Lady Jill Biden will travel to Ohio, New Jersey, Georgia, and Pennsylvania, as part of the Biden-Harris Administration's Investing in America tour to highlight to how Bidenomics is delivering investments and jobs to communities across the country. During her travel, Dr. Biden will visit the Investing in America Workforce Hubs in Columbus, Augusta, and Pittsburgh. Dr. Biden will also deliver remarks at the National Governors Association (NGA) Summer Meeting in Atlantic City, New Jersey to highlight the importance of investing in workforce training programs across the country.
On Wednesday, July 12th at 1:30 PM, the First Lady will visit the Investing in America Workforce Hub in Columbus, OH to participate in Columbus' Workforce Hub launch convening and highlight commitments and initiatives led by the City of Columbus, Columbus State Community College, local employers, and unions. Portions of this event will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Tuesday, July 11th at 12:00 PM.
On Thursday, July 13th at 1:00 PM, the First Lady will deliver remarks at the National Governors Association (NGA) Summer Meeting in Atlantic City, NJ to highlight the importance of investing in workforce training programs that are preparing high school and community college students for good-paying jobs created by the President's Investing in America agenda. The First Lady's remarks will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Tuesday, July 11th at 6:00 PM.
On Tuesday, July 18th, the First Lady will local officials and key stakeholders leading Investing in America Workforce Hubs in Augusta, GA and Pittsburgh, PA to discuss ways the local community is working together to expand pathways to good-paying jobs.
Additional details on the First Lady's travel and schedule to follow.
