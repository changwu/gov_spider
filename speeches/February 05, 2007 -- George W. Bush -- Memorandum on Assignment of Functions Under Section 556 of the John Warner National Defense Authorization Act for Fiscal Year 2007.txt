
    Memorandum for the Secretary of Defense
Subject: Assignment of Functions under Section 556 of the John Warner National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007
By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and laws of the United States, including section 301 of title 3, United States Code, I hereby assign to you the functions of the President under section 556 of the John Warner National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007 (Public Law 109-364).
You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush
