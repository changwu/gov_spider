
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Rep. Udall (D) Arizona and three others)
The Administration supports House enactment of H.R. 4378, but will seek changes in the Senate, including those to:
— stipulate that the Department of Defense will be a member of the Commission;
— include National Park System lands in Arlington County, Virginia in Area II. The scope of the bill currently covers only the District of Columbia;
— require approval by the Secretary of the Interior of design and site location for memorials subject to the bill;
— clarify that the bill applies only to National Park System lands; and
— other technical changes.
