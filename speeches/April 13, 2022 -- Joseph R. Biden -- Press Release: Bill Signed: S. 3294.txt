
    On Wednesday, April 13, 2022, the President signed into law:
S. 3294, which directs the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library to obtain and install statues honoring Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O'Connor and Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg in the U.S. Capitol or on the Capitol Grounds.
Thank you to Senators Amy Klobuchar, Murkowski, Collins, and Sinema, and Democratic Women's Caucus (DWC) Co-Chairs Congresswomen Lois Frankel, Brenda Lawrence, and Jackie Speier, and Vice Chairs Congresswomen Veronica Escobar and Sylvia Garcia, and Bipartisan Women's Caucus Co-Chairs Congresswomen Madeleine Dean and Jenniffer González-Colón.
