
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report three deferrals of budgetary resources, totaling $122.8 million.
These deferrals affect the International Security Assistance program, and the Departments of Health and Human Services and State.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, October 19, 1995.
