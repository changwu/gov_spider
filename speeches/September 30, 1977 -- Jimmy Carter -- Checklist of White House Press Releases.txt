
    The following releases of the Office of the White House Press Secretary, distributed during the period covered by this issue, are not included in the issue.
Released September 26, 1977

Announcement: nomination of Damon J. Keith to be United States Circuit Judge for the Sixth Circuit

Released September 30, 1977
Announcement: nomination of Robert T. O'Leary to be United States Attorney for the District of Montana and Archie P. Sherar to be United States Marshal for the Eastern District of Washington
