
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one proposed rescission of budget authority totaling $1,700,000 and one revised deferral of budget authority totaling $21,061,187.
The actions affect programs in the Departments of Health and Human Services and Labor.
The details of the proposed rescission and deferral are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
December 21, 1983.
