
    On Thursday, December 10th, Vice President Mike Pence will travel to Greenville, South Carolina. There, the Vice President will participate in a roundtable highlighting the unprecedented public-private partnership to combat the coronavirus, Operation Warp Speed and South Carolina's plan to operationalize vaccinations once approved. Following, the Vice President will travel to Augusta, Georgia for a Defend the Majority Rally. Later that evening, the Vice President will return to Washington, D.C.
