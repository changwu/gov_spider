Charles Dunne: Nominee for United States Marshal, Eastern District of New York
Charles Dunne currently works for the New York City Police Department. He joined the force in 1990 and has worked his way up the ranks to his current position as Inspector. Dunne received his B.A. degree from Fairfield University in 1989 and his M.A. degree in 1997 from John Jay College of Criminal Justice. In 2002, Dunne also earned his law degree from St. John's University School of Law.
