
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Brenda Premo to be a member of the National Council on the Handicapped for a term expiring September 17, 1991. This is a reappointment.
Since 1977 Ms. Premo has been executive director of Dayle Mcintosh Center for the Disabled in Anaheim, CA. Prior to this, she was an administrative aide for the Orange County department of education, 1976-1977.
Ms. Premo graduated from California State University at Long Beach (B.A., 1975). She was born February 4, 1952, in Longhead, CA, and currently resides in Stanton, CA.
