
    Bellevue, WA – Former Arkansas Governor and Presidential Candidate Mike Huckabee was joined today by Dr. Joseph Fuiten, founder of Positive Agenda and Pastor of Cedar ParkChurch in Bothell, WA, to announce a growing list of endorsements from leaders of the faith community in Washington State. The announcement was made at a news conference at the Harbor Club in Bellevue, Wash.
"I'm honored to have the support of Dr. Fuiten. He is truly a man of great faith and conviction," Huckabee said. "I'm proud to have his support, and equally excited about the list of supporters he brings to the table."
"Mike Huckabee is a person of judgment and character who can be relied upon to make strong decisions as President," Dr. Fuiten said. "He represents my values."
The growing list of Washington State faith leaders endorsing Huckabee includes:
