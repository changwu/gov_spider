
    MANCHESTER, NH -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Former Pennsylvania Governor and Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security Tom Ridge will participate in the announcement of New Hampshire Veterans for John McCain on Thursday, July 17th. Governor Ridge served as a U.S. Army infantry staff sergeant in Vietnam and earned the Bronze Star for Valor, the Combat Infantry Badge and the Vietnamese Cross of Gallantry.
Thursday, July 17, 2008
MANCHESTER, NEW HAMPSHIRE
WHO: Governor Tom Ridge
New Hampshire Veterans for McCain
