
    The President has announced the appointment of four members to the Board of Foreign Scholarships for terms expiring September 22, 1983. They are:
MARIO A. ANGLADA, of New York, vice Tomas Rivera, term expired.
H. BRANDT AYERS, of Alabama, vice Elbert Benjamin Smith, term expired.
ADELAIDE CROMWELL GULLIVER, of Massachusetts, vice Monroe D. Donsker, term expired.
HARRISON E. SALISBURY, of Connecticut, vice Battle Bull, term expired.
