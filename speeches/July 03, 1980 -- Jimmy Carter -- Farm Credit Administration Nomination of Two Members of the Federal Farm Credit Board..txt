
    The President today announced that he will nominate two persons to be members of the Federal Farm Credit Board. They are:
RALPH RAIKES, of Ashland, Nebr., who raises corn, soybeans, and milo, and livestock. He served as director of Farm Credit Banks of Omaha for 26 years.
WILLIAM D. WAMPLER, Of Dayton, Va., who raises poultry and cattle and serves as vice president of Wampler Foods, Inc.
