Q. Heard anything from China, Mr. President? Their reaction, the Chinese reaction?
The President. I don't know what their—I feel very good about our policy. I think it's a good policy. I don't want to isolate China. I want to do what's good for—just the Chinese people. But I think standing up for American values and values in China is the way to go. I think this is the right policy.
