
    
  Dr. Paul to visit Rochester, Chanhassen, Arden Hills-Bethel University, St. Cloud, and Minneapolis

LAKE JACKSON, Texas â€” 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul will campaign in Minnesota on two separate days in the run-up to the Tuesday, February 7th caucus. Dr. Paul will visit the North Star State onSaturday, February 4th and Monday, February 6th.
The 12-term Congressman from Texas will first visit the state on Saturday, February 4th for town hall meetings in Rochester and Chanhassen, and a Bethel University rally in Arden Hills. The campus rally is sponsored by the Bethel University College Republicans.
Dr. Paul's second visit on Monday, February 6th will feature a St. Cloud Town Hall & Rally, and a Minneapolis Rally.
"We're delighted to have Ron Paul return to Minnesota where last November he attracted more than 3,000 supporters to a rally held in St. Cloud. Dr. Paul's upcoming visit will energize supporters and promises to help us achieve a strong showing in the upcoming caucus," said Marianne Stebbins, the Ron Paul 2012 Presidential Campaign's Minnesota Chair.
