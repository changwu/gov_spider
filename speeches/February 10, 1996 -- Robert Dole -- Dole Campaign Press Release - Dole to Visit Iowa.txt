
    Senate Majority Leader Bob Dole will visit Iowa on Sunday, February 11, 1996. He will attend events in Davenport, Cedar Rapids and Waterloo. He will be joined by Elizabeth Dole, Robin Dole, Senator Chuck Grassley, Congressman Jim Lightfoot and Congressman Jim Leach at different events throughout the day. Media attending should arrive thirty minutes early for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials.
