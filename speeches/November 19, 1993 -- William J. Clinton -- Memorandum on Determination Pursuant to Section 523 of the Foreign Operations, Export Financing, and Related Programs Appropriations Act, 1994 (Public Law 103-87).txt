
    Presidential Determination No. 94-4
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to section 523 of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1994 ( Public Law 103-87), I hereby certify that withholding funds from international financial institutions and other international organizations and programs during FY 1994, pursuant to the limitation contained therein prohibiting the obligation of funds appropriated by that Act to finance indirectly any assistance or reparations to certain specific countries, is contrary to the national interest.
You are authorized and directed to publish this determination in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, November 19, 1993.
