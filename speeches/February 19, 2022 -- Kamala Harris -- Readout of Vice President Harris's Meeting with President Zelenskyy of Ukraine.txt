
    Vice President Kamala Harris met today with President Volodymyr Zelenskyy of Ukraine in Munich, Germany. They discussed recent developments and assessments of Russia's massive military build-up around Ukraine.
The Vice President underscored the U.S. commitment to Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity. They discussed the united Transatlantic approach if Russia further invades Ukraine, and the Vice President outlined the swift and severe economic measures that have been prepared alongside our Allies and partners. The Vice President and President Zelenskyy agreed on the importance of diplomacy and de-escalation.
