
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one new deferral of budget authority amounting to $6.0 million for the antirecession financial assistance fund of the Department of the Treasury. In addition, I am reporting a revision in the basis for and a reduction in the amount of a previously transmitted Department of Commerce deferral.
The details of each deferral are contained in the attached reports.
JIMMY CARTER
The White House,
April 28, 1977.
