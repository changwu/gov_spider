
    To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with the request contained in the resolution of the Senate of the 17th instant, in regard to certain correspondence* between James Buchanan, then President of the United States, and Lewis Cass, Secretary of State, I transmit a report from the Department of State, which is accompanied by a copy of the correspondence referred to.
U. S. GRANT.
*Regarding the policy to be pursued to avert civil war, then threatening, which correspondence led to the resignation of Mr. Cass.
