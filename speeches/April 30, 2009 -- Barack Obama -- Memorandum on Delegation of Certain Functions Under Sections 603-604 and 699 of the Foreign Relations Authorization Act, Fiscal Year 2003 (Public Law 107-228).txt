
    Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject: Delegation of Certain Functions Under Sections 603-604 and 699 of the Foreign Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Year 2003 (Public Law 107-228)
By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, including section 301 of title 3, United States Code, I hereby delegate to you the functions conferred upon the President by sections 603-604 and 699 of the Foreign Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Year 2003 (Public Law 107-228).
You are authorized and directed to transmit this determination and certification to the appropriate committees of the Congress and to publish it in the Federal Register.
BARACK OBAMA
