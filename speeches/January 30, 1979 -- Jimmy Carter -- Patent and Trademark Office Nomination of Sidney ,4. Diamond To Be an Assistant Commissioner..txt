
    The President today announced that he will nominate Sidney A. Diamond, of Tucson, Ariz., to be an Assistant Commissioner of Patents and Trademarks.
Diamond, 64, is an attorney and is presently counsel to a New York law firm. He is an expert on patent and copyright law and has served as chairman of the American Bar Association's section of patent, trademark, and copyright law. He is vice president of the International Patent and Trademark Association and the author of numerous articles on trademark and patent law in legal and other periodicals.
