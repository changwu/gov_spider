
    The President today announced his intention to designate Pearl M. Bailey to be a Representative of the United States of America to the 43d Session of the General Assembly of the United Nations (September 20 to December 1988).
Since 1933 Ms. Bailey has been an entertainer with the William Morris Agency, Inc., in New York City.
Ms. Bailey was born March 29, 1918, in Newport News, VA, and currently resides in Lake Havasu City, AZ.
