
    The following list includes the President's public schedule and other items of general interest announced by the Office of the Press Secretary and not included elsewhere in this issue.
December 27
The White House announced that the President will visit Davos, Switzerland, on January 29, 2000, to attend the World Economic Forum.
December 28
The President announced his intention to recess appoint Frank S. Holleman III as Deputy Secretary of the Department of Education.
December 31
In the morning, the President and Hillary Clinton attended the opening ceremony of the "America's Millennium" celebration on 12th St. and Constitution Ave.
In the afternoon, the President and Hillary Clinton attended the "Millennium Around the World" celebration at the International Trade Center.
In the evening, the President and Hillary Clinton attended the "America's Millennium" celebration at the Lincoln Memorial.
