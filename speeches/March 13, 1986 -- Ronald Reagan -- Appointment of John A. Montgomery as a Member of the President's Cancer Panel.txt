
    The President today announced his intention to appoint John A. Montgomery to be a member of the President's Cancer Panel for a term expiring February 20, 1989. This is a reappointment.
Dr. Montgomery is senior vice president and director of the Kettering-Meyer Laboratory at Southern Research Institute in Birmingham, AL. He has served on the President's Cancer Panel since 1983.
Dr. Montgomery graduated from Vanderbilt University (A.B., 1946; M.S., 1947) and the University of North Carolina (Ph.D., 1951). He is married, has four children, and resides in Birmingham, AL. He was born March 29, 1924, in Greenville, MS.
