
    The President today announced that he will nominate four persons to be members of the United States Metric Board. They are:
ALFREDO A. CANTU, of Denver, Colo., owner of Cantu Advertising and Graphics;
BRUCE PAUL JOHNSON, of Reno, Nev., chairman of the electrical engineering department at the University of Nevada (reappointment);
JAMES M. ROSSER, Of Bradbury, Calif., president of California State University at Los Angeles and a professor of health care management there;
DENNIS R. SMITH, of Middleboro, Mass., a high school mathematics teacher and metric coordinator for the Middleboro Public Schools (reappointment).
