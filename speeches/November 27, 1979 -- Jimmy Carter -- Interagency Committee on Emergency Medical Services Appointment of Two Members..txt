
    The President today announced the appointment of two persons as members of the Interagency Committee on Emergency Medical Services. They are:
ALLEN JOSEPH HERBERT, a Ruston, La., physician in family practice and clinical assistant professor of family medicine and pediatrics at Louisiana State University School of Medicine;
MARVIN J. MOORE, of Fort Worth, Tex., community relations director for Trinity Emergency Medical Services, a system serving eight counties. He is a freelance broadcaster and producer and is active in community affairs.
