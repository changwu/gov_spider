
    
  Whistle stop attracts large crowd of supporters and undecided voters

DENVER, Colorado — 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul had another positive showing at his last of three Colorado events today.
Held at the City of Colorado Springs Municipal Airport, this whistle stop featured a hanger filled with 750 supporters and undecided voters.
For his part, at 2:00 p.m. the 12-term Congressman from Texas spoke about his limited-government platform amid the cheers of Coloradans and in view of plenty of media.
