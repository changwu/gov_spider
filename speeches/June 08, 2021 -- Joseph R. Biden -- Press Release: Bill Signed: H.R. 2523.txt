
    On Tuesday, June 8, 2021, the President signed into law:
H.R. 2523, the "Training in High-demand Roles to Improve Veteran Employment Act or the THRIVE Act," which clarifies provisions of the COVID-19 Veteran Rapid Retraining Assistance program, and to make certain technical corrections to the Johnny Isakson and David P. Roe, M.D. Veterans Health Care and Benefits Improvement Act of 2020.
Thank you to Representatives Mike Levin and Mike Bost for all their work.
