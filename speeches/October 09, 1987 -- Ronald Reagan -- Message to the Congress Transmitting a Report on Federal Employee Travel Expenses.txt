
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with section 515(b) of the Supplemental Appropriations Act of 1987, P.L. 100-71, I herewith transmit a report specifying my determination of the uniform percentage necessary to reduce outlays for travel, transportation, and subsistence by $18 million government-wide in Fiscal Year 1987. Federal agencies have been instructed to make the required reductions.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
October 9, 1987.
