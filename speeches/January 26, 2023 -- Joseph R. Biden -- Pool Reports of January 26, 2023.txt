Speaking Program
The First LadyElaine Tso, CEO of Asian Services in Action (ASIA)The President
Background on Speaker
Elaine Tso serves as the CEO at Asian Services in Action (ASIA). In this role, she is responsible for leading the largest health and social services agency in the State of Ohio focused on addressing the needs of Asian Americans, Native Hawaiians, and Pacific Islanders (AA and NHPIs), immigrants and refugees. ASIA's mission is to serve, support, and advocate for AA and NHPIs, and immigrants and refugees, so they prosper and flourish. Elaine supervises a leadership team responsible for direct health and social services, policy advocacy, capacity building, community engagement, and special projects. Elaine also has the responsibility for ensuring compliance of the requirements for ASIA's International Community Health Center (ICHC), a Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC).

Background on Performers
The Choy Wun Lion Dance Troupe is a non-profit organization whose mission is to preserve and spread Chinese cultural awareness through performance arts. This group was founded in 2012 and is based out of Fairfax, Virginia. The group's name, Choy Wun, means Reaching for the Cloud, and the spirit of the name is to remind each of us to reach for the skies each and every day, whether it's lion dancing or in their personal life or career.
