
    On Tuesday, August 11th, the Vice President will travel to Arizona. In Tucson, the Vice President will accept the endorsement of the Arizona Association of Police, which represents 47 member agencies, and deliver remarks reinforcing the Trump administration's continued support of the men and women serving in law enforcement and their unwavering commitment to never defund the police. Then, the Vice President will participate in the Latter-day Saints for Trump Coalition rollout in Mesa. Later that evening, the Vice President will return to Washington D.C.
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
