
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the Closing Ceremony of the Olympic Games in Beijing, China on August 24, 2008.
The Honorable Condoleezza Rice, Secretary of State, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Elaine L. Chao, Secretary of LaborThe Honorable Michael O. Leavitt, Secretary of Health and Human ServicesThe Honorable Clark T. Randt, Jr, United States Ambassador to the People's Republic of ChinaThe Honorable Karen Hughes, Global Vice Chair of Burson-MarstellerMr. Peter Ueberroth, President of the United States Olympic CommitteeMs. Michelle Kwan, Figure Skating Champion and American Public Diplomacy Envoy
