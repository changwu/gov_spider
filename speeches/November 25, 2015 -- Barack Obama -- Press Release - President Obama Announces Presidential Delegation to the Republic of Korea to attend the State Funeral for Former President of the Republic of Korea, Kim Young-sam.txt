
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the Republic of Korea to attend the State Funeral for the former President of the Republic of Korea, Kim Young-sam, on November 26, 2015.
The Honorable Mark Lippert, U.S. Ambassador to the Republic of Korea, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Kathleen Stephens, former U.S. Ambassador to the Republic of Korea, Department of State
