
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Malcolm Forbes, Jr., of New Jersey, to be a member of the Board for International Broadcasting for a term expiring April 28, 1989. This is a reappointment, and upon confirmation, he will be redesignated Chairman.
Mr. Forbes is president and chief operating officer of Forbes, Inc. He graduated from Princeton University (B.A., 1970). Mr. Forbes was born July 18, 1947, in Morristown, NJ.
