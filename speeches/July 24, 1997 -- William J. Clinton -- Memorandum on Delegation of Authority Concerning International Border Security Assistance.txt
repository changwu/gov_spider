
    Memorandum for the Secretary of Defense
Subject: Delegation of Authority Under Section 1424 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1997
By the authority vested in my by the Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, including section 301 of title 3 of the United States Code, I hereby delegate to you, in consultation with the Secretary of State, the authority vested in the President under section 1424(c) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1997 (Public Law 104-201).
You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the Federal Register.
William J. Clinton
[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 8:45 a.m., July 29, 1997]
