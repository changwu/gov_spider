
    Des Moines, IA -- Today, November 10th, Senator Barack Obama will complete his five-day "Change We Can Believe In" tour with stops at the Iowa Farmer's Union, a pre-JJ rally with Grammy winner John Legend, and the Jefferson Jackson Dinner. Senator Obama's wife, Michelle, will join Barack as they rally Iowans before the Jefferson Jackson Dinner. Following the rally, Michelle Obama will attend an NAACP reception in Des Moines before joining Senator Obama at the Jefferson Jackson Dinner.
On Sunday in Des Moines, Senator Obama will be interviewed by Tim Russert on Meet the Press. Des Moines NBC affiliate WHO-TV will air the interview live at a special time of 8:00 AM.
