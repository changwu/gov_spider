
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report six new deferrals totalling $511.1 million and two revisions to previously transmitted deferrals increasing the amount deferred by $14.9 million. These items affect programs in the Departments of Commerce, Justice, State and Transportation; the International Communication Agency; the Railroad Retirement Board; and the President's Commission on Pension Policy.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
JIMMY CARTER
The White House,
November 15, 1979.
