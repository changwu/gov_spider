
    On February 13th, the Vice President will travel to South Carolina. There, the Vice President will attend a Trump Victory lunch in West Columbia, SC. Later that day, the Vice President will deliver remarks at The Citadel. Following, the Vice President will deliver remarks at The Citadel Republican Society Patriot Dinner and receive the Nathan Hale Patriot Award. Later that evening, the Vice President will return to Washington, D.C.
