
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report two revised deferrals of budget authority, now totaling $555.2 million.
The deferrals affect the Department of Agriculture. The details of the two revised deferrals are contained in the attached report.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, June 8, 1994.
