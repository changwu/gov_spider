MEDIA ADVISORY: LARGER-THAN-EXPECTED EVENT INTEREST AND COLD WEATHER LEAD TO OBAMA AMES RALLY SITE CHANGE
DES MOINES - In anticipation of larger-than-expect interest in Senator Obama's visit and cold weather, U.S. Senator Barack Obama's Iowa campaign today announced that the Ames rally on Sunday will be moved to a larger venue.
"Interest in Senator Obama's visit has been larger than we anticipated and we want to make sure that no one is left outside in this cold weather," said Obama Iowa campaign State Director Paul Tewes.
