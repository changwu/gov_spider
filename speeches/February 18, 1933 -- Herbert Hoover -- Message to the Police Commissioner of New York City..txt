
    [Released February 18, 1933. Dated February 16, 1933]
My dear Mr. Commissioner:
I want to assure you of my grateful appreciation of the fine arrangements made by your Department for my recent visit.
The valued service rendered by the members of the force added much to the comfort of the party.
Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Commissioner Edward P. Mulrooney, Police Headquarters, Center Street, New York, New York]
