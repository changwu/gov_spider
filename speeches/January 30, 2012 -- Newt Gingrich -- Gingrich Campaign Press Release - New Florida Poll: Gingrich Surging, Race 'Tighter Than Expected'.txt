
    
A new InsiderAdvantage poll conducted Sunday night in Florida shows late breaking movement to Newt Gingrich. Newt has narrowed the gap to just five points, the poll of 646 likely Republican voters shows:
Romney - 36%
Gingrich - 31%
Santorum - 12%
Paul - 12%
Other/Undecided - 9%
"The race will be tighter than expected," Matt Towery, chief pollster of InsiderAdvantage said in an interview with Newsmax.
