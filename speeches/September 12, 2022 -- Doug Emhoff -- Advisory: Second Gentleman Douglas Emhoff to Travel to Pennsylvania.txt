
    On Thursday, September 15th, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will travel to Allentown, Pennsylvania and Philadelphia, Pennsylvania to join Secretary Miguel Cardona on the Road to Success Back to School Bus Tour. In Allentown, the Second Gentleman and Secretary Cardona will highlight how community schools are helping students recover academically and obtain the mental health support they need. In Philadelphia, they will discuss the Biden-Harris Administration's plans to provide debt relief to millions, including teachers and administrators through Public Service Loan Forgiveness.
