
    On Thursday, February 16, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will travel to Dallas, Texas to deliver remarks at the BBYO International Convention. The Second Gentleman will also participate in a roundtable discussion with BBYO attendees on combating antisemitism in the United States. The remarks and roundtable will be open to pre-credentialed media.
