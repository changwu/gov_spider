
    President Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Bamako, Mali to attend the Presidential Inauguration of His Excellency Amadou Toumani Toure, President of the Republic of Mali, on June 8, 2007. The delegation will include the following individuals:
Head of Delegation:
The Honorable Mike Johanns, Secretary of Agriculture.
Delegates:
The Honorable Josephine K. Olsen, Deputy Director of the Peace Corps;
Morgan W. Davis, Chairman, Global Strategies for HIV Prevention;
Mary Beth Leonard, Deputy Chief of Mission, U.S. Embassy, Mali;
Steven Phillips, Medical Director, Global Issues and Projects, Exxon Mobil Corporation;
Judy Van Rest, Executive Vice President, International Republican Institute.
