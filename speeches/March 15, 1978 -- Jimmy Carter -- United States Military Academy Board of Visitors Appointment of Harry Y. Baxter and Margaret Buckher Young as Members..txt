
    The President today announced the appointment of Harry Y. Baxter and Margaret Buckner Young as members of the Board of Visitors to the United States Military Academy for terms expiring December 30, 1980.
Baxter, 47, of Burlington, Iowa, is a stockbroker with Schoff & Baxter.
Young, 56, of New Rochelle, N.Y., is the author of several children's books and taught at Kentucky State College and Atlanta University.
