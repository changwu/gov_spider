
    Senate Majority Leader Bob Dole has scheduled a visit to Springfield on Sunday, March 17th and Monday, March 18th. The stop includes a rally in the Rotunda of the Illinois State Capitol. Media planning to cover the rally may receive Dole for President credentials in the Press Room of the State Capitol from 8:30 am to 11:30 am on Monday, March 18th. Media will be asked to provide local or national media credentials. Media may also cover the arrival and departure of the Senator's aircraft, "Leader's Ship," at Springfield Airport. Media planning to cover the airport events should arrive 30 minutes before the events for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national credentials.
