
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Roger William Jepsen to be a member of the National Credit Union Administration Board for the term of 6 years expiring August 2, 1993. This is a reappointment.
Since 1985 Senator Jepsen has been a member and chairman of the National Credit Union Administration Board. Prior to this he served as a United States Senator, from 1979 to 1984.
Senator Jepsen graduated from Arizona State University (B.A., 1950; M.B.A., 1953). He served in the U.S. Army, 1946-1947, and the Army Reserve, 1948-1960. Senator Jepsen was born December 23, 1928, in Cedar Falls, IA. He is married, has six children, and resides in Fairfax, VA.
