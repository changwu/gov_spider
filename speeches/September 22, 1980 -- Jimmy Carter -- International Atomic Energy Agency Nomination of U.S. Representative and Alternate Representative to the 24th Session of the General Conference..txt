
    On August 26, the President nominated John C. Sawhill as Representative of the United States to the 24th Session of the General Conference of the International Atomic Energy Agency, and Gerard C. Smith and Roger Kirk as Alternate Representatives. The Conference is being held September 22 to 26 in Vienna, Austria.
Mr. Sawhill has requested that the President withdraw his nomination, as he will be unable to attend the Conference. The President has done so and has nominated Mr. Smith as the U.S. Representative. To replace him as Alternate Representative, the President has nominated Joseph M. Hendrie, Commissioner of the Nuclear Regulatory Commission.
