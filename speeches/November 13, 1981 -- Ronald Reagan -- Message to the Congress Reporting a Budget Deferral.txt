
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one deferral of $108 thousand in fiscal year 1982 funds.
This action is taken to restrain spending of funds made available by the Continuing Resolution, P.L. 97-51.
The deferral contained in this message is for the Department of Interior's Historic Preservation Fund.
The details of the deferral are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
November 13, 1981.
