
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Margaret Archambault to be a member of the Board of Trustees of the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, Smithsonian Institution, for a term expiring September 1, 1996. She would succeed Orval Hansen.
Mrs. Archambault has been chairman of the Women's Board of the United Service Organization in Chicago since 1986 and was president of the Women's Board in 1985. She has been the Illinois representative on the Advisory Committee on the Arts for the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts.
Mrs. Archambault was born June 22, 1918, in Butte, MT. She is married, has three children, and resides in Chicago, IL.
