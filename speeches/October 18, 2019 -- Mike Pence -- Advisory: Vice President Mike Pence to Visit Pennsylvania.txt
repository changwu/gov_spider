
    On Monday, October 21st, Vice President Pence will travel to Duryea, PA. There, the Vice President will visit Schott North America Inc, a technology based group of glass manufacturers and developers. The Vice President will tour the facility and deliver remarks highlighting the administration's efforts to pass the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement as part of a National Association of Manufacturer's event
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
