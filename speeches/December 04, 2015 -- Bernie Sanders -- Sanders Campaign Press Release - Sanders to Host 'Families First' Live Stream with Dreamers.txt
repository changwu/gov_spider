
    BURLINGTON, Vt – U.S. Sen. Bernie Sanders on Monday will host a live telecast to discuss his bold "Families First" immigration plan, which focuses on common sense reforms to build the middle class and embrace our nation's diversity. The Democratic Party presidential candidate will be joined by Dreamers, undocumented families from the greater Washington, D.C. area, an unaccompanied minor from El Salvador and Erika Andiola, a Dreamer and Sanders campaign Latino outreach strategist.
