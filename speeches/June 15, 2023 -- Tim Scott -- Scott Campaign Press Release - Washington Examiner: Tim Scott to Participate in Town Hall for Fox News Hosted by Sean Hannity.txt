
    Presidential candidate Sen. Tim Scott (R-SC) will participate in a town hall event for Fox News this month.
Sean Hannity will host the town hall in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, on June 20.
The event will air on Fox News's Hannity at 9 p.m.
This will be the second candidate town hall hosted by the network, the first being with former President Donald Trump on June 1.
