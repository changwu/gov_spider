
    The President today announced the recess appointment of Vernon Thomson as a member of the Federal Election Commission. He replaces Max L. Friedersdorf, who is resigning.
Thomson, 75, was a member of the FEC from 1976 until 1979, when he retired. He was a Member of the House of Representatives from 1961 to 1974 and Governor of Wisconsin from 1956 to 1958.
