
    Today, the DNC announced that six candidates have been invited to participate in the ninth Democratic presidential primary debate. Each candidate was invited based on the qualification criteria agreed to by the DNC and NBC News. The debate will take place tonight in Las Vegas, Nevada.
Based on the qualification criteria agreed to by the DNC and NBC News, and announced in January 2020, the following candidates have been invited to participate in the ninth primary debate:
Vice President Joe Biden
Mayor Michael Bloomberg
Mayor Pete Buttigieg
Sen. Amy Klobuchar
Sen. Bernie Sanders
Sen. Elizabeth Warren
