
    TALLAHASSEE, FL -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that U.S. Senator Joe Lieberman (I-CT) will participate in a town hall meeting in Surfside, Florida on Monday, July 21st to discuss key issues affecting South Florida, the American Jewish community and the upcoming Presidential election.

WHO: Senator Joe Lieberman (I-CT)
WHAT: Town Hall Meeting
WHEN: Monday, July 21, 2008 at 1:00 p.m. EDT
WHERE: The Shul of Bal Harbour
