
    On Tuesday, September 12th, an event will be held at the White House to celebrate the 2023 Praemium Imperiale Laureates. The event will feature remarks from First Lady Jill Biden and former Secretary of State Hillary Rodham Clinton.
The Praemium Imperiale is a global arts prize awarded annually by the Japan Art Association for lifetime achievement in the arts. The Praemium Imperiale Laureates were first celebrated at the White House by President Clinton and Mrs. Clinton in 1994.
This event will be open to pre-credentialed media. This event will also be livestreamed via whitehouse.gov/live. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Friday, September 8th at 6:00 PM ET.
