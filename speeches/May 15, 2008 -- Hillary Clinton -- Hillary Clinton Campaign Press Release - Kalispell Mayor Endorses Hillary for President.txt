
    Kalispell Mayor Pam Kennedy today endorsed Hillary Clinton for President, citing her long record of standing up for Americans and delivering results.
"Hillary Clinton has been standing up for women and families throughout her long career in public service," Kennedy said. "She is the candidate who best understands the issues facing Montana families."
"I am extremely honored to be supported by great Montana leaders across this great state," Clinton said. "From delivering health care to all Montanans to boosting the economy, I look forward to working with Mayor Kennedy on the pressing issues facing Flathead Valley residents when I'm President."
Pamela B. Kennedy has served as mayor of Kalispell since January 2002.
