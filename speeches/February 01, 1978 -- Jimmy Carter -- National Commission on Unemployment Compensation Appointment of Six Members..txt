
    The President today announced the appointment of six persons as members of the National Commission on Unemployment Compensation:
WILBUR J. COHEN, dean of the School of Education and professor of education, and professor of public welfare administration in the School of Social Work, at the University of Michigan;
BEATRICE COLEMAN, president, chairman of the board, and chief executive and operating officer of Maidenform, Inc.;
WILBUR DANIELS, executive vice president of the ILGWU;
J. ELDRED HILL, JR., executive director of UBA, Inc., a Washington research and consulting firm dealing with unemployment and workmen's compensation legislation;
KEN MORRIS, international executive board member and director of Region 1-B of the United Auto Workers;
JAMES R. O'BRIEN, assistant director of the AFL-CIO department of social security.
