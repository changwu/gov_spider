
    SANTA FE, NM-- New Mexico Governor and Democratic Presidential candidate Bill Richardson will campaign in Iowa on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday of this week. He will give a speech entitled "Veterans and their Families-- The Cost of the Iraq War" on Thursday, December 20, and he will unveil his national mental health policy on Friday, December 21. Governor Richardson will return to Iowa on Wednesday, December 26.
