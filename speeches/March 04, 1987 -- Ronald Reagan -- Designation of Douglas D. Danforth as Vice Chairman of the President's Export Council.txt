
    The President announced his intention to designate Douglas D. Danforth, of Pennsylvania, Vice Chairman of the President's Export Council.
Mr. Danforth was first appointed to the President's Export Council in September 1985. He currently serves as chairman and chief executive officer, Westinghouse Electric Corp., in Pittsburgh, PA.
Mr. Danforth graduated from Syracuse University (B.S., 1947). Mr. Danforth is married, has four children, and resides in Venetia, PA. He was born September 25, 1922, in Syracuse, NY.
