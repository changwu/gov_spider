In-town pool report #9 - Schumer/Jeffries debt limit
House and Senate Democratic leaders appeared outside the West Wing after their meeting with the president and vice president. Schumer and Jeffries delivered brief statements but did not entertain questions.
Schumer said that the Democrats were calling on the Republicans to outline their plans for addressing the debt limit. As always check quotes against the transcripts.
"They've made commitments to put bills on the floor by regular order. Well, let's see what their plan is. Let's see what their plan is on the debt ceiling," Schumer said. "Do they want to cut Social Security, do they want to cut Medicare, do they want to cut veterans benefits, do they want to cut police? Do they want to cut food for needy kids"
Jeffries said, speaking of the debt limit, that the bills need to be paid that Congress has already incurred but added, "We're ready to have a common sense, forward looking discussion about the future, through the appropriate mechanism. The budget process, the appropriation process."
