
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report eight new deferrals of budget authority for 1985 totaling $107,881,834. The deferrals affect the Departments of Energy, Justice, and State, the Board for International Broadcasting and the United States Information Agency.
The details of these deferrals are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
October 31, 1984.
