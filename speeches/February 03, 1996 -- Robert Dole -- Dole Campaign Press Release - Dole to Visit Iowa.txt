
    Senate Majority Leader Bob Dole will visit Iowa on February 4, 1996. He will attend events in Spencer, Storm Lake and Council Bluffs. Mrs. Elizabeth Dote will join Senator Dole in Spencer, Iowa. Senator Chuck Grassley will join the Senator at all events throughout the day and Congressman Tom Latham will attend the Spencer and Storm Lake events. Media attending should arrive thirty minutes early for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials.
