
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to nominate one individual and designate one individual to serve in his administration:
The President intends to nominate Ann C. Rosenthal of Iowa, to be a Member of the Board of Directors of the National Institute of Building Sciences, for the remainder of a three-year term expiring September 7, 2003 and an additional three-year term expiring September 7, 2006.
The President intends to designate Christopher A. Wray of Georgia, to be Acting Assistant Attorney General (Criminal Division).
