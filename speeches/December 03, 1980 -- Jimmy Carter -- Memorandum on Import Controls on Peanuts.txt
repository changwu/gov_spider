
    Memorandum for the United States Trade Representative
By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and statutes of the United States, including Section 301 of Title 3 of the United States Code, I hereby authorize you to perform on my behalf, with respect to the importation of peanuts only, all functions vested in me by Section 22 of the Agriculture Adjustment Act of 1933, as amended (7 U.S.C. 624).
This delegation of authority shall expire on January 20, 1981.
This memorandum shall be published in the FEDERAL REGISTER.
JIMMY CARTER
