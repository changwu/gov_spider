
    Notable Mississippi newspapers and political leaders agree that Ohio Gov. John Kasich is the Republican Party's best choice for the presidential nomination and encouraged Mississippi voters to vote for him in the upcoming March 8, 2016, Republican primary election.
This week, John Kasich received the endorsements of all five Mississippi newspapers that endorsed a candidate for the Republican nomination: theNatchez Democrat, Greenwood Commonwealth, Clarion-Ledger, Neshoba Democrat and Madison County Journal.
Below is a list of Mississippi public officials who endorse Gov. Kasich.
