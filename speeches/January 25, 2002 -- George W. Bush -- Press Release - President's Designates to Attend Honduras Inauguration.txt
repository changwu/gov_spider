
    President George W. Bush today announced his designation of the following six individuals to represent the United States at the Inauguration of his Excellency Ricardo Maduro Joest, President of Honduras on January 27, 2002.
The Honorable Gale Norton, Secretary of the Interior will serve as the leader of the delegation.
The Honorable Frank Almaguer, United States Ambassador to HondurasThe Honorable Otto Reich, Assistant Secretary of State for Western Hemisphere AffairsMr. William P. "Scott" Bush, Chief Marketing Officer, Maritz Inc.Mr. Perfecto Rivera, Vice President of Community Relations, Merchants and Manufacturers BancorpMr. Jack Vaughan, Jr., Vice-Chairman, Vaughan Petroleum, Inc.
