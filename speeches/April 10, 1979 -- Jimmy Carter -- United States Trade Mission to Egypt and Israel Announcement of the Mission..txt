
    The President has asked Special Trade Representative Robert Strauss to lead a U.S. trade mission to Egypt and Israel from April 16 to April 20.
In meetings with trade, business, and labor officials of both countries, the U.S. delegation will explore whys to carry out the President's commitment to increased U.S. trade and investment in both Egypt and Israel.
The group will include Members of Congress and representatives of U.S. agencies, as well as industry and labor leaders. They will leave Washington for Cairo on April 16 and will spend April 17 and 18 in Egypt and April 19 and 20 in Israel.
