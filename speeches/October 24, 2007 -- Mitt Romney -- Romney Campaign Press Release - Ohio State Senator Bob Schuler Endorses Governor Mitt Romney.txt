Background On Ohio State Senator Bob Schuler:
State Senator Bob Schuler Has Served The 7th Senate District Of Ohio Since 2003. He is currently the chair of the Energy and Public Utilities Committee, and has served as a member on a number of additional committees. He has been the Executive Committee Chairman for the Planning Partnership, a board member for the Glad House, sits on the Scarlet Oaks School's Business Advisory Council, is the host of Issues Forum TV Show and is a volunteer for both Tall Stacks and Habitat for Humanity.
He began his political career as a member of the Deer Park City Council from 1974-1985. From 1988-1992, Schuler served as a Sycamore Township Trustee. He then served in the Ohio State House of Representatives from 1993-2000. State Senator Schuler has been a member of various committees in Hamilton County, and has won many awards for his public service to the people of Ohio.
