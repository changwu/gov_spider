
    
  Continues pattern of huge crowds at Granite State town hall meetings

CONCORD, NH - 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul continued his final swing through New Hampshire with a jam-packed town hall this afternoon in Meredith.

Following strong back-to-back performances in the last two GOP debates before the primary on Tuesday, Paul spoke to a crowd of 500 supporters, undecided voters, and media.
"This is what a surge looks like," said State. Sen. Jim Forsythe, Ron Paul 2012 New Hampshire campaign chair.
