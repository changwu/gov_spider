
    On Wednesday, June 22nd at 3:00 PM ET, First Lady Jill Biden will host a screening of the PBS documentary, Hiding in Plain Sight: Youth Mental Illness. The documentary follows the mental health journeys of more than 20 young Americans, and features accounts from their parents, teachers, friends, health care providers, and mental health experts. Executive producer Ken Burns, Director/Co-Producer Erik Ewers, Director/Co-Producer Christopher Loren Ewers, the creative team, and several youth from the film will attend the screening, which will be held in the East Room of the White House. Remarks before the screening will be open press. For interested media, please RSVP HERE by Tuesday 6/21 at 12:00 PM ET.
