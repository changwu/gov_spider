
    On Sunday, March 6th, Vice President Kamala Harris will travel to Selma, Alabama to walk across the Edmund Pettus Bridge to commemorate the Anniversary of Bloody Sunday. She will also meet with civil rights leaders and deliver remarks.
The Vice President will be joined by Secretary of Housing and Urban Development Marcia Fudge, Secretary of Transportation Pete Buttigieg, Secretary of Education Miguel Cardona, Environmental Protection Agency Administrator Michael Regan, and Deputy Secretary of Veteran Affairs Donald Remy. Additional details to follow.
