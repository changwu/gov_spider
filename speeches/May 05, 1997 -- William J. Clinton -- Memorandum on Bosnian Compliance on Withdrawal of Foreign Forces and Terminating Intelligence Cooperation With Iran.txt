
    Presidential Determination No. 97–22
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject: Bosnian Compliance on Withdrawal of Foreign Forces and Terminating Intelligence Cooperation With Iran
Pursuant to Public Law 104–208, I hereby determine and certify that the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina has complied with Article III of Annex 1–A of the General Framework Agreement for Peace in Bosnia and Herzegovina concerning the withdrawal of foreign forces; and that intelligence cooperation on training, investigations, and related activities between Iranian officials and Bosnian officials has been terminated.
You are authorized and directed to transmit this determination and certification to Congress and to arrange for its publication in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, May 5, 1997.
