
    President Donald J. Trump and First Lady Melania Trump today announced the official reopening of public tours at the White House. Public tours must be requested through Members of Congress, and requests may be submitted up to three months in advance. Tours are scheduled on a first-come-first-served basis.
Tours are self-guided, last approximately 45 minutes, and are available during the following hours:
Tuesday – Thursday from 7:30 AM to 11:30 AM
Friday – Saturday from 7:30 AM to 1:30 PM
Further details, including what visitors will need to access the White House campus, can be found at https://www.whitehouse.gov/participate/tours-and-events.
In addition, the 24-hour Visitors Office information line is available at 202-456-7041.
