
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the President's Commission on the United States Postal Service:
James A. Johnson of the District of Columbia, and upon appointment designate Co-Chairman
Harry Pearce of Michigan, and upon appointment designate Co-Chairman
Richard C. Levin of Connecticut
Norman I. Seabrook of New York
Carolyn L. Gallagher of Texas
Robert S. Walker of Pennsylvania
Joseph R. Wright of New York
Don V. Cogman of Arizona
Dionel E. Aviles of Texas
