
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Robert G. Dederick to be Under Secretary of Commerce for Economic Affairs. This is a new position.
He is presently serving as Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Economic Affairs. He was with the Northern Trust Co. in 1964-1981, serving as senior vice president and chief economist in 1970-1981. He was with New England Mutual Life Insurance Co. in 1957-1964.
He graduated from Harvard University (A.B., A.M., Ph.D.). He is married, has three children, and resides in Hinsdale, Ill. He was born November 18, 199.9.
