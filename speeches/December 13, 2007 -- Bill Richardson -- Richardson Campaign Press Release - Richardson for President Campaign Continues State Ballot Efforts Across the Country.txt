
    SANTA FE, NM-- The Richardson for President campaign today announced that New Mexico Governor Bill Richardson is on the primary ballot in twenty states and the District of Columbia. Richardson will be represented at the caucuses in an additional nine states.
The Richardson campaign today filed in Alaska, Arizona, and Louisiana, bringing the combined primary and caucus state total up to 29 states and the District of Columbia.
"My campaign is gaining organizational strength every single day," Richardson said. "I am very proud of my hardworking staff and volunteers. They have been essential in helping me run an energized, grassroots campaign across the country."
