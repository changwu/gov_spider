
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report 8 new deferrals of budget authority for 1986 totaling $2,023,327,275. The deferrals affect accounts in Funds Appropriated to the President, the Departments of Commerce, Defense-Military, Health and Human Services, Transportation, and Treasury.
The details of these deferrals are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
November 25, 1985.
