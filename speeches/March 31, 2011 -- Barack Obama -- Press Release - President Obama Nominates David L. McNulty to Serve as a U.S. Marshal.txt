
    WASHINGTON- Today, President Obama nominated David L. McNulty to serve as a U.S. Marshal in the state of New York.
"Throughout his career, David L. McNulty has been a dedicated law enforcement professional who has shown an unwavering commitment to public service," said President Obama. "I am honored to nominate him to serve the American people as a U.S. Marshal."
David L. McNulty: Nominee for United States Marshal for the Northern District of New York
David L. McNulty currently serves as a Major with the New York State Police in Albany, New York. Major McNulty rose through the New York State Police ranks after joining as a Trooper in 1978.
