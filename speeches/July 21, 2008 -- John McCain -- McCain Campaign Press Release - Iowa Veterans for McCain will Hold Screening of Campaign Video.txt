
    DES MOINES, IA -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Iowa Veterans for John McCain will host a screening ofthe campaign video entitled, "The Obama Iraq Documentary: Whatever the Politics Demand" on Tuesday, July 22nd. Iowa Veterans for McCain Chairman Major General, (Ret.) Evan (Curly) Hultman and Vice Chairman Colonel Charles (Chuck) Larson Sr. will speak. This event occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across the country.
