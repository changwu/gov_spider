
    Rudy Giuliani Presidential Campaign Manager Mike DuHaime made the following statement today regarding the signing of legislation moving the New York state primary to February 5th:
"Rudy Giuliani is the Republican candidate who can compete and win in New York State. Voters here know first-hand that Rudy is a true leader and proven problem-solver who has made a real difference in people's lives. He will bring the prosperity New York City saw under his tenure as Mayor to the entire country."
