
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Mexico City, Mexico to attend the inauguration of His Excellency Felipe de Jesus Calderon Hinojosa, President of the United Mexican States on December 1, 2006.
The Honorable George H.W. Bush, Former President of the United States, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Antonio O. Garza, United States Ambassador to Mexico
The Honorable Alberto R. Gonzales, Attorney General of the United States
The Honorable Carlos M. Gutierrez, Secretary of Commerce
