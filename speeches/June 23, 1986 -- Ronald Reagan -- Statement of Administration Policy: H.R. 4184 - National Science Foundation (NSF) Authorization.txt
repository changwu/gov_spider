
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
FINAL
(REVISION)(House)(Fuqua (D) Florida)
H.R. 4184 is strongly opposed by the Administration. Only if the following provisions were deleted would the Administration support H.R. 4184:
(1) prohibit the use of funds to implement changes to OMB Circular A-21, made subsequent to February 11, 1986;
(2) eliminate the Presidential appointment of four NSF Assistant Directors; and
(3) provide NSF with authority to indemnify grantees, contractors, and subcontractors associated with the Ocean Drilling Program.
