
    On Wednesday, February 19th, the Vice President will travel to Hampton, VA. There, the Vice President will visit the NASA Langley Research Center. At Langley, the Vice President will tour the facility and deliver remarks to NASA employees. Following, the Vice President will visit Hampton University's Proton Therapy Institute in celebration of Black History Month and to highlight the university's efforts in providing state-of-the art cancer research and treatment. There, the Vice President will tour the world's largest free-standing proton therapy facility and participate in a roundtable with Hampton University students, staff, and patients. Later that day, the Vice President will travel to Virginia Beach and visit with Naval Special Warfare Operators.
