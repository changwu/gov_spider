
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend a ceremony commemorating the 65th Anniversary of the Warsaw Ghetto Uprising in Warsaw, Poland on April 15, 2008.
The Honorable Michael Chertoff, Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Victor Ashe, U.S. Ambassador to Poland
The Honorable Phyllis Heideman, Managing Partner, Heideman Nudelman & Kalik, PC
David Mitzner, Founder and President, RIDA Development Corporation
Bill Lowenberg, President, Lowenberg Corporation
