
    President Bush to Designate Three Individuals to Serve in His Administration
President George W. Bush today announced his intention to designate three individuals to serve in his administration.
The President intends to designate Julie Nixon Eisenhower of Pennsylvania, to be Chairman of the President's Commission on White House Fellowships.
The President intends to designate Vance D. Coffman of Maryland, to be Chairman of the President's National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee.
The President intends to designate F. Duane Ackerman of Georgia, to be Vice Chairman of the President's National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee.
