
    HOUSTON, Texas – The Cruz for President campaign announced a new TV ad, "Victories", running statewide in Iowa through January 3rd. The ad highlights Cruz's victories defending the Mojave Memorial Cross, the Texas Capitol Ten Commandments monument, the Second Amendment, and the words "under God" in the Pledge of Allegiance. The ad may be viewed here.
