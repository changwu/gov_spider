
    [Released October 11, 1930. Dated October 10, 1930]
MY FELLOW CITIZENS join with me in extending to Your Excellency cordial greetings on this auspicious anniversary. We earnestly desire for your country the blessings of peace and prosperity.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency, Chiang Chung-cheng, President of the National Government of the Republic of China, Nanking, China]
