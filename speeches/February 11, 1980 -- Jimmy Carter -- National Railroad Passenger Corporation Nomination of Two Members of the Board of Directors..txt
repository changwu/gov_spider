
    The President today announced that he will nominate two persons for reappointment as members of the Board of Directors of the National Railroad Passenger Corporation for terms expiring July 18, 1982. They are:
James R. Mills, of San Diego, Calif., president pro tem of the California State Senate. Mills has served in the senate since 1966. He is chairman of the Transportation Committee of the National Conference of State Legislatures.
Frank H. Neel, of Thomasville, Ga., president of Neel Air Conditioning and Heating Co. He was a member of the T'homasville Board of Commissioners for 12 years.
