
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report six deferrals of budget authority totaling $96,285,288.
The deferrals affect programs in the Departments of Energy, Health and Human Services, and Justice.
The details of these deferrals are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
October 29, 1987.
