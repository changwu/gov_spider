
    President Bush will welcome President Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva of Brazil to Camp David on March 31, 2007. The President looks forward to exploring ways to deepen the U.S.-Brazil relationship with President Lula, including working together to strengthen democracy and international economic cooperation and to develop alternative energy sources. President Lula hosted President Bush at his country home outside Brasilia in November 2005, and President Lula will welcome the President in Sao Paulo on March 9, 2007.
