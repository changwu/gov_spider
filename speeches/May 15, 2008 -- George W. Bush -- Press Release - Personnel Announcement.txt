
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Bethlehem to attend the Palestine Investment Conference May 21-23, 2008.
The Honorable Robert M. Kimmitt, Deputy Secretary, Department of the Treasury, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable John J. Sullivan, Deputy Secretary, Department of Commerce
The Honorable Robert Mosbacher, Jr., President and Chief Executive Officer, Overseas Private Investment Corporation
The Honorable Larry W. Walther, Director, U.S. Trade and Development Agency
Mr. Walter Isaacson, President and Chief Executive Officer, Aspen Institute Chair, U.S.-Palestinian Partnership
Dr. Ziad Asali, President & Founder, American Task Force on Palestine Co-Chair, U.S.-Palestinian Partnership
