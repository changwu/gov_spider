
    On Friday, March 23, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 2254, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Janet Capello Post Office Building;
H.R. 2302, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Dr. John F. Nash, Jr. Post Office;
H.R. 2464, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the John Fitzgerald Kennedy Post Office;
H.R. 2672, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Sgt. Douglas J. Riney Post Office;
H.R. 2815, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Gunnery Sergeant John Basilone Post Office;
H.R. 2873, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Staff Sergeant Peter Taub Post Office Building;
H.R. 3109, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Sr. Chief Ryan Owens Post Office Building;
H.R. 3369, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Howard B. Pate, Jr. Post Office;
H.R. 3638, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Rutledge Pearson Post Office Building;
H.R. 3655, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Dr. Walter S. McAfee Post Office Building;
H.R. 3821, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Zack T. Addington Post Office;
H.R. 3893, which designates a facility of the Unites States Postal Service as the Robert H. Jenkins, Jr. Post Office;
H.R. 4042, which designates a facility of the Unites States Postal Service as the Borinqueneers Post Office Building; and
H.R. 4285, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the James C. 'Billy' Johnson Post Office Building.
