
    The President today announced his intention to appoint the following individuals to be members of the Advisory Committee on the Arts of the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, Smithsonian Institution:
Leota Hays is a general partner of Franklin Investment. She has two children and resides in Jackson, Miss.
Thomas D. Higgins is a computer systems manager for Union Carbide. He is married, has four children, and resides in Charleston, W. Va.
Peggy Mallick is a political, civic, and cultural contributor in the community. She is married, has one child, and resides in Casper, Wyo.
Lindsay J. Morgenthaler is active in civic and cultural affairs in the community. She is married, has four children, and resides in Cleveland, Ohio.
