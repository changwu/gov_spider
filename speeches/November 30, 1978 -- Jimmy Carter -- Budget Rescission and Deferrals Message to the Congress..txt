
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith propose rescission of $75,000 in unneeded funds appropriated to the Foreign Claims Settlement Commission.
In addition, I and reporting four new deferrals of budget authority totalling $889 million and two revisions to previously transmitted deferrals increasing the amount deferred by $21.4 million in budget authority. These items involve the military assistance program and programs in the Departments of Commerce, Defense, Justice, and State.
The details of the rescission proposal and the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
JIMMY CARTER
The White House,
November 30, 1978.
