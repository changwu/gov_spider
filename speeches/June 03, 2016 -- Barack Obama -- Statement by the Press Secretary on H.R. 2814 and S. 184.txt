
    On Friday, June 3, 2016, the President signed into law:
H.R. 2814, which designates the Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in Sevierville, Tennessee, the Dannie A. Carr Veterans Outpatient Clinic; and
S. 184, the "Native American Children's Safety Act," which amends the Indian Child Protection and Family Violence Prevention Act to require background checks before foster care placements are made by tribal social services agencies.
