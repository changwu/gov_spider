
    On Wednesday, August 2, 2006, the President has signed into law:
H.R. 4456, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Hattie W. Caraway Station;
H.R. 4561, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Francisco "Pancho" Medrano Post Office Building;
H.R. 4688, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Mayor John Thompson "Tom" Garrison Memorial Post Office;
H.R. 4786, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the H. Gordon Payrow Post Office Building;
H.R. 4995, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Ronald Bucca Post Office; and
H.R. 5245, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Matthew Lyon Post Office Building.
