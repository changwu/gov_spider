
    The President today announced that he will nominate Thomas R. Donahue, of Washington, D.C., to be a member of the Board of Directors of the Communications Satellite Corporation. He would replace the late George Meany.
Donahue is secretary-treasurer of the AFL-CIO and was executive assistant to Meany from 1973 to 1979. He was Assistant Secretary of Labor for Labor-Management Relations from 1967 to 1969.
