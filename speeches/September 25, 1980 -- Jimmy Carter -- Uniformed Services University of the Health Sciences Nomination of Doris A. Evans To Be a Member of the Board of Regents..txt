
    The President today announced that he will nominate Doris A. Evans, of Cleveland Heights, Ohio, to be a member of the Board of Regents of the Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences for a term expiring in 1983.
Evans is a pediatrician and executive director of the Glenville Health Association in Cleveland. She is an assistant professor of community health and pediatrics at Case Western Reserve University School of Medicine.
