
    On Monday, September 25th at 4:00 PM ET, First Lady Jill Biden will host a welcome reception at the White House for the White House Historical Association (WHHA) 2023 Presidential Sites Summit. Dr. Biden will deliver remarks at the reception, along with John F.W. Rogers, Chairman of the WHHA Board of Directors. This event will be open to pre-credentialed media.
The WHHA Presidential Sites Summit which will take place from Monday, September 25th through Thursday, September 28th, is a conference held every two years that draws leaders from museums, historic homes, and presidential libraries across the country to share innovative ideas and discuss insights related to preserving our country's rich history.
