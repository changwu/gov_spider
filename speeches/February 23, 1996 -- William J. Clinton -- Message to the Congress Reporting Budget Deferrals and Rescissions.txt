
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report three new deferrals and one revised deferral, totaling $3.6 billion, and four rescission proposals of budgetary resources, totaling $140 million.
These deferrals affect the International Security Assistance programs as well as programs of the Agency for International Development.
The rescission proposals affect the Department of Defense.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, February 23, 1996.
