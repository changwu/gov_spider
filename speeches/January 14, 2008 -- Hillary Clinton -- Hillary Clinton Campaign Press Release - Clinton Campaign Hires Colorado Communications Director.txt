
    The Clinton Campaign today announced that Jessica Santillo has been named Colorado Communications Director.
"I am proud to be joining the tremendous team of dedicated staff and volunteers in Colorado," Santillo said. "Over the next month, we will wage a dynamic and vigorous effort to ensure that Hillary's message of real change and experience reaches every corner of the state."
Santillo joins the Colorado team after working on the Iowa campaign team. She is also a veteran of several congressional offices and campaigns.
