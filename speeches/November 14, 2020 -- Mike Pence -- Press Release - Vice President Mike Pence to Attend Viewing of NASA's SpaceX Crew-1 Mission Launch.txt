
    On Sunday, November 15th, Vice President Mike Pence and Second Lady Karen Pence will travel to Cape Canaveral, Florida. There, the Vice President and Second Lady will attend a viewing of NASA's SpaceX Crew-1 Mission Launch. The Resilience is the first operational spacecraft certified by NASA for commercial human flight to the International Space Station. President Trump's leadership has revitalized America's space enterprise and ensured the strength of the American space program for years to come. Later that evening, the Vice President and Second Lady will return to Washington, D.C.
