
    In another new poll, Data for Progress and Climate Power find that voters across the political spectrum back the Inflation Reduction Act. The poll finds:

Democrats (95%), Independents (73%), and Republicans (52 %) alike support the Inflation Reduction Act
Over half (51%) of voters think the Inflation Reduction Act will increase America's energy security.
Nearly 2 out of 3 of voters (64%) say they would be more likely to vote for a candidate who supports the Inflation Reduction Act.
