
    On Saturday, November 2nd, the Vice President will travel to Charlottesville, VA. Then, the Vice President will visit Patriot Industries, a family-owned business, in Louisa, VA. There, the Vice President will deliver remarks on the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement as part of an America First Policies series called "USMCA: A Better Deal for American Workers." Following, the Vice President will travel to Virginia Beach, VA. There, he will deliver remarks at a Republican State Leadership Committee's Get Out The Vote event.
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
