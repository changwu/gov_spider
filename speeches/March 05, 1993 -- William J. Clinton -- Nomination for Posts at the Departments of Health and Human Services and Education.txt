
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Bruce Vladeck to be Administrator of the Health Care Financing Administration at the Department of Health and Human Services and Norma Cantu to be Assistant Secretary for Civil Rights at the Department of Education. "I am very pleased to make these nominations today to two extremely important positions," said the President. "Bruce Vladeck and Norma Cantu are highly talented individuals with unique qualifications for the leadership roles that I have asked them to take."
