
    On Friday, April 13, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 4547, the "Strengthening Protections for Social Security Beneficiaries Act of 2018," which modifies oversight of the representative payee system for Social Security beneficiaries; and
S. 772, the "Ashlynne Mike AMBER Alert in Indian Country Act," which reauthorizes and amends the program that provides grants for AMBER Alert communication plans.
