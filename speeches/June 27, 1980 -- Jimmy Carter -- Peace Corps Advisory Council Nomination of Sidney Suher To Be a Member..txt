
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Sidney Suher, of Rochester, N.Y., to be a member of the Peace Corps Advisory Council. Suher has been a partner in the law firm of Suher & Heller since 1963.
He was born April 8, 1934, in Holyoke, Mass. He received a B.A. from Cornell University in 1957 and a J.D. from Boston University Law School in 1960.
