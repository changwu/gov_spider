
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to nominate Jesse Seroyer, Jr., to be United States Marshal for the Middle District of Alabama. Seroyer has been a Criminal Investigator with the State of Alabama Office of the Attorney General, where he has been promoted through the ranks to Deputy Chief Investigator. He served for eleven years with the Opelika Police Department and was promoted to the rank of detective. Seroyer is a graduate of Darden High School in Opelika.
