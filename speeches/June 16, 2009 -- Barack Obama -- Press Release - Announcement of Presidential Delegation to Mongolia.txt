
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Ts. Elbegdorj, President-elect of Mongolia, on June 18, 2009.
The Honorable Mark C. Minton, Ambassador of the United States to Mongolia, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
Mr. Nicholas M. Hill, Deputy Chief of Mission, Embassy of the United States to Mongolia
Mr. Stanley Roth, Former Assistant Secretary of State for East Asian & Pacific Affairs and current Vice President, International Government Relations, Boeing International
