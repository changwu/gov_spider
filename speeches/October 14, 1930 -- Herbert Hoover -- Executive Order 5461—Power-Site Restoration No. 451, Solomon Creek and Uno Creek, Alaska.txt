
    So much of the order of July 31, 1915, creating Power-Site Reserve No. 491, as affects lands hereinafter described is hereby revoked:
All lands within one-quarter of a mile of the stream flowing in Solomon Gulch between the point of discharge of said stream into Port Valdez at mean high tide, and a point 2 miles upstream from said point of discharge; except such lands as are included in Project No. 1101 as defined by the Federal Power Commission in a notice to the General Land Office dated June 25, 1930;
All lands within one-quarter of a mile of Uno Creek between its point of discharge into Shoup Bay, an arm of Port Valdez, at mean high tide, and a point 1% miles upstream from said point of discharge.
HERBERT HOOVER
The White House,October 14, 1930
