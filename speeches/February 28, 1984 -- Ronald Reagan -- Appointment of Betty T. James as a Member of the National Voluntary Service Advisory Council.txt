
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Betty T. James to be a member of the National Voluntary Service Advisory Council. She will succeed C. William Verity.
Mrs. James is a homemaker in New York City. Before moving to New York, she was active in community affairs and fund-raising for various private institutions in southern California.
She graduated from San Jose State University (B.A., 1955). She is married and resides in New York City. Mrs James was born August 19, 1933, in Amarillo, TX.
