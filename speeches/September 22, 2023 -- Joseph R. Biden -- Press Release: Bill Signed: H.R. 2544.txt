
    On Friday, September 22, 2023, the President signed into law:
H.R. 2544, the "Securing the U.S. Organ Procurement and Transplantation Network Act," which allows for the award of multiple grants, contracts, or cooperative agreements to operate the Organ Procurement and Transplantation Network.
Thank you to Representatives Bucshon and Kelly and Senators Wyden, Grassley, Cardin, Young, and Cassidy for their leadership.
