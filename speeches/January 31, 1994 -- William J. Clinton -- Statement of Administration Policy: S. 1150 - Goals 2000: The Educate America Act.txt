
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(SENT 2/1/94)(Senate)(Sen. Kennedy (D) MA)
The Administration strongly supports Senate passage of S. 1150. The bill would: (1)improve learning and teaching by providing a national framework for education reform so that all students can reach internationally competitive standards, (2) enable our Nation to reach the National Education Goals, and (3) establish a national board to promote the development and adoption of a voluntary national system of occupational skill standards.
