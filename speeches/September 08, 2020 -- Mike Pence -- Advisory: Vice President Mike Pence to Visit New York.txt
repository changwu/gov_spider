
    On Friday, September 11th, Vice President Mike Pence and Second Lady Karen Pence will travel to New York City, New York. There, the Vice President and Second Lady will visit Ground Zero and participate in The National September 11 Memorial & Museum's 19th Anniversary Commemoration Ceremony honoring and remembering those killed in the September 11th, 2001, terrorist attacks. Following, the Vice President and Second Lady will participate in the Tunnel to Towers Foundation's September 11th Memorial Ceremony. Later that day, the Vice President and Second Lady will return to Washington, D.C.
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
