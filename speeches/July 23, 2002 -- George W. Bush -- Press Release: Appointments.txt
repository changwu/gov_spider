
    President Bush to Appoint Two Individuals to Serve as Members of the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation
President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint two individuals to serve as Members of the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation for the remainder of a four-year term expiring June 10, 2006.
Susan Schanlaber of Illinois to serve as a Historic Preservation ExpertEmily Anne Rich Summers of Texas to serve as a Public representative
