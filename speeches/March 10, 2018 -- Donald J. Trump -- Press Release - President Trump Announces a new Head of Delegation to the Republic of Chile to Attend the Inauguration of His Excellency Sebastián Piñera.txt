
    President Donald J. Trump announced the designation of a new Head of Delegation to the Republic of Chile to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Sebastián Piñera on March 11, 2018, in Valparaiso, Chile.
The Honorable Alex M. Azar II, Secretary of Health and Human Services, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Carol Z. Perez, United States Ambassador to the Republic of Chile
