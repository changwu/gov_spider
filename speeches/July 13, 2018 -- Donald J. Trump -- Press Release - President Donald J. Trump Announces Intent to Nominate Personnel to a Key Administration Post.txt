
    President Donald J. Trump today announced his intent to nominate the following individual to a key position in his Administration:
Richard S. Tischner of Virginia, to be Director of the Agency for the District of Columbia Court Services and Offender Supervision for a six-year term.
Mr. Tischner currently serves as an Assistant United States Attorney and the Chief of the Superior Court Division at the United States Attorney's Office for the District of Columbia. Previously, he served as an attorney for the Merit Systems Protection Board and the Federal Trade Commission. Mr. Tischner has served as an attorney in the United States Federal Government for more than 33 years, including 30 years as an Assistant United States Attorney at the Department of Justice.
