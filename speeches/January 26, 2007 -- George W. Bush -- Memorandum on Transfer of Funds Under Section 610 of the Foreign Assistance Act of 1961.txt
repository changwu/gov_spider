
    Presidential Determination No. 2007-11
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject: Transfer of Funds Under Section 610 of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961
Pursuant to the authority vested in me by the Constitution and laws of the United States, including section 610 of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended (22 U.S.C. 2360) (the "Act"), I hereby determine it necessary for the purposes of that Act that up to $86.362 million in fiscal year 2006 funds made available under chapter 4 of part II of the Act be transferred to, and consolidated with, funds made available under chapter 8 of part I of the Act, and such funds are hereby so transferred and consolidated.
You are authorized and directed to report this determination to the Congress and to publish it in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush
