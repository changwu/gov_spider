
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Vento (D) Minnesota)
The Administration supports H.R. 3252.
Pay-As-You-Go Scoring
H.R. 3252 affects receipts and direct spending; therefore, it is subject to the pay-as-you-go (PAYGO) requirement of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act (OBRA) of 1990. OMB's preliminary PAYGO estimate is zero.
