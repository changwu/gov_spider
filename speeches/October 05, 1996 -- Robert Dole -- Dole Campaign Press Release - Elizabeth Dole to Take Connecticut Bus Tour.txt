
    Elizabeth Dole, wife of Republican presidential nominee Bob Dole, will be campaigning by bus through Connecticut ending up in New York on Monday, October 7, 1996. This first bus tour will begin in Hartford and travel through Waterbury, Fairfield and Stamford, Connecticut. Additional local stops will be made as the bus sweeps across the Connecticut countryside.
A separate bus and filing center in Fairfield and Stamford will be provided for the media. Media planning to travel on the bus should contact Mandy Locke to R.S.V.P. and plan to arrive at 7:30 a.m. at the Motel Hotel, 15 Farm Spring Road, Farmington, Connecticut, for check- in. Media should also be to present local, state or national media credentials. No exceptions will be made due to security precautions.
