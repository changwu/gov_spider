
    On Monday, March 6th, First Lady Jill Biden, U.S. Deputy Secretary of Labor and nominee for U.S. Secretary of Labor Julie Su, and U.S. Deputy Secretary of Energy David Turk will travel to Valparaiso, Indiana to highlight the Biden Administration's commitment to strengthening our economy and building pathways to good-paying jobs through career-connected learning.
At 12:45 PM, the First Lady, Deputy Secretary Su, and Deputy Secretary Turk will arrive at Porter County Regional Airport in Valparaiso, IN. This arrival will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Friday, March 3rd at 12:00 PM.
At 1:00 PM, the First Lady, Deputy Secretary Su, and Deputy Secretary Turk will visit Ivy Tech Community College in Valparaiso, IN to highlight the Biden-Harris Administration's commitment to career-connected learning programs that build pathways for good-paying jobs. This visit will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Friday, March 3rd at 12:00 PM.
**All times are local.**
