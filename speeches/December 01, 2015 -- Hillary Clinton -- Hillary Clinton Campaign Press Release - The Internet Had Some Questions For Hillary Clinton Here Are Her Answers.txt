Hillary is the first presidential candidate ever to take questions on the crowdsourced question-and-answer platform Quora. Here's what she had to say.

She took on Republican attacks against Planned Parenthood.



She laid out a detailed plan to lower prescription drug costs.



She defended the Affordable Care Act and pledged to do even more to make health care affordable.



And, of course, she talked about the prospect of being "grandmother-in-chief."
