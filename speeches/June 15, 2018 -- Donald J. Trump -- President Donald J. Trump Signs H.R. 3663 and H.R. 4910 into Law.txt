
    On Friday, June 15, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 3663, which designates the medical center of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Huntington, West Virginia, as the Hershel "Woody" Williams VA Medical Center; and
H.R. 4910, the "Veterans Cemetery Benefit Correction Act," which requires the National Park Service to furnish outer burial receptacles for veterans and service members buried in national cemeteries it manages.
