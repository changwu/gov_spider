
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report three new deferrals of budget authority totaling $16,118,000.
The deferrals affect Energy Activities and the Department of Justice.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
July 28, 1983.
