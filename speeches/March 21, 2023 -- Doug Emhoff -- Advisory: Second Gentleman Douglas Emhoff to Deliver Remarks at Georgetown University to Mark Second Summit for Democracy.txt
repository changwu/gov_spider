
    On Friday, March 24, ahead of the 2023 Summit for Democracy, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will deliver remarks at Georgetown University's "Promoting Equitable Gender Norms to Foster Democratic Resilience" event. The event will be co-hosted by the Georgetown Institute for Women, Peace and Security, the governments of Sweden and Romania, and International IDEA. In his remarks, the Second Gentleman will discuss the importance of advancing women's political participation and leadership around the world. This event will be open to pre-credentialed media.
