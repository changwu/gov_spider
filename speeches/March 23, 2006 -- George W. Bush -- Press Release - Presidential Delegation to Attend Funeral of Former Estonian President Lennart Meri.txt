
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Tallinn, Estonia to attend the Funeral of Former Estonian President Lennart Meri on March 26, 2006.
The Honorable David A. Sampson, Deputy Secretary of Commerce, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Aldona Zofia Wos, US Ambassador to Estonia
The Honorable Daniel Fried, Assistant Secretary of State for European and Eurasian Affairs
