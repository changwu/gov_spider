
    Presidential Determination No. 93-34
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to section 2(c)(1) of the Migration and Refugee Assistance Act of 1962, as amended, 22 U.S.C. 2601(c)(1), I hereby determine that it is important to the national interest that up to $ 6,300,000 of the $ 14,000,000 of the funds previously authorized for use in support of Angolan repatriation be available to meet the urgent and unexpected needs of Mozambican refugees and returnees. This determination amends Presidential Determination 92-39 of August 17, 1992, to permit the use of up to $ 6,300,000 of the funds authorized by that determination from the U.S. Emergency Refugee and Migration Assistance Fund to meet the needs of Mozambican refugees and returnees through contributions to the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees.
You are authorized and directed to inform the appropriate committees of the Congress of this determination and the obligation of funds under this authority, and to publish this determination in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, August 19, 1993.
