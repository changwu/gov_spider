
    On Friday, November 17, 2017, the President signed into law:
H.R. 304, the "Protecting Patient Access to Emergency Medications Act of 2017," which authorizes the Department of Justice to register certain providers of emergency medical services to dispense controlled substances; and
H.R. 3031, the "TSP Modernization Act of 2017," which provides flexibility in making withdrawals from a Thrift Savings Plan account.
