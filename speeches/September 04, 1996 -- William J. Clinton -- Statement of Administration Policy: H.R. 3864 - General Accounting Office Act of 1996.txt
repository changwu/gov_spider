
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICYThis Statement Has Been Coordinated by OMB with the Appropriate Agencies
(House)(LaTourette (R) OH and 3 cosponsors)
The Administration supports House passage of H.R. 3864 and may seek technical amendments as the bill is considered further by the Congress. The amendments could include requiring the Comptroller General to settle all claims and issue decisions on all requests that are received by the Comptroller General on or before the effective date of the legislation.
