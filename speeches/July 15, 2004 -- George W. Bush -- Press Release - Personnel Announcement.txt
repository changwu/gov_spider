
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the 2004 Olympic Games in Athens, Greece.
Former President George H.W. Bush will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are: Mrs. Barbara Bush The Honorable Thomas Miller, U.S. Ambassador to Greece Ms. Barbara Bush Ms. Jenna Bush, Archbishop Demetrios, Primate of the Greek Orthodox Church in America,* Ms. Chris Evert, Tennis Champion Mr. Alex Spanos, Owner and Chairman of A.G. Spanos Companies and the San Diego Chargers Mrs. Faye Spanos, Spouse of Alex Spanos
*Designated August 6, 2004
