
    On Thursday, June 15, the President and the First Lady will host a screening of the film "Flamin' Hot," an inspiring story about determination and resilience, directed by Eva Longoria. The screening will take place on the South Lawn and will include the cast of the film, families, and Latino community leaders. Remarks by the President, First Lady, and Ms. Longoria will be open press.
