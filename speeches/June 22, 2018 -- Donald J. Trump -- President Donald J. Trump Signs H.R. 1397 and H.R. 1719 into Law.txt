
    On Friday, June 22, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 1397, which directs the transfer of administrative jurisdiction of certain Federal land along the George Washington Memorial Parkway, in Fairfax County, Virginia; and
H.R. 1719, the "John Muir National Historic Site Expansion Act," which authorizes the National Park Service to acquire by donation approximately 44 acres of land in Martinez, California, for inclusion in the John Muir National Historic Site.
