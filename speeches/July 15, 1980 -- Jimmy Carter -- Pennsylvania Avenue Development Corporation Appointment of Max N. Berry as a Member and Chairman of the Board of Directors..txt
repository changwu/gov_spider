
    The President today announced the intention to appoint as a member and Chairman Max N. Berry, of Washington, D.C., to the Pennsylvania Avenue Development Corporation.
Berry was born December 29, 1935, in Cushing, Okla. He received a B.A. in 1958 and an LL.B. in 1960 from the University of Oklahoma. Berry received an LL.M. from Georgetown Law Center in 1963. He served in the United States Army from 1960 to 1963.
From 1963 to 1967, he served in the Office of the General Counsel at the Department of the Treasury. Since 1967, Berry has specialized in the private practice of administrative law, dealing with various Federal Government agencies as well as with the Members and committees of Congress. Berry has been a senior partner with Berry, Epstein, Sandstrom & Blatchford since 1967.
