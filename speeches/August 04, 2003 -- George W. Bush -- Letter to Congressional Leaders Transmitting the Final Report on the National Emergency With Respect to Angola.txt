
    Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
Consistent with section 401(c) of the National Emergencies Act, 50 U.S.C. 1641(c), and section 204(c) of the International Emergency Economic Powers Act, 50 U.S.C. 1703(c), I am transmitting a final report prepared by my Administration on the national emergency with respect to the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA) that was declared in Executive Order 12865 of September 26, 1993, and terminated in Executive Order 13298 of May 6, 2003.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
