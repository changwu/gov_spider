
    On Friday, September 28, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 6157, the "Department of Defense and Labor, Health and Human Services Appropriations Act, 2019 and Continuing Appropriations Act, 2019," which provides full-year funding through September 30, 2018, for projects and activities of the Federal Government included in the following appropriations bills: Department of Defense Appropriations bill (Division A); and Departments of Labor, Health and Human Services, and Education, and Related Agencies Appropriations bill (Division B). Division C of the enrolled bill provides fiscal year 2019 appropriations through Friday, December 7, 2018, for continuing projects and activities of the remaining agencies of the Federal Government not covered by Divisions A and B, or by Public Law 115-244, the Energy and Water, Legislative Branch, and Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriations Act, 2019.
