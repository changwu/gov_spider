
    Gonzalez says Hillary's experience makes her the best choice for President
Texas DNC member Jaime A. Gonzalez Jr. announced his support for Hillary Clinton today.
"I am supporting Hillary Clinton because I am convinced that she is the best choice for President," said Gonzalez. "She has shown the leadership qualities that we need to win the presidency in the general election and give Democrats a victory in November."
Gonzalez is an attorney and has been a DNC member since 2000. He is a member of the American Association for Justice and the Texas Trial Lawyers Association.
"I am honored and grateful to have Mr. Gonzalez's support," said Clinton. "I look forward to working together to put our country back on the right track."
