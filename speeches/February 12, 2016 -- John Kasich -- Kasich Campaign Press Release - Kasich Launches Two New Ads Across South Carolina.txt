
    Today John Kasich's presidential campaign committee began airing two new ads in TV markets across South Carolina.
"Healing" informs America of John Kasich's motivation for running for the nation's highest office: He believes the Lord put us on this earth to use the gifts that we've been given to bring about a healing.

  

"100 Days" points to John Kasich's record of results and lays out his agenda for the first 100 days of his presidency.
