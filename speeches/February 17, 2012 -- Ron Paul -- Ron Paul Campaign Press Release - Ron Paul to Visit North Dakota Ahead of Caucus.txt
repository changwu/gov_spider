
    
  Campaigns and also attends a North Dakota Policy Council event

BISMARCK, North Dakota — 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul will campaign in North Dakota for two days in the run-up to the Tuesday, March 6th Caucus.
In addition to campaigning for the Republican nomination, the 12-term Congressman from Texas will attend an educational event sponsored by the North Dakota Policy Council.
