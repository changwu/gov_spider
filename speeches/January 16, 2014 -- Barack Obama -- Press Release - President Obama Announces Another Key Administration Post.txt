President Obama announced his intent to nominate the following individual to a key Administration post:
Rick Christman, Nominee for Member, Board of Directors of the Corporation for National and Community Service
Rick Christman has been the CEO of Employment Solutions since 1989. He was a Member of the Board of Directors of the Corporation for National and Community Service from April 2012 to October 2013. Mr. Christman was the Executive Director at the Bluegrass Association for Retarded Children from 1985 to 1989. From 1981 to 1985, Rick Christman was the Director for Mental Retardation Services at the Western Kentucky Regional MH-MR Board. Before that, Mr. Christman was a Rehabilitation Specialist at the J.U. Kevil Center from 1977 to 1981 and a Special Education Teacher in Elroy Wisconsin from 1975 to 1976. Mr. Christman received a B.S. from the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire and an M.S. from the University of Wisconsin-Stout.
