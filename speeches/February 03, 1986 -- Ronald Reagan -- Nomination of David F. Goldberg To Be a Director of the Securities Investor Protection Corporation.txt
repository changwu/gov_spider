
    The President today announced his intention to nominate David F. Goldberg to be a Director of the Securities Investor Protection Corporation. This is a reappointment.
Since 1969 Mr. Goldberg has been chairman and general partner, Goldberg Brothers and Goldberg Securities, in Chicago, IL. He was a general partner with Geldermann and Co., 1958-1969.
Mr. Goldberg attended Northwestern University in Evanston, IL. He is married and has four children. He was born February 26, 1931, in Chicago, IL, and resides in Golf, IL.
