
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report eight new deferrals of budgetary resources, totaling $4.8 billion.
These deferrals affect programs of the Department of State, the Social Security Administration, and International Security Assistance.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, February 3, 1998.
