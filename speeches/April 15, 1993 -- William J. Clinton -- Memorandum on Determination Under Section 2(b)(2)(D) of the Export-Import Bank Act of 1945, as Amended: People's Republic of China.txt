
    Presidential Determination No. 93-19
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to section 2(b)(2)(D) of the Export-Import Bank Act of 1945, as amended (12 U.S.C. 635(b)(2)(D)), I determine that it is in the national interest for the Export-Import Bank of the United States to extend a loan in the amount of $ 78,232,982 to the People's Republic of China in connection with the purchase of U.S. equipment and services for Qidashan Iron Ore Mine and Benefication Plant in Liaoning Province.
You are authorized and directed to report this determination to the Congress and publish it in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, April 15, 1993.
