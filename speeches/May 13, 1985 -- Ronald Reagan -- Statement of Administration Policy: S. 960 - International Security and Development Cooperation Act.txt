
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(Revision)(Senate)(Senator Lugar (R) Indiana)
The Administration supports Senate passage of S. 960, if it is amended to:
— authorize sufficient funding for security assistance programs;
— eliminate the strictures on assistance to the contras:
— allow flexibility on disbursement of assistance to Israel;
— modify the provisions on the Palestine Liberation Organization to eliminate the constitutional concerns regarding the separation of powers, and Presidential authority to conduct foreign policy;
— allow the President to deny funds to foreign nongovernmental or multi-lateral organizations that perform abortions; and
— increase the assistance levels for Turkey and revise the terms for Greece.
