Boston, MA – Today, Governor Mitt Romney announced the Romney for President Washington Statewide Finance Steering Committee. These 28 Republican leaders will play a critical role in growing grassroots support for Governor Romney in the state and ensuring he'll have the resources needed to win not only in Washington, but across the country.
"These Republicans will be instrumental in helping raise the necessary funds for a successful campaign to bring conservative change to the nation's capital," said Governor Romney. "With their help, we will return our government to the Republican principles of lower taxes, restrained spending and smaller government. I look forward to working with them to ensure the strength of America's future."
