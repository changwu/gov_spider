
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Dorothy Livingston Strunk to be Assistant Secretary of Labor for Mine Safety and Health. She would succeed David A. Zegeer.
Since 1979 Mrs. Strunk has served as the senior legislative associate on the Committee on Education and Labor, U.S. House of Representatives. Previously she was the office manager for the Committee on Ways and Means in the U.S. House of Representatives, 1978-1979.
Mrs. Strunk was born January 2, 1948, in Nanticoke, PA. She is married, has two children, and currently resides in Fort Washington, MD.
