
    Office Opening Followed by Debate Watch Party
Kentucky for Hillary today announced the opening of its state headquarters in Louisville. The office will open TODAY, Wednesday, April 16 at 6:30 p.m., and will be followed by a debate watch party to cheer Hillary on during the Democratic Debate tonight at 8 p.m. on ABC.
Host: Kentucky for Hillary staff, volunteers, and supporters
When: TODAY, April 16, at 6:30 p.m. EDT
Where: 1357 Bardstown Road, Louisville, KY
