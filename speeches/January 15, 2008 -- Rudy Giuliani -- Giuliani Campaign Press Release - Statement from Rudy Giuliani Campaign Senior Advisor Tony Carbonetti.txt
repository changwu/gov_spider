
    The Rudy Giuliani Presidential Committee released the following statement tonight from Senior Advisor Tony Carbonetti:
"We congratulate Mitt Romney for winning his home state of Michigan. It's clear after tonight that while the race remains fluid and competitive, our strategy remains on track. Rudy is going to continue to campaign aggressively in Florida and after the energy we've seen on the trail this past week, we're confident that we'll be successful on the 29th."
