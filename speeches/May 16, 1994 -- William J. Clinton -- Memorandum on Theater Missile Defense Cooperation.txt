
    Presidential Determination No. 94–24
Memorandum for the Secretary of Defense
Subject: Theater Missile Defense Cooperation with the Allies
1. Pursuant to the authority vested in me by the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1994, Public Law 103–160, I hereby certify that the Director, Ballistic Missile Defense Organization, has formally submitted to representatives of the member nations of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and Japan, Israel, and South Korea a proposal concerning coordination of the development and implementation of U.S. Theater Missile Defense (TMD) programs with TMD programs of our friends and allies.
2. You are authorized and directed to notify the Congress of this determination and to publish it in the Federal Register.
William J. Clinton
