
    On Friday, June 3, the Vice President and Second Gentleman will travel to Reno, Nevada, where the Vice President will deliver remarks at the United States Conference of Mayors' Annual Meeting. The Vice President's remarks will be pooled press and open to pre-credentialed media. Additional details to follow.
The Vice President and Second Gentleman will then travel to Los Angeles, California.
During the week of June 6, the Vice President and Second Gentleman will remain in Los Angeles and attend the Ninth Summit of the Americas and associated events. This trip will be pooled press and open to pre-credentialed media. Additional details to follow.
