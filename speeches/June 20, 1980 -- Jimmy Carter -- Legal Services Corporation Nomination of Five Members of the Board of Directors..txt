
    The President today announced five persons whom he will nominate for reappointment as members of the Board of Directors of the Legal Services Corporation. They are:
STEVEN L. ENGELBERG, of Chevy Chase, Md., a partner in the Washington, D.C., firm of Grove, Engelberg & Gross, P.C.;
CECILIA D. ESQUER, of Tempe, Ariz., a partner in the firm of Esquer & Silvas;
HILLARY RODHAM, of Little Rock, Ark., an associate in the litigation division of Rose, Nash, Williamson, Carroll, Clay & Giroir;
RICHARD ALLAN TRUDELL, Of Oakland, Calif., executive director of the American Indian Lawyer Training Program, Inc.;
JOSEPHINE WORTHY, of Holyoke, Mass., who is active in local legal services organizations and other community activities.
