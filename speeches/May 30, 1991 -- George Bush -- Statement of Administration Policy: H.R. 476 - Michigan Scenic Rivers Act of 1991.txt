
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(SENT 5/31)(House)(Kildee (D) Michigan and 22 others)
The Administration has no objection to House passage of H.R. 476. However, the Administration will seek amendments in the Senate to (1) authorize certain river restoration techniques consistent with river management plans and (2) require agreements with the State of Michigan before studies of certain additional river segments are initiated.
