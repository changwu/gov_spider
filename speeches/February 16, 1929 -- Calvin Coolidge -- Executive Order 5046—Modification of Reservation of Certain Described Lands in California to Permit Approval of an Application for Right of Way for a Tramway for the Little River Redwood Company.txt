
    The Executive order of October 28, 1925, withdrawing Lot 6 Sec. 6, Lots 1 and 2 Sec. 7, T. 7 N., R. 1 E., H.M., California, under the provisions of the act of Congress approved June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847), as amended, is hereby modified to the extent of authorizing the Secretary of the Interior to approve the application by the Little River Redwood Company, for right of way for a tramway crossing certain lands covered by the said reserve.
CALVIN COOLIDGE
The White House,February 16, 1929.
