
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the Opening Ceremonies of the IX Paralympic Winter Games in Torino, Italy.
The Honorable Margaret Spellings, U.S. Secretary of Education, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Ronald P. Spogli, U.S. Ambassador to Italy
Mr. Kirk Bauer, J.D., Executive Director, Disabled Sports USA
Mr. Chad Colley, Two-time Paralympic Gold Medal Winner (Alpine Skiing)
Ms. Nancy Starnes, Vice President and Chief of Staff, National Organization on Disability
