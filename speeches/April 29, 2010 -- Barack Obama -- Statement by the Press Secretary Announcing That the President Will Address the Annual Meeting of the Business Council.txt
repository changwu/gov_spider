
    On the morning of Tuesday, May 4, 2010, President Barack Obama will address the annual meeting of the Business Council in Washington, DC. The President will discuss his efforts to spur job creation and rebuild our economy for years to come. He will also address the important role the business community plays in the fight to get the American people back to work. Treasury Secretary Tim Geithner will also address the Council on Tuesday, and Office of Management and Budget Director Peter R. Orszag will address the Council on Wednesday.
