
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Jean Lawson Stone to be a member of the National Voluntary Service Advisory Council. This is a new position. Mrs. Stone is former mayor of the village of Scarsdale, N.Y. She now serves as a trustee of Rollins College. She served as a member of the board of trustees for the village and town board in 1978-1981 and as police commissioner in 1980. She has served as chairman of the zoning and planning, public safety, and personnel committees for the village of Scarsdale.
Mrs. Stone graduated from the University of Texas (B.F.A., 1945). She is married, has four children, and resides in Scarsdale, N.Y. She was born February 23, 1924, in Del Rio, Tex.
