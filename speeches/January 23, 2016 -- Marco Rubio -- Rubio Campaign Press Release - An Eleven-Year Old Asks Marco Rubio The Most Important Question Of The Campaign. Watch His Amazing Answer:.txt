
    
January 23, 2016
An 11-year-old girl in New Hampshire asked Marco what he felt was the most pressing issue facing our nation. 
Marco's answer was the one only he can give: 
He and his parents have lived the American Dream, and he wants the next generation — like his questioner and his kids — to have the same chance.

Marco knows that America's best days don't have to be behind us. He's fighting to ensure that our brightest days remain ahead.
