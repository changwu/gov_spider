
    GOP Presidential frontrunner Senator Bob Dole will visit North Dakota on Tuesday, January 16, 1996. Senator Dole will be joined by Governor Ed Schafer throughout the day. They will attend an agricultural briefing at the Melroe Manufacturing Company in Bismarck from 9:20 a.m. to 10:20am CST. They will then travel to Minot, ND where they will tour the Terhorst Manufacturing Company from 11:15 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. CST. Media attending should arrive thirty minutes early for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials.
