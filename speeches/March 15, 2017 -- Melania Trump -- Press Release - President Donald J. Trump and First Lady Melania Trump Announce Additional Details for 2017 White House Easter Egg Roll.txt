
    President Donald J. Trump and First Lady Melania Trump are pleased to announce additional details for the 139th White House Easter Egg Roll taking place Monday, April 17, 2017, on the South Lawn of the White House.
ONLINE TICKET LOTTERY
White House Easter Egg Roll tickets are free to the public and can be requested through an online lottery opening today, Wednesday, March 15, 2017, at 12:00 PM EST and closing on Saturday, March 18, 2017, at 12:00 PM EST. Entering the lottery is free of charge, and winners will be notified via email on Friday, March 31, 2017. Tickets are not for sale or resale. Those interested in attending can enter the lottery here.
COMMEMORATIVE EGGS
This year's official White House Easter Egg Roll commemorative eggs are available to the general public. More information and online purchase will be available soon by visiting the lottery's website.
ABOUT THE EVENT
The White House Easter Egg Roll is a timeless White House tradition, dating back to 1878 and the presidency of President Rutherford B. Hayes. The President and First Lady are honored to continue the traditions of the past, while weaving new traditions into the fabric of our Nation's history.
