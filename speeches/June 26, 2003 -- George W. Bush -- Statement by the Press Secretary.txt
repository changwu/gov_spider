
    On Thursday, June 26, 2003, the President signed into law:
S.J.Res. 8, which expresses the sense of Congress with respect to raising awareness and encouraging prevention of sexual assault in the United State and supporting the goals and ideals of National Sexual Assault Awareness and Prevention Month.
S. 703, which designates the regional headquarters building for the National Park Service under construction in Omaha, Nebraska, as the Carl T. Curtis National Park Service Midwest Regional Headquarters Building.
