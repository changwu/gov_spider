
    The President today announced he will nominate Howard Jenkins, Jr., of Kensington, Md., for reappointment as a member of the National Labor Relations Board for a term expiring August 27, 1983.
Mr. Jenkins has been a member of the NLRB since 1963. Prior to joining the Board, he served in several positions in the Department of Labor between 1956 and 1963. From 1946 to 1956, he was professor of law at Howard University.
Mr. Jenkins was born June 16, 1915, in Denver, Colo. He received an A.B. degree from the University of Denver in 1936 and an LL.B degree from the University of Denver in 1941.
