
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(SENT 11/3/93)(House)(Brooks (D) Texas and 2 others)
The Administration strongly supports H.R. 3355.
H.R. 3355 authorizes appropriations of $3.45 billion over FYs' 1994-1999 to allow States and localities to provide more law enforcement officers and to support programs where police work with community residents to reduce and prevent crime. It represents a vital step in putting more police on the streets. Increasing police presence and expanding community policing (Efforts are an integral part of the Administration's anti-crime and anti-drug efforts.
