
    
  Holds town hall meetings at college campuses in both states to accommodate crowd size

LAKE JACKSON, Texas — 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul will campaign in Illinois and Missouri ahead of the states' voting contests.
Dr. Paul's visits to Illinois and Missouri, respectively, will occur on Wednesday, March 14th and Thursday, March 15th. The Illinois primary will take place on Tuesday, March 20th. The Missouri county caucuses begin today, with heavy activity on Saturday the 17th, and continue through Saturday the 24th.
Open press availabilities will follow both campaign events.
Details of Dr. Paul's Illinois and Missouri events are as follows. All times Central.
