
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint six individuals to serve in his Administration.
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the Board of Trustees of the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts:
Elliott Bryan Broidy of California
Emilio Estefan, Jr. of Florida
Sheldon B. Kamins of Maryland
James V. Kimsey of the District of Columbia
Norman Y. Mineta of California
Stephen A. Wynn of Nevada
