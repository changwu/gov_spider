
    Verona, PA - New polling shows Republican Presidential Candidate Rick Santorum with a solid lead over the establishment and insider choice, former Massachusetts Governor Mitt Romney.
A poll conducted by Wenzel Strategies on behalf of a political action committee affiliated with Citizens United shows Rick Santorum leading Mitt Romney by 20-points.
Republican Presidential Match-Up:
Santorum: 46%
Romney: 26%
Gingrich: 9%
The poll was conducted from March 26 - 27 with 997 respondents, with a margin of error of +/- 3%.
