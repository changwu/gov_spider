
    On Friday, July 28, Vice President Harris will travel to Des Moines, IA to hold a moderated conversation on the Biden-Harris Administration's continued commitment to protecting reproductive freedom across America.
Joined by health care providers, patients, local leaders, and advocates for reproductive freedom, the Vice President will discuss the state's unpopular efforts to enact an extreme abortion ban, lift up stories of those impacted by this health care crisis, outline the Administration's ongoing work to protect access to reproductive health care throughout the country, and reiterate her call for Congress to pass federal legislation that restores the protections of Roe v. Wade.
