
    President Donald J. Trump today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Indonesia to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Joko Widodo, President of the Republic of Indonesia, on October 20, 2019.
The Honorable Elaine L. Chao, Secretary of Transportation, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Joseph R. Donovan Jr., United States Ambassador to the Republic of Indonesia
