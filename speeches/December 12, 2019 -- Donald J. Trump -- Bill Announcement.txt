
    On Thursday, December 12, 2019, the President signed into law:
H.R. 887, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service in Orem, Utah, as the Jerry C. Washburn Post Office Building;
H.R. 1252, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service in Van Nuys, California, as the Marilyn Monroe Post Office;
H.R. 1253, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service in Pacoima, California, as the Ritchie Valens Post Office Building;
H.R. 1526, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service in Tumwater, Washington, as the Eva G. Hewitt Post Office; and
H.R. 1844, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service in Elgin, Illinois, as the Corporal Alex Martinez Memorial Post Office Building.
