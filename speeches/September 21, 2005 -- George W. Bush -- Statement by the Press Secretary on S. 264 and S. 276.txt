
    On September 21, 2005, the President has signed into law:
S. 264, the "Hawaii Water Resources Act of 2005," which authorizes the Department of the Interior to participate in the design, planning, and construction of three separate water reclamation projects in Hawaii.
S. 276, the "Wind Cave National Park Boundary Revision Act of 2005," which authorizes the Secretary of the Interior to acquire land for and revise the boundaries of the Wind Cave National Park in South Dakota.
