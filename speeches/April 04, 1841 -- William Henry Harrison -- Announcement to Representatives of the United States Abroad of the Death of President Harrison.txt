
    DEPARTMENT OF STATE,
Washington, April 4, 1841.
SIR: It has become my most painful duty to announce to you the decease of William Henry Harrison, late President of the United States.
This afflicting event took place this day at the Executive Mansion, in this city, at thirty minutes before I o'clock in the morning.
I am, sir, your obedient servant,
DANL. WEBSTER.
