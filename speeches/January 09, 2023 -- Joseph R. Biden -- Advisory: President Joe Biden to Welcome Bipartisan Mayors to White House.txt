
    On Friday, January 20, the President will welcome bipartisan mayors attending the U.S. Conference of Mayors Winter Meeting to the White House. The President will deliver remarks celebrating the achievements of the past 18 months – including the American Rescue Plan, the Inflation Reduction Act, the CHIPS and Science Act, the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law, and the Bipartisan Safer Communities Act – and focusing on the bipartisan work that needs to be done to implement these and other historic legislative victories at the local level to make a difference in people's lives. This event in the East Room will be open to pre-credentialed media. Additional details to follow.
