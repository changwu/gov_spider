
    Phoenix, AZ - The Rick Santorum for President Campaign is proud to announce that it has received the endorsement of the Arizona Right to Life Political Action Committee (AZRTL PAC).
In a statement released by AZRTL PAC which cited Senator Santorum's work to ban partial birth abortions, pass the Born Alive Infant Protection Act, and support the Congressional Coalition on Adoption, President Michelle Clements said:
"There are few who are as articulate and passionate on life issues as Senator Santorum," said Michelle Clements, president of Arizona Right to Life. "He not only voted the right way, he talks about the dignity and value of every human life whenever he has the opportunity, and he lives out those values in his own personal life. We are grateful for his leadership and proud to endorse him."
