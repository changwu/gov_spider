It's hard to declare anyone a "winner" of a debate where every candidate tries to move our country backward, so we decided to anoint the Republican presidential debaters with some superlatives of our own.


It was close, Dr. Carson, but Governor Bush had you beat!


But it will somehow end with making America great again.


Because Republican candidates for president are the true victims of oppression.


No matter which Republican wins, women lose!
