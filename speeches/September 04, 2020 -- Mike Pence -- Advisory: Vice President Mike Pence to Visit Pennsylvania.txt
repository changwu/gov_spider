
    On Wednesday, September 9th, Vice President Mike Pence will travel to Pennsylvania for a bus tour. Beginning in Murrysville, Pennsylvania, Vice President Pence will visit Cornerstone Ministries Church and participate in a roundtable discussion with pro-life advocates. Following, the Vice President will participate in a fireside chat highlighting President Trump's record as the most Pro-Life president ever. Afterward, the Vice President will visit PennEnergy Resources, LLC in Freedom, Pennsylvania. There, the Vice President will deliver remarks at Workers for Trump, focusing on the Administration's commitment to American energy independence, fracking, and Pennsylvania energy jobs. Later that evening, the Vice President will return to Washington, D.C.
