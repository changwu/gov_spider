
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Turkey to attend the Opening of the Baku-Tbilisi-Ceyhan Pipeline on July 13, 2006.
The Honorable Jeffrey Clay Sell, Deputy Secretary of Energy, will lead the delegation.
Accompanying member of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Ross Wilson, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United States of America to the Republic of Turkey
