
    President Trump recently released the American Dream Plan to reaffirm his commitment to fight for Hispanic prosperity and opportunity for all to achieve the American Dream. The American Dream Plan is based on four core principles: Fueling a thriving economy; promoting education opportunity for all; preserving freedom; and supporting faith, family, and community. The Plan also includes key policy promises the President has delivered for the Hispanic American community. President Trump's Administration will continue to support Hispanic Americans by expanding our economy, offering school choice, defending our constitutional rights, combating socialism, and expanding quality, affordable healthcare options for all.
