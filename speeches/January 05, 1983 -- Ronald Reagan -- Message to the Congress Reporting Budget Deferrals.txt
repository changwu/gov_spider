
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report four deferrals totaling $38,897,952 and a revision to a previous deferral, increasing the amount deferred by $10,000,000.
The deferrals affect programs in the Departments of Agriculture, Commerce, Housing and Urban Development, and Interior.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
January 5, 1983.
