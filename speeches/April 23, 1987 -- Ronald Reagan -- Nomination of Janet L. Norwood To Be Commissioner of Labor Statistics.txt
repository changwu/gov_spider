
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Janet L. Norwood to be Commissioner of Labor Statistics, United States Department of Labor, for a term of 4 years. This is a reappointment.
Mrs. Norwood has served as the Commissioner of Labor Statistics at the Department of Labor (DOL) since 1979. Previously, she was Acting Commissioner of Labor Statistics (DOL), 1978-1979; and Deputy Commissioner of Labor Statistics (DOL), 1973-1978.
Mrs. Norwood graduated from Douglass College, Rutgers University (B.A., 1945) and Tufts University (M.A., 1946; Ph.D., 1949). She was born on December 11, 1923, in Newark, NJ. Mrs. Norwood is married, has two children, and currently resides in Bethesda, MD.
