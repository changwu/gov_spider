WH in-town pool report #2 — Putin, MBS
The White House sends along the following readouts from POTUS' calls today with Russian President Vladimir Putin and Saudi Crown Prince Mohammad bin Salman:
Today, President Donald J. Trump spoke with President Vladimir Putin of Russia. President Trump thanked President Putin for working with the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and other oil producing states to calm global energy markets. President Trump welcomed the commitment by Russia to return oil production to levels consistent with global energy and financial market stability.
Today, President Donald J. Trump spoke with Crown Prince Mohammad Bin Salman Al Saud of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The President thanked the Crown Prince for working with Russia and other oil producing states to calm global energy markets. President Trump welcomed the commitment by Saudi Arabia to return oil production to levels consistent with global energy and financial market stability.
