
    Washington, DC -- Governors for Dole for President will hold a press conference discussing welfare reform on Tuesday, February 6, 1996 at 12:45 p.m. at the J.W. Marriott. Among the prominent Dole supporters participating are Governor Steve Merrill (NH) and Governor David Beasley (SC). Media attending should arrive thirty minutes early and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials.
