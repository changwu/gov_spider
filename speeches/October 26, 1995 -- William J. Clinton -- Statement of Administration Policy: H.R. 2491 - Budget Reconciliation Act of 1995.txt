
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Kasich (R) OH)
If this budget reconciliation legislation — with its extreme spending cuts and huge tax cuts flowing disproportionately to people who do not need them — were sent to the President in its current form, he would veto it The reasons are set forth in the attached letter of October 18 from Alice M. Rivlin, Director of the Office of Management and Budget, to Chairmen Kasich and Solomon. Additional Administration concerns with H.R. 2517 are also attached.
