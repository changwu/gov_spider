
    The President today announced the appointment of five persons as members of the Committee for Purchase from the Blind and Other Severely Handicapped. They are:
EDMUND ALVAREZ, Director of the Office of Operations of the Department of Agriculture;
ROBERT P. GRAHAM, Commissioner of the Federal Supply Service, General Services Administration;
REAR ADM. EUGENE A. GRINSTEAD, Commander, Naval Supply Systems Command and Chief of Supply Corps for the U.S. Navy;
MAJ. GEN. DEWEY K. K. LOWE, Director of Procurement Policy for the Deputy Chief of Staff, Systems and Logistics, U.S. Air Force;
BRIG. GEN. ERNEST A. VULEY, JR., Director of Materiel Management, U.S. Army Materiel Development and Readiness Command.
