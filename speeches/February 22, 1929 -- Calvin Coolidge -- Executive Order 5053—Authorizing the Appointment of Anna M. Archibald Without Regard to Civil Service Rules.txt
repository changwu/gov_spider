
    Mrs. Anna M. Archibald, widow of Captain Robert J. Archibald, U. S. Marine Corps, killed in an airplane crash, November 1, 1928, while a student at the Army Air Corps Tactical School, may be appointed to any position for which she is qualified in the Federal service without regard to the Civil Service Act or Rules.
This order is issued upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Navy. The Civil Service Commission is unable to concur.
CALVIN COOLIDGE
The White House,February 22, 1929.
