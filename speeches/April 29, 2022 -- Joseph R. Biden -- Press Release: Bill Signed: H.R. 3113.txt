
    On Friday, April 29, 2022, the President signed into law:
H.R. 3113, the "Modernizing Access to Our Public Land Act," which digitizes and makes publicly available geographic information system mapping data relating to public access to Federal land and waters for outdoor recreation.
Thank you to Representatives Blake Moore, Fulcher, Neguse, and Schrier and Senators Risch, King, Daines, Heinrich, Crapo, Manchin, Collins, Kelly, and Barrasso for their leadership.
