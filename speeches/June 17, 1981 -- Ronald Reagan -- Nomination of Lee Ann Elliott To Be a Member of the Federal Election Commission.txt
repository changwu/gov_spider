
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Lee Ann Elliott to be a member of the Federal Election Commission for the term expiring April 30, 1987, vice Joan Aikens.
Since 1979 Mrs. Elliott has been vice president, Bishop, Bryant & Associates, Inc., of Washington, D.C. She has also been associate editor, Political Action Report, Tyke Research Associates. In 1970-79 she was associate executive director, American Medical Political Action Committee, and served as assistant director in 1961-70.
Mrs. Elliott is a member of the board of directors of the American Association of Political Consultants, Public Affairs Committee of the Chamber of Commerce of the United States, and board of directors and past president of the Chicago Area Public Affairs Group. She is a recipient of the Award of Excellence in Serving Corporate Public Affairs from the National Association of Manufacturers (1979).
Mrs. Elliot graduated from the University of Illinois (B.A.) and attended Northwestern University. She resides in Skokie, Ill., and was born June 26, 1927.
