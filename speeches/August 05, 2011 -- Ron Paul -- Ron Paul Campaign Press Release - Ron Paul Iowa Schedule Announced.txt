
    Candidate to speak, meet voters in run-up to Ames straw poll
LAKE JACKSON, Texas — The Ron Paul 2012 presidential campaign today announced that Congressman Ron Paul will be making multiple stops in Iowa beginning August 9th and leading up to the Ames Straw Poll on August 13th. Congressman Paul will be joined by his son, the junior Senator from Kentucky, Senator Rand Paul for five of the nine scheduled stops.
Details of Congressman Paul's upcoming appearances, including those featuring Senator Paul, are as follows. All times CDT.
