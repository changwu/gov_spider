
    To the Congress of the United States:
Enclosed is the "Annual Report of the National Advisory Council on Extension and Continuing Education for FY 1977," prepared in accordance with the requirement of Section III(b) of Title III of the Higher Education Act of 1965, covering calendar year 1976, preceding my term of office.
This report addresses the need for continuing education programs and the Council's recommendations for policies, funding and administrative structures toward this end.
The Report of the Council is being carefully studied and evaluated. I am forwarding the Report so that it is available to the Congress for its deliberations.
JIMMY CARTER
The White House,
December 1, 1977.
