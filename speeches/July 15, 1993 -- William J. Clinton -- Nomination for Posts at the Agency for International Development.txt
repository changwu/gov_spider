
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Margaret Carpenter, Assistant Administrator for Asia and Douglas Stafford, Assistant Administrator for Food and Humanitarian Assistance at the Agency for International Development, U.S. International Development Cooperation Agency.
"With their dual experience in the field of foreign affairs and understanding of the good global assistance can do, I am certain both Margaret and Doug will work hard to fulfill AID's mission of providing economic and humanitarian assistance to the developing world," the President said.
