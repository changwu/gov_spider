
    On Friday, August 26, 2022, the President signed into law:
S. 4458, the "Ensuring the Best Schools for Veterans Act of 2022," which improves the process for how VA decides and evaluates programs of education for violations of the 85/15 rule.
Thank you to Senators Tester, Moran, Collins, and Blackburn, and Representatives Takano and Bost, for their leadership.
