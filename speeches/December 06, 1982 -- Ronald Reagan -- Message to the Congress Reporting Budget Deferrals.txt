
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report 5 new deferrals of fiscal year 1983 funds totaling $624,650,000.
The deferrals are for programs in International Security Assistance and the Department of Housing and Urban Development.
The details of each deferral are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
December 6, 1982.
