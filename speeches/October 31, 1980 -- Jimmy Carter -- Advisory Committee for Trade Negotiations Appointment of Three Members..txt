
    The President today announced the appointment of three members of the Advisory Committee for Trade Negotiations for 3-year terms. They are:
LEE L. MORGAN, of Peoria, Ill., chairman and chief executive officer of Caterpillar Tractor Co.;
RUDOLPH A. OSWALD, of Potomac, Md., director of the AFL-CIO's Department of Research, former assistant director of the Department of Education of the AFL-CIO, and former director of research with the Service Employees International Union;
GLENN E. WATTS, of Chevy Chase, Md., president of the Communications Workers of America and a member of the executive council of the AFL-CIO.
