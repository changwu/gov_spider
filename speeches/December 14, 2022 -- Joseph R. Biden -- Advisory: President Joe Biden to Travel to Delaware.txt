
    On Thursday, December 15, the President will travel to Wilmington, Delaware. On Friday, December 16, the President will participate in a town hall at the Major Joseph R. "Beau" Biden III National Guard/Reserve Center in New Castle, Delaware. The President will speak with veterans and veteran survivors to discuss the historic expansion of benefits and services resulting from the PACT Act. The Delaware event is just one of more than 90 events occurring across the nation to encourage veterans to sign up for health care, get screened for toxic exposure, and submit a claim if they are experiencing a toxic exposure related condition. Additional details to follow.
