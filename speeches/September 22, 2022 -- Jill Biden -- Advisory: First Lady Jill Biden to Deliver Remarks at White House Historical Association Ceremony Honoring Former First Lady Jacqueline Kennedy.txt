
    On Friday, September 23rd at 2:30 PM ET, First Lady Jill Biden will deliver remarks at a White House Historical Association dedication ceremony honoring former First Lady Jacqueline Kennedy at the Decatur House. The ceremony will dedicate a newly installed garden featuring a new sculpture to celebrate Mrs. Kennedy's historic legacy in restoring the White House and preserving Lafayette Square. This event will be open press. For interested media, please RSVP HERE by today, Thursday 9/22, at 6:00 PM ET.
