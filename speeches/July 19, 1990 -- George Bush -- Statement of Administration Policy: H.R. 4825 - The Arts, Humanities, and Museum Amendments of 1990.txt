
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House Rules)(Williams (D) MT and Bilbray (D) NV)
The Administration supports reauthorization of the National Endowment for the Arts, the National Endowment for the Humanities, and the Institute of Museum Services. For the provisions affecting the Endowment for the Humanities, the Institute of Museum Services and other provisions except the National Endowment for the Arts, the Administration supports enactment of H.R. 4825 as introduced.
For the National Endowment for the Arts, the Administration recognizes that there will be substantive amendments proposed, and will comment on those amendments at the appropriate time.
