
    Today Governor John Kasich's presidential campaign filed a full slate of delegates and alternates at the office of Ohio Secretary of State Jon Husted to appear on the ballot in Ohio.
Said Governor Kasich, "No Republican president has ever won the White House without winning Ohio, and I know Ohio better than anyone else in the race. Many people on this list are longtime friends and colleagues. I'm grateful for their constant encouragement and willingness to share their time and expertise. Support in Ohio is rock solid."
