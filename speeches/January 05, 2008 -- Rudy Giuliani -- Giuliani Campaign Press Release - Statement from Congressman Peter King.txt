
    The Rudy Giuliani Campaign released the following statement from Congressman Peter King following the ABC/WMUR debate:
"It is no surprise Rudy Giuliani remained focused on how we can solve the big issues facing our country. Rudy proved once again he is the strong, tested leader who is ready to tackle difficult changes. You can count on Rudy's steadfast, principled leadership to defeat the Islamic terrorists, cut taxes and restrain runaway spending and end illegal immigration."
