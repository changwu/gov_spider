
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Santiago, Chile, to attend the Bicentennial of the Republic of Chile on September 18, 2010.
The Honorable Arturo Valenzuela, Assistant Secretary of State for Western Hemisphere Affairs, will be the head of the delegation.
Member of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Alejandro D. Wolff, U.S. Ambassador-designate to Chile
