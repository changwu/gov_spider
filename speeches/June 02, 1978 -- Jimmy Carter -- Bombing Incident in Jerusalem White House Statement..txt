
    The President condemns the tragic event in Jerusalem where a cowardly and senseless bombing took the lives of a number of innocent civilians, including an American, and wounded many more.
The killing and maiming of innocent people advances no cause and serves no meaningful purpose.
Every such act of calculated cruelty places new obstacles in the way of those who seek peace, while strengthening the hand of those who feed on conflict. The United States denounces this latest example of terrorism and extends its sympathy to the victims and their families.
