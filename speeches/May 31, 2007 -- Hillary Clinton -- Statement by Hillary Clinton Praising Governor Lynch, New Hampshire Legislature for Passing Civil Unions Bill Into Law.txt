
    MANCHESTER, NH -- Senator Hillary Clinton made the following statement today in response to the news that Governor John Lynch signed the New Hampshire Civil Unions legislation into law.
"The state of New Hampshire has a lot to be proud of today. Governor John Lynch and the Granite State legislature have shown commendable leadership in their efforts to end discrimination against gays and lesbians," said Senator Clinton. "I applaud New Hampshire for moving forward and continuing to lead the country in protecting the rights and freedoms of all its citizens."
