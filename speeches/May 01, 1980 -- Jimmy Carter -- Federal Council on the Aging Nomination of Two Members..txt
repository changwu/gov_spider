
    The President today announced that he will nominate two persons as members of the Federal Council on the Aging. They are:
REV. MSGR. CHARLES J. FAHEY, director of the All University Gerontological Center at Fordham University, currently a member of this Council, and former chairman of the Commission on Aging for the National Conference of Catholic Charities;
SHIMEJI KANAZAWA, director of the Western Gerontological Society in Honolulu, Hawaii.
The President also announced that on confirmation Fahey would be designated Chairman of this Council.
