
    Rudy Giuliani Presidential Campaign Manager Mike DuHaime made the following statement today on the signing of legislation moving the New Jersey state primary to February 5th:
"We are confident Rudy Giuliani is the Republican candidate who can win New Jersey. New Jersey voters have seen first-hand Mayor Giuliani's proven leadership transforming our neighbor across the river into a place where businesses grow, families feel safe and the economy is revitalized."
