
    The President spoke by phone today with President Enrique Pena Nieto of Mexico regarding bilateral and broader economic issues. The two leaders agreed on the importance of further deepening the United States-Mexico economic relationship to ensure both countries continue as leaders in the global economy. More specifically, they discussed the status of the Trans-Pacific Partnership negotiations and the importance of bringing the negotiations to a swift conclusion. President Obama and President Pena Nieto ended the call by reaffirming the importance of all aspects of the bilateral relationship.
