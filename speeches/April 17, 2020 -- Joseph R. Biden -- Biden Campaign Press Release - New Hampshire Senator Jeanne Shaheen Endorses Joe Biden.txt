
    Today, New Hampshire Senator Jeanne Shaheen released the following statement endorsing Joe Biden for President
"My friend Joe Biden has dedicated his life to making a difference for American families. From VAWA to the ACA, he's shown the values and persistence needed to get the job done. This is the most consequential election of my lifetime. I said from the beginning I would support our nominee and I am excited to endorse Joe. Standing together, we can build a stronger economy and brighter future for all Granite Staters."
Senator Shaheen joins a broad and diverse list of Granite State leaders who have endorsed Joe Biden, including Senator Maggie Hassan; Congresswoman Annie Kuster; former Governor John Lynch; former Congresswoman Carol Shea Porter; Senate President Donna Soucy; State Senators Lou D'Allesandro, Melanie Levesque, and Martha Fuller Clark; New Hampshire Speaker of the House Steve Shurtleff; and many more.
Nationally, Biden for President has previously announced more than 2,500 endorsements from national, state, and local leaders, including current and former U.S. senators and representatives, governors, state elected officials, community leaders, and national security professionals.
