Boston, MA – Today, Governor Mitt Romney announced the Romney for President North Dakota Statewide Steering Committee. These Republican leaders will help build Governor Romney's strong grassroots support in North Dakota and communicate to voters about his plan to build a stronger America through sound conservative principles.
"This team of hard-working Republicans will play an important role in continuing to expand our grassroots campaign organization in North Dakota," said Governor Romney. "They will help me bring conservative change to Washington and represent the platform of our conservative coalition – a strong military, a strong economy and strong families."
Romney For President North Dakota Statewide Steering Committee:
- State Representative George Keiser – Representative for House District 47, Bismarck
  - Julie Fedorchak – President of Liffrig Communication and former Communications Director to Governor Schafer, Bismarck
  - Robert Harms – Owner of the Harms Group and former Legal Counsel to Governors Hoeven and Schafer, Bismarck
  - Robert Lindberg – Partner at Advenio Partners LLP and former Chair of the North Dakota College Republicans, Fargo
  - Harold Newman – President and CEO of Newman Traffic Signs, Jamestown
  - Lisa Carlson – Banker, Bismarck
  - Michael Little – President of University of North Dakota College Republicans, Grand Forks
