
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the 10th Anniversary of the Normalization of U.S.-Vietnam Relations in Hanoi, Vietnam on July 12, 2005.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Gordon H. Mansfield, Deputy Secretary of Veterans Affairs, will lead the delegation.
The Honorable Michael W. Marine, Ambassador of the United States to Vietnam.
