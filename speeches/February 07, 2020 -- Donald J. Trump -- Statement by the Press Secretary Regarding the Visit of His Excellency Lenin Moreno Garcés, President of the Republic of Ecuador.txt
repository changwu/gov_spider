
    President Donald J. Trump and First Lady Melania Trump will welcome President Lenin Moreno Garcés and First Lady Rocio González de Moreno of the Republic of Ecuador to the White House on February 12, 2020. This visit is an opportunity to expand the new and positive bilateral relationship that the United States has built with Ecuador. We look forward to discussing ways to strengthen our ties with Ecuador to foster democracy and prosperity in the Western Hemisphere.
