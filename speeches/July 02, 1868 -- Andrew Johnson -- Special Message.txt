
    To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State of the 2d instant, together with accompanying papers.*
ANDREW JOHNSON
*Petitions of merchants and shipowners of New York and Boston relative to the detention, at the request of the House of Representatives, of the ironclad monitors Onteto and Catawba , purchased from the United States by Swift & Co., and supposed to be intended for the Government of Peru, then at war with a power friendly to the United States.
