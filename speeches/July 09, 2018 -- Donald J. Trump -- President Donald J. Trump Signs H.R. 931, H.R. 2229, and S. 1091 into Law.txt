
    The President signed into law:
H.R. 931, the "Firefighter Cancer Registry Act of 2018," which establishes and maintains a voluntary registry of firefighters to collect data on cancer incidence;
H.R. 2229, the "All Circuit Review Act," which provides permanent authority for judicial review of certain Merit Systems Protection Board decisions related to whistleblowers; and
S. 1091, the "Supporting Grandparents Raising Grandchildren Act," which creates an Advisory Council to support grandparents raising grandchildren.
