
    On Wednesday, May 18th, at 11:00 AM ET, First Lady Jill Biden will join U.S. Surgeon General Dr. Vivek Murthy, Ambassador Susan E. Rice, Mental Health Advocate Selena Gomez, and a group of young leaders for a White House Conversation on Youth Mental Health.
This conversation is in connection with the Mental Health Youth Action Forum led by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services and MTV Entertainment (MTVE) in support of the Biden-Harris Administration's Unity Agenda's emphasis on tackling the nation's mental health crisis. This event will be open to pre-credentialed media. For interested pre-credentialed media, please RSVP HERE by Tuesday 5/17 at 5:00 PM ET.
