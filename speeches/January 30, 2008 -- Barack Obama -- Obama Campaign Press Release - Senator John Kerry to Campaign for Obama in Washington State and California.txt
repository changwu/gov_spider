
    Kerry to Attend Events in Seattle, Tacoma, San Francisco, San Jose and Sacramento
Chicago, IL - On Friday, February 1st and Saturday, February 2nd, Senator John Kerry (D-Mass.) will host "Change We Can Believe In" events in Washington and California. Senator Kerry will make stops in Seattle and Tacoma on Friday and San Francisco, San Jose and Sacramento on Saturday.
Kerry will discuss with Washington and California voters why Barack Obama is the only candidate who can bring change we can believe in.
