
    The President today announced that he will nominate Anne Cox Chambers, of Atlanta, Ga., to be Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United States to Belgium. Ms. Chambers is chairman of the Atlanta Newspapers.
She was born December 1, 1919, in Dayton, Ohio. She attended Finch College.
Chambers is a director of Cox Broadcasting Corp., Cox Enterprises, Inc., the Fulton National Bank, the Atlanta Humane Society, Atlanta Landmarks, and the Institutional Development Corp.
She is on the executive committee of Central Atlanta Progress and a founder and trustee and the first president of the Forward Arts Foundation. She is a founding trustee of the Southern Center for International Studies and has served on Governor Busbee's Commission of the Georgia White House Conference on Handicapped Individuals.
