
    Treasury Secretary John Snow will read the holiday storybook "Olive,The Other Reindeer", by Vivian Walsh and J. Otto Seibold, on the White House web site (www.whitehouse.gov) tonight at 8 p.m. (Eastern Time).
Laura Bush, Former First Lady Barbara Bush, White House Chief of Staff Andy Card, Treasury Secretary John Snow, Commerce Secretary Don Evans, Education Secretary Rod Paige, Senior Advisor Karl Rove, OMB Director Josh Bolten and SBA Administrator Hector Barreto are all participants in the nightly bedtime story feature on the web site.
The bedtime story videos are just part of the White House holiday section. Other features which can be found at www.whitehouse.gov/holiday include the new Barney Cam video, "Barney Cam II: Barney Reloaded," the 2003 White House holiday book, 360 degree views of the decorated state rooms, historic photos of holidays at the White House and Presidential holiday cards dating back to the Roosevelt administration.
