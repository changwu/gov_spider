
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the State of Israel to attend events commemorating the 10th anniversary of the assassination of Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin on November 14, 2005.
The Honorable James A. Baker III, former Secretary of State, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Condoleezza Rice, Secretary of State The Honorable Richard A. Jones, U. S. Ambassador to Israel The Honorable C. David Welch, Assistant Secretary of State for Near Eastern Affairs The Honorable Elliott Abrams, Deputy Assistant to the President and Deputy National Security Advisor for Global Democracy Strategy
