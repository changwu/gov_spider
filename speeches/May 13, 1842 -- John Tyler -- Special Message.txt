
    To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I transmit herewith a report* from the Postmaster-General, made in pursuance of the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 21st of March last, together with the accompanying documents.
JOHN TYLER.
*Transmitting lists of postmasters and others appointed by the President and Post-Office Department from April 4, 1841 to March 21, 1842.
