
    The President today announced that he will nominate Bob Clement, of Knoxville, Tenn., to be a member of the Board of Directors of the Tennessee Valley Authority for the remainder of the term expiring May 18, 1981. He would replace William Jenkins, who has resigned.
Clement was born September 23, 1943, in Nashville, Tenn. He received a B.S. from the University of Tennessee at Knoxville in 1967 and an M.B.A. from Memphis State University in 1968.
Clement served a 6-year term on the Tennessee Public Service Commission, including 2 years as chairman (from 1976 to 1978). He was previously employed with the Nashville brokerage insurance firm of Blair, Follin, Alien and Walker, and with the Bureau of Business Research in Memphis. He is currently with Bob Clement and Associates in Nashville. Clement has served as chairman of the National Association of Regulatory Commissioners' Presidential Advisory Committee and as vice president of the Southeast Association of Regulatory Commissioners.
