
    Republican presidential candidate Rick Santorum (R-PA) issued the following statement following Governor Bobby Jindal's decision to suspend his presidential campaign.
Rick Santorum said:
Governor Jindal is a committed public servant and strong conservative leader. He brought a tremendous energy and passion for returning America to its rightful place in the World. This is not the last the country has heard from Bobby Jindal and I wish him and his family the best in their next pursuits.
