
    Memorandum for the Secretary of Transportation
Subject: Resumption of Foreign Air Cargo Service to Lebanon
By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 1114(a) of the Federal Aviation Act of 1958, as amended ("the Act") (49 U.S.C. 1514), I hereby determine that the prohibition of all transportation services to Lebanon by Presidential Determination 85 - 14 of July 1, 1985, is hereby amended to permit the outward carriage of cargo to Lebanon by foreign carriers. All other prohibitions set forth in Presidential Determination 85 - 14, including the prohibition on U.S. air carriers flying into Lebanon, remain in effect.
You are directed to bring this determination immediately to the attention of all air carriers within the meaning of section 101(3) of the Act (49 U.S.C. 1301(3)).
You are further directed to publish this determination in the Federal Register.
George Bush
[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 10:38 a.m., August 20, 1992]
