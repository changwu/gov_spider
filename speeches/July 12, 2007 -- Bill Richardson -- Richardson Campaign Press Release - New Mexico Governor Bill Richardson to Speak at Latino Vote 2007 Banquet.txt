
    SANTA FE, NM -- Democratic Presidential candidate Governor Bill Richardson will address the Latino Vote 2007 Banquet tomorrow night in Los Angeles.
"As a public servant and a Latino, I know how important it is for all members of our community to exercise their fundamental right to vote, and we must ensure that every vote counts," said Governor Richardson. "In 2008 Latino voters will play a critical role in choosing the next President of the United States- the stakes are incredibly high."
WHEN: Friday, July 13th, 9:00 PM
WHAT: Speech to the Latino Vote 2007 Banquet
WHERE: Center at the Cathedral Plaza, 555 West Temple Street, Los Angeles, CA
