
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report revisions to three previously transmitted deferrals increasing the amount deferred by $33.8 million. These revisions to existing deferrals involve programs in the Departments of Transportation and the Treasury and the International Communication Agency.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
JIMMY CARTER
The White House,
February 14, 1979.
