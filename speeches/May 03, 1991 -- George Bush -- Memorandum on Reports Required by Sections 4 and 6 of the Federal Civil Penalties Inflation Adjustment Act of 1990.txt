
    Memorandum for the Director of the Office of Management and Budget
By the authority vested in me by the Constitution and laws of the United States of America, including sections 4 and 6 of the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act of 1990 (Public Law 101-410), and section 301 of title 3 of the United States Code, I hereby delegate to you the responsibility for submitting reports on civil monetary penalties to the Committee on Governmental Affairs of the Senate and the Committee on Government Operations of the House of Representatives and to the Congress as required by sections 4 and 6 of that Act.
You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the Federal Register.
GEORGE BUSH
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, May 3, 1991.
