
    Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to title 3 U.S.C. section 301, I hereby designate and empower the Secretary of State to perform, without the approval, ratification, or other approval of the President, the functions of the President set forth in Title VIII of the Foreign Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 1990 and 1991; Public Law 101-246.
This memorandum shall be published in the Federal Register.
GEORGE BUSH
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, March 14, 1990.
