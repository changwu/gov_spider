
    LAS VEGAS, NV -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Melinda Colucci (USN, Retired); Sean Fellows (former USAF Captain); Captain Steve Long (USAF, Retired; POW, Vietnam); and Joe Brown (former USMC Corporal) will participate in the announcement of Nevada Veterans for John McCain on Tuesday, July 22nd. This press event occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across Nevada and the country.
Tuesday, July 22, 2008
LAS VEGAS, NEVADA
WHO: Nevada Veterans for McCain
Melinda Colucci - Chief, USN (Ret.)
Sean Fellows - Former USAF Captain
Steve Long - Captain, USAF (Ret.); POW, Vietnam
Joe Brown - Former USMC Corporal
WHAT: Remarks at The Leatherneck Club
WHEN: Tuesday, July 22, 2008 at 11:00 a.m. PDT
