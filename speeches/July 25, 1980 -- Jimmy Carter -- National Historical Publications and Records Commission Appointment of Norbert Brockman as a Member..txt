
    The President today announced the appointment of Norbert Brockman, of Dayton, Ohio, as a member of the National Historical Publications and Records Commission.
Brockman, 46, has been a member of the Society of Mary (Marianists) since 1952 and was ordained a priest in 1973. He is director of the Marianist Training Network, a training group that provides educational and renewal services and consulting to religious and non-profit organizations.
Brockman directs an annual training program for religious archivists. In 1979 he organized the first national meeting of Catholic church archivists, and he has brought this group into affiliation with the Society of American Archivists.
