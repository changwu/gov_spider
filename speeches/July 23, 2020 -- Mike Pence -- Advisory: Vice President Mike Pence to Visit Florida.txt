
    On Monday, July 27th, Vice President Pence will travel to Miami, Florida to mark the beginning of Phase III trials for a Coronavirus vaccine. At the University of Miami, the Vice President will participate in a roundtable with university leadership and researchers on the incredible progress of a Coronavirus vaccine. Following, the Vice President will participate in a press briefing. Later that evening, the Vice President will return to Washington, D.C.
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
