
    From Monday, June 12th to Wednesday, June 14th, the First Lady will travel to New York City, New York and San Francisco and Los Angeles, California.
On Monday, June 12th, in the evening, the First Lady will deliver remarks at a political event for the Biden Victory Fund in New York City, NY. This event will be covered by an editorial pool only.
On Tuesday, June 13th, in the early afternoon, the First Lady will deliver remarks at a political event for the Biden Victory Fund in San Francisco, CA. This event will be covered by an editorial pool only.
Later in the afternoon, the First Lady will deliver remarks at a political event for the Biden Victory Fund in San Francisco, CA. This event will be covered by an editorial pool only.
In the evening, the First Lady will deliver remarks at the GIFFORDS Law Center's 30th Anniversary Celebration in San Francisco, CA to recognize three decades of lifesaving progress to break cycles of gun violence. This event will be open press and managed by GIFFORDS. For interested media, RSVP HERE through GIFFORDS.
On Wednesday, June 14th, in the afternoon, the First Lady will deliver remarks at a political event for the Biden Victory Fund in Los Angeles, CA. This event will be covered by an editorial pool only.
