
    Presidential Determination No. 88-4
Memorandum for the Honorable George P. Shultz, the Secretary of State
Pursuant to Section 620 E(e) of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended, 22 U.S.C. 2375(e), I hereby certify that Pakistan does not possess a nuclear explosive device and that the proposed United States assistance program will reduce significantly the risk that Pakistan will possess a nuclear explosive device.
You or your delegatee are authorized and directed to publish this determination and certification in the Federal Register.
RONALD REAGAN
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, December 17, 1987.
