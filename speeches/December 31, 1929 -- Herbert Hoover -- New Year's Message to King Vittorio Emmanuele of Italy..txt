
    [Released December 31, 1929. Dated December 30, 1929]
I THANK Your Majesty for your cordial new year greetings. May the coming years bring to Your Majesty health and happiness, and to the great nation over which you preside, continued peace and prosperity.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Majesty Vittorio Emmanuele III, King of Italy, Rome]
