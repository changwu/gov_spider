
    President Donald J. Trump will welcome Taoiseach Leo Varadkar to the White House on March 12, 2020, in celebration of St. Patrick's Day. The President and the Taoiseach will discuss how to strengthen relations between the United States and Ireland, including the robust economic and cultural ties and joint commitment to maintain the gains of the Good Friday Agreement. The President also looks forward to hosting the Taoiseach at the annual Shamrock Bowl presentation.
