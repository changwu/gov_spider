
    
  Visits The Last Frontier for town hall meetings in Fairbanks and Anchorage

ANCHORAGE, Alaska — 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul will visit Alaska in advance of the state's caucus on March 6th — Super Tuesday — making stops in Fairbanks and Anchorage.
Both events are town hall meetings and both will be followed by an open media availability.
