In-Town Pool Report #12 - Rosh Hashanah call
Pool listened to President Biden speak to rabbis by phone to honor the Jewish High Holy Days. Attendees include faith leaders, rabbis, and members of the Jewish community, according to the White House press office.
He was introduced by Rabbi Erica Asch, president of the Central Conference of American Rabbis. He began speaking at 6:33 p.m. and finished at about 6:42 p.m. Recording can be found here:  https://drive.google.com/file/d/11pUvfPUsMhqKYVfuADCsoCauPjqjSX89/view?usp=sharing.
Highlights of speech:
Message of the High Holy Days is universal.
"It's never too late to repent, to change, to begin anew."
Important to show the world that we celebrate and protect Jewish AmericansTalked about federal funding for synagogue security, U.S. National Strategy to Counter Antisemitism, appointment of Ambassador Deborah Lipstadt, visiting Yad Vashem in Israel, hosting Israeli President Herzog, and nominating Jack Lew to be ambassador to Israel.
