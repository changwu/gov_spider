
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint ten individuals and designate one individual to serve in his Administration:
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the National Veterans Business Development Corporation, for a six-year term beginning September 22, 2006:

  Donald G. Christensen, of Utah
Jeffrey W. Gault, of Virginia
Samuel Metters, of Texas

The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the National Cancer Advisory Board:

  Anthony Atala, of North Carolina (Science / Environmental Carcinogens), for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 9, 2012
Bruce Allan Chabner, of Massachusetts (Science), for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 9, 2012
Donald S. Coffey, of Maryland (Science), for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 9, 2012
Lloyd K. Everson, of Texas (Public), for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 9, 2010
Judah Folkman, of Massachusetts (Science), for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 9, 2012
Robert A. Ingram, of North Carolina (Public), for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 9, 2012
Karen Dow Meneses, of Florida (Science), for the remainder of a six-year term expiring March 9, 2012
The President intends to designate Carolyn D. Runowicz, of Connecticut, to be Chairman of the National Cancer Advisory Board, for a two-year term.
