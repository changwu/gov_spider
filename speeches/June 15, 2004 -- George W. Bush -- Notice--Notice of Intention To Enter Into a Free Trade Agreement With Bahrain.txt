
    Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of the Trade Act of 2002, I have notified the Congress of my intention to enter into a free trade agreement with the Kingdom of Bahrain.
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of that Act, this notice shall be published in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush
The White House,
June 15, 2004.
[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 8:45 a.m., June 17, 2004]
