
    The following list includes the President's public schedule and other items of general interest announced by the Office of the Press Secretary and not included elsewhere in this issue.
November 5
In the morning, the President traveled from Los Angeles, CA, to Anaheim, CA. He then traveled to San Francisco, CA, in the afternoon.
November 6
In the late morning, the President traveled to Seattle, WA. He traveled to Minneapolis, MN, in the afternoon.
November 7
In the morning, the President and Hillary Clinton traveled to Flint, MI. They traveled to Wilmington, DE, in the afternoon and returned to Washington, DC, in the evening.
The President announced his intention to appoint Richard St. Germaine as a member of the National Advisory Council on Indian Education.
The President announced his intention to nominate Johnnie Carson to be U.S. Ambassador to Zimbabwe.
