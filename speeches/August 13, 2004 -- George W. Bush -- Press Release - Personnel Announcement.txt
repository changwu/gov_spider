
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the inauguration of His Excellency Leonel Fernandez, President of the Dominican Republic, on August 16, 2004.
The Honorable Ann M. Veneman, Secretary of Agriculture, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Hans H. Hertell, United States Ambassador to the Dominican Republic
Ms. Anna Cablik, President of Anatek, Inc.
Mr. Sergio Pino, President of Century Partners Group
Mr. Raul R. Romero, President and CEO of S&B Infrastructure, Ltd.
