
    During this year's National Governors Association Winter Meeting, First Lady Jill Biden will host a spousal lunch and service project with Governors' spouses from across the country.
Friday, February 10, 2023
At 1:00 PM, the First Lady will welcome Governors' spouses from across the country to the White House for a luncheon that will include a performance by Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater. Dr. Biden's remarks and the dance performance will be covered by a pool spray. For media interested in joining the pool, RSVP HERE by Thursday, February 9, at 5:00 PM.
Saturday, February 11, 2023
At 1:00 PM, as part of the First Lady's Joining Forces initiative, Dr. Biden will host a service project with Governors' spouses and United Through Reading at the historic Decatur House in Washington, D.C. Dr. Biden and the group will assemble reading kits for children of National Guard personnel. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Thursday, February 9, at 5:00 PM.
**All times are local.**
