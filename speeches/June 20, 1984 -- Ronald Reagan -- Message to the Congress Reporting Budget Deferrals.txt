
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report three new deferrals of budget authority totaling $1,850,000 and five revised deferrals of budget authority which now total $98,533,000. The deferrals affect the Department of Energy.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
June 20, 1984.
