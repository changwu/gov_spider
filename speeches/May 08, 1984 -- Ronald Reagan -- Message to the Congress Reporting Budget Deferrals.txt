
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one new deferral of budget authority totaling $101,999,550 and one revision to a previously reported deferral increasing the amount deferred by $778,000. The total for the previously deferred deferral is now $55,850,019.
The deferrals affect Funds Appropriated to the President and the Department of Agriculture.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
May 8, 1984.
