
    On Thursday, September 22nd, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will attend and deliver remarks at the Equity in Focus Summit in Washington, D.C., on the Biden-Harris Administration's actions to promote equity in the workplace. The summit will be co-hosted by the U.S. Department of Labor and the Cornell University School of Industrial and Labor Relations. The summit will focus on the importance of prioritizing gender and racial equity in policy making and workforce development.
