
    The President today announced the appointment of Milton S. Kronheim, of the District of Columbia, to be a member of the President's Council on Physical Fitness and Sports.
Kronheim was born October 2, 1888, in Washington, D.C. He is chairman of the board of the Milton S. Kronheim & Co., Inc., of Washington and the Kronheim Co., Inc., of Baltimore. Mr. Kronheim has been very active for many years in civic and community affairs and is a sports enthusiast.
