
    The President today announced the appointment of Lamar E. Carroon, of Jackson, Miss., as Federal Representative to the Sabine River Compact Administration.
Carroon, 55, has been with the U.S. Geological Survey since 1950 and has served in a number of assignments in different areas of the country. Since 1968 he has been chief of the Mississippi District of the Water Resources Division of USGS.
