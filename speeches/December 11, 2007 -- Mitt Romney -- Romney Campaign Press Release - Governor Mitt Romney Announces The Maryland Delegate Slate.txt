Boston, MA – The Romney campaign has filed a complete slate of 24 National Convention Delegate candidates across the state. These candidates will represent Governor Romney at the primary election on February 12, 2008.
"Maryland voters understand we must bring conservative change to Washington," said Governor Romney. "Working with our leadership team and delegate slate, I will continue to discuss my vision for building a stronger America with a strong military, strong economy, and strong families."
"Governor Romney has the exceptional energy and leadership we will need to confront a new generation of challenges," said State Senator Alex Mooney, a Romney for President candidate for National Convention Delegate in the Sixth Congressional District. "Maryland Republicans believe in his plans to reform health care, lower taxes and secure our borders. The Maryland delegate candidates are proud to support Governor Romney who has the values and vision to lead our nation."
