
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(SENT 5/20/94)(House)(Hamilton (D) IN)
The Administration supports H.R. 4429.
Pay-As-You-Go Scoring
H.R. 4429 would increase receipts and is, therefore, subject to the pay-as-you-go requirement of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1990. OMB's preliminary scoring estimate of this bill is $48.6 million for FYs 1994-1998.
