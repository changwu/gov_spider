
    President and Mrs. Bush will host King Abdullah and Queen Rania of Jordan at Camp David on September 18 and 19, 2003. Jordan is a close friend of the United States, and a partner in the fight against terror and the pursuit of Middle East peace. The President looks forward to discussing with King Abdullah progress in Iraq and other Middle East-related issues, as well as ways to strengthen the U.S.-Jordanian bilateral relationship.
