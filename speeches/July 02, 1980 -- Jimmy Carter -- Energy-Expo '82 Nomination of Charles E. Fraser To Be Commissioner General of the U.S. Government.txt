
    The President today announced that he will nominate Charles E. Fraser, of Hilton Head Island, S.C., to be Commissioner General of the United States Government for Energy-Expo 1982.
Fraser, 51, is chairman and founder of Sea Pines Co. and chairman of Hilton Head Management Services. He is a trustee of the Southern Center for International Studies and has previously served as chairman of the board of trustees of the National Recreation and Park Association and as a member of the South Carolina Parks, Recreation, and Tourism Commission.
