
    National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met with Finance Minister Lawrence Wong of Singapore today at the White House. Mr. Sullivan and Mr. Wong reflected on the strength of the U.S.-Singapore Strategic Partnership and reviewed opportunities to build on the momentum generated by Prime Minister Lee's recent visit to the United States, including his discussions with President Biden at the White House. Mr. Sullivan and Mr. Wong discussed regional and global issues, including Russia's war against Ukraine, COVID-19 and health security, the crisis in Burma, and shared efforts to develop an Indo-Pacific Economic Framework that advances inclusive and sustainable prosperity.
