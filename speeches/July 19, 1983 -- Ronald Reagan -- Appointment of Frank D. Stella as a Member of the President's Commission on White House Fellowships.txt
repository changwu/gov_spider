
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Frank D. Stella to be a member of the President's Commission on White House Fellowships. This is an initial appointment.
Mr. Stella is president and founder of the F. D. Stella Products Go. in Detroit, Mich. He was a member of the board of trustees (1971-1980) and the executive committee (1971-1977) of the University of Detroit. He served as chairman of the Income Tax Board of Review, city of Detroit, for over 9 years.
He is married, has seven children, and resides in Detroit, Mich. He was born January 21, 1919, in Jessup, Pa.
