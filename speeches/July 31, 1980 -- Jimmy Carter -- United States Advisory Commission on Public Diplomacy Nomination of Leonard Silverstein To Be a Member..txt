
    The President today announced that he will nominate Leonard Silverstein, of Bethesda, Md, for reappointment as a member of the United States Advisory Commission on Public Diplomacy.
Silverstein is senior partner in the Washington law firm of Silverstein and Mullens. He is executive director of the Commission on Private Philanthropy and Public Needs and serves as chairman of the advisory board and chief editor for "Tax Management."
