
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to nominate Michelle Guillermin to be Chief Financial Officer of the Corporation for National and Community Service. She is the President and Co-Founder of the Guillerman Group, a consulting firm specializing in project, process, customer and brand management. From 1989 to 1995, she was with Prudential Bank and Trust Company in Atlanta, Georgia, first as Vice President and Assistant Comptroller and then as Vice President of Reenginnering. Guillermin is a graduate of the University of Virginia.
