
    On April 7th at 11:30 AM ET, First Lady Jill Biden will be joined virtually by U.S. military families, advocates, and stakeholders from around the world as she gives remarks to set forth the priorities of the next phase of Joining Forces, a White House initiative to support military and veteran families, caregivers and survivors. This virtual event will be held at the White House Complex, in the Eisenhower Executive Office Building's South Court Auditorium, and will be pooled press / open press via livestream. Additional details to follow.
Following the remarks, the First Lady will head to Arlington, Virginia, where she will tour the Military OneSource call center at 1:30 PM ET. Military OneSource is a Department of Defense resource providing 24/7 support to service members, their families, and survivors. Additional details to follow.
