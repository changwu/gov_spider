
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Richard J. Fitzgerald to be a member of the Board of Trustees of the Harry S. Truman Scholarship Foundation for a term expiring December 10, 1991. This is a reappointment.
Judge Fitzgerald has been serving on this Board since 1982. He is currently the presiding judge of the criminal division of the circuit court of Cook County. Judge Fitzgerald graduated from St. Louis University School of Law. He is married, has two children, and resides in South Holland, IL. He was born January 23, 1914, in Hammond, IN.
