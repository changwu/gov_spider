
    On Friday in Washington, DC, Lyn Nofziger and Mary Matalin will hold a press conference at the National Press Club in the Zenger Room. They will release a scorecard for voters to help keep track of Clinton's debate performance. The Dole campaign will also release the location of more than 100 debate watching events where this scorecard will be used. Media planning to attend must arrive thirty minutes early and be prepared to provide local or national credentials.
All times are local. More details to follow.
