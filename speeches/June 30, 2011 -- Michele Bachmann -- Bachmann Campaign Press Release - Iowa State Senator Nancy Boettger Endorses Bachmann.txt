
    Harlan, Iowa — The Bachmann for President campaign announced today receiving the endorsement of Iowa State Senator Nancy Boettger.
"I am excited to have Nancy's support for the Republican nomination for president," Bachmann said. "As a former educator, Nancy understands the importance of raising children to be good stewards in the future."
Senator Boettger and her husband Dave are farmers who live in the city of Harlan, Iowa where they run a bed and breakfast. They have four children and 11 grandchildren.  
 "Michele Bachmann is the kind of no nonsense leader that America needs," said Sen. Boettger. "Michele will stand up for what is right in any situation and I'm proud to lend my support to her campaign."
