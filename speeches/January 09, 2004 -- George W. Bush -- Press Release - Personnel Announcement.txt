
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint three individuals to serve in his administration:
The President intends to appoint Mark Anthony Carter, of West Virginia, to be a member of the Federal Service Impasses Panel at the Federal Labor Relations Authority, for a five-year term expiring January 10, 2009.
The President intends to appoint John Gilbert Cruz, of California, to be a member of the Federal Service Impasses Panel at the Federal Labor Relations Authority, for a five-year term expiring January 10, 2009.
The President intends to appoint Grace Flores-Hughes, of Virginia, to be a member of the Federal Service Impasses Panel at the Federal Labor Relations Authority, for a five-year term expiring January 10, 2009.
