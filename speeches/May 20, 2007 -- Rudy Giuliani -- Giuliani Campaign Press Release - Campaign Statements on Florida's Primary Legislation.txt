
    The Rudy Giuliani Presidential Committee released the following statements today following the signing of legislation advancing Florida's primary to January 29th.
"Floridians know that Rudy Giuliani will bring the same record of leadership and proven results to the White House that he demonstrated in turning around New York City," said Florida Attorney General and Giuliani Florida Chairman Bill McCollum.
"Rudy Giuliani is the candidate who will win in Florida. We have a strong and growing team on the ground, and the Mayor's optimistic vision for the country is resonating across the state," said campaign manager Michael DuHaime.
