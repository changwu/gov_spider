
    On Friday, July 5, 2019, the President signed into law:
H.J.Res. 60, which requests the Department of the Interior to authorize arrangements for the use of the National Mall to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the Apollo 11 moon landing;
H.R. 2940, which extends Temporary Assistance for Needy Families, Child Care Entitlements, and other HHS grant programs; and
S. 2047, which extends the Medicaid community mental health services demonstration program for two weeks.
