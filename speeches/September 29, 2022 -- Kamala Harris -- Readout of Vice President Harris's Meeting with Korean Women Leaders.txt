
    Vice President Kamala Harris today met with a group of Korean groundbreaking women in Seoul, Republic of Korea (ROK), and highlighted the importance she places on gender equity and women's empowerment around the world. The participants, all of whom have achieved great accomplishments in their respective fields, discussed barriers they faced and possible steps to reduce such barriers for the next generation of women leaders. They discussed how the ROK and the United States can continue to work together to promote shared values and provide greater opportunities for women.
