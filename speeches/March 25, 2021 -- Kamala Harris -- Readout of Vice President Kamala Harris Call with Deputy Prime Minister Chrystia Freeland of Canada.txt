
    Vice President Kamala Harris spoke today with Deputy Prime Minister Chrystia Freeland of Canada and affirmed her commitment to deepening the strong alliance between Canada and the United States. Vice President Harris and Deputy Prime Minister Freeland discussed partnering on issues of mutual interest in the Northern Triangle, including working together to address the root causes contributing to migration from Central America. They also spoke about how the United States and Canada can work together on supporting women in the care economy, helping small businesses including by promoting access to capital, and strengthening labor unions.
