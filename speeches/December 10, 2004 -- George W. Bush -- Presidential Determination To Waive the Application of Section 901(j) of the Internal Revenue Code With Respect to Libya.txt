
    Presidential Determination No. 2005-12
Memorandum for the Secretary of Treasury

Subject:  Presidential Determination to Waive the Application of Section 901(j) of the Internal Revenue Code with Respect to Libya
By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Constitution and the laws of the United States, including section 901(j)(5) of the Internal Revenue Code (the "Code"), I hereby waive the application of section 901(j)(1) of the Code with respect to Libya.
I hereby authorize and direct you to arrange for publication of this determination in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush
