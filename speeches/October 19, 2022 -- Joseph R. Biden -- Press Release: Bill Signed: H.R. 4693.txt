
    On Wednesday, October 19, 2022, the President signed into law:
H.R. 4693, the "Global Malnutrition Prevention and Treatment Act of 2021," which authorizes the United States Agency for International Development to undertake efforts to prevent and treat malnutrition globally.
Thank you to Representatives McCaul, Meeks, Young Kim, and Houlahan, and Senators Coons, Wicker, Kaine, and Boozman, and many others for their leadership.
