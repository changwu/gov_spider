
    For the second time in a week, Ben Carson took the lead in a national poll, drawing 29 percent support in an NBC/Wall Street Journal survey released on Monday evening and eclipsing Donald J. Trump, who had 23 percent.
It is the highest any Republican candidate has reached in that survey and bolsters a New York Times poll last week that showed Mr. Carson, a retired neurosurgeon, passing Mr. Trump, who had led state and national polls for months.
