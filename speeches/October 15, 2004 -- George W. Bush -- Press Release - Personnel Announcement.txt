
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to designate three individuals to serve in his administration:
The President intends to designate the following Members of the Board of Governors of the United Service Organizations, Incorporated, for a three-year term:
Darrell Irions of Florida
Dee J. Kelly, Jr. of Texas
The President intends to designate William A. Moorman, of Illinois, to be Acting Assistant Secretary of Veterans Affairs (Management).
