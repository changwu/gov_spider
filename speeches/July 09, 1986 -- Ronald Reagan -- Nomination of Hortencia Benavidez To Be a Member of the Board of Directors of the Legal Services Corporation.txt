
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Hortencia Benavidez to be a member of the Board of Directors of the Legal Services Corporation for a term expiring July 13, 1989. This is a reappointment.
Ms. Benavidez has been a member of this Board since 1984. She is employed by the El Paso Catholic Pentecostal Renewal Office in El Paso, TX.
Ms. Benavidez attended Kansas City Business School and Durham Business College. She was born October 10, 1931, in El Paso, TX.
