
    SANTA FE, NM -- Democratic Presidential candidate New Mexico Governor Bill Richardson will announce the formation of Women for Richardson and release his national women's policy platform in an event in Concord, New Hampshire, Monday, July 16th at 12:00 PM.
"I have fought for women and their issues throughout my career," said Governor Richardson. "I look forward to continuing that fight for fairness in the White House."
WHEN: Monday, July 16th, 12:00 PM
WHAT: "Women for Richardson" National Kickoff Event
WHERE: Concord City Auditorium - Main Lobby, 2 Prince Street, Concord, NH
