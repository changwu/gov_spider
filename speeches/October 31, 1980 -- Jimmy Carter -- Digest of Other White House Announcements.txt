
    The following listing includes the President's public schedule and other items of general interest announced by the White House Press Office and not included elsewhere in this issue.
October 25
Following campaign appearances in Ohio, the President went to Camp David, Md., for a weekend stay.
October 27
The President returned to the White House from Camp David.
The President left the White House for a week of campaign activities throughout 16 States. Releases and announcements issued after the cut-off date of this issue begin on page 2617.
October 31
The President has announced his intention to appoint two persons to be Government members of the Board of Directors of the National Consumer Cooperative Bank. The members are:
JAMES A. JOSEPH, Under Secretary of the Interior;
RICHARD JOHN RIOS, Director of the Community Services Administration.
