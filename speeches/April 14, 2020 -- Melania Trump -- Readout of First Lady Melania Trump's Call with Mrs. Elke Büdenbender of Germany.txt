
    Today, First Lady Melania Trump spoke with Mrs. Elke Büdenbender of Germany. Mrs. Trump and Mrs. Büdenbender discussed the latest efforts in the United States and Germany to combat the coronavirus. The two women noted shared concern over some of the disinformation that has emerged, and committed to encouraging Americans and Germans to be cautious. Mrs. Trump reaffirmed that the United States stands with Germany and the rest of Europe in the fight against the pandemic.
