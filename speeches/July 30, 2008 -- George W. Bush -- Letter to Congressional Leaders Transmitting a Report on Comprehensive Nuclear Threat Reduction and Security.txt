
    Dear Madam Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
I am pleased to transmit to the Congress, consistent with the provisions of section 699M of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2008 (Public Law 110-161), the enclosed report entitled, "Comprehensive Nuclear Threat Reduction and Security Plan."
This report, compiled by the Departments of State, Defense, Energy, and Homeland Security, details Federal plans to ensure that all weapons and weapons-usable material at vulnerable sites are sesecure from terrorist threats. As required, the report includes information on sustainability plans, interagency cooperation, programmatic and budgetary requirements, and international engagement related to these vital national security efforts.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
