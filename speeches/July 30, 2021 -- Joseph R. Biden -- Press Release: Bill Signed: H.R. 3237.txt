
    On Friday, July 30, 2021, the President signed into law:
H.R. 3237, the "Emergency Security Supplemental Appropriations Act, 2021," which provides additional fiscal year (FY) 2021 emergency funding to address the aftermath of the January 6 insurrection, needs arising from the withdrawal of American troops from Afghanistan, and expenses incurred on the Capitol complex as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
