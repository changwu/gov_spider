
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Joan F. Tobin, of Washington, D.C., for reappointment as a member of the Board of Directors of the Communication Satellite Corporation (COMSAT) for a 3-year term.
Tobin, 36, has been a member of this board since 1978. She is president of Tobin Enterprises, Inc., which holds major or controlling interests in growth companies, analyzes potential mergers, acquisitions, and partnerships, structures financial arrangements, and actively participates in the management of the component companies. She is also vice president of Tobin International, an export-import business.
