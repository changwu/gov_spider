
    On Wednesday, June 15, 2022, the President signed into law:
H.R. 3579, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 East Main Street in Maroa, Illinois, as the Jeremy L. Ridlen Post Office
Thank you to Representatives Rodney Davis, Cheri Bustos, and many others for their leadership.
H.R. 4168, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 6223 Maple Street, in Omaha, Nebraska, as the Petty Officer 1st Class Charles Jackson French Post Office.
Thank you to Representatives Don Bacon and Adrian Smith for their leadership.
