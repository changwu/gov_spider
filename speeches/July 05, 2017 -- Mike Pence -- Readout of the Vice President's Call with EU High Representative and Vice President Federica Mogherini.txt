
    The Vice President spoke by phone today with EU High Representative and Vice President Federica Mogherini. The leaders agreed on the importance of the ongoing Conference on Cyprus in Crans-Montana, Switzerland, and expressed hope that the Greek Cypriot and Turkish Cypriot leaders will agree to a settlement that would reunify Cyprus as a bizonal, bicommunal federation to the benefit of all Cypriots. The Vice President and High Representative and Vice President Mogherini also discussed trade and the Vice President's upcoming travel to Estonia, Georgia, and Montenegro.
