
    Today, President Donald J. Trump announced his intent to appoint the following individuals to key positions in his Administration:
Denise N. Saul of New York, to be a Member of the Board of Trustees of the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, for a term expiring September 1, 2025.
Jennifer M. Fischer of Michigan, to be a Member of the Board of Trustees of the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, for a term expiring September 1, 2025.
Robert Eugene Simison of Idaho, to be a Member of the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation, for a term expiring June 10, 2021.
