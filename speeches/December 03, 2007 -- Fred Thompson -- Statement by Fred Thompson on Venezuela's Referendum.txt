
    McLean, VA - Senator Thompson today released the following statement on Venezuela's Referendum:
"Today we congratulate millions of Venezuelans for voting in favor of freedom and democracy and against the constitutional dictatorship proposed by Hugo Chavez.
"By courageously opposing an autocrat who has successfully sought to intimidate and terrorize his own people as he rolls back democracy, Venezuelans have at least temporarily saved their country from Castro-style totalitarian rule.
"All of us who believe in freedom, democracy, and respect for basic human rights should celebrate this important victory."
