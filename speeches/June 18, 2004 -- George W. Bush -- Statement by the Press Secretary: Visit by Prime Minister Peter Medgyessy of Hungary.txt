
    President Bush will welcome Prime Minister Peter Medgyessy of Hungary to the White House on June 22, 2004. Hungary is a close friend and ally of the United States, and a valued contributor to the international effort to bring peace and democracy to Iraq. The President and Prime Minister Medgyessy will discuss a number of key issues, including combating terrorism, U.S.-Hungarian economic relations, and the U.S.-European Union Summit in Ireland and the NATO Summit in Turkey later this month.
