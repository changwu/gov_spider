
    On Wednesday, December 7, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will convene a roundtable with Jewish leaders to discuss the rise of antisemitism and efforts to combat hate in our nation. Ambassador Susan Rice, White House Domestic Policy Advisor; Ambassador Deborah Lipstadt, Special Envoy to Monitor and Combat Antisemitism; and Keisha Lance Bottoms, Senior Advisor to the President for Public Engagement, will join the roundtable. The remarks at the top of the roundtable will be pooled press. Additional details to follow.
