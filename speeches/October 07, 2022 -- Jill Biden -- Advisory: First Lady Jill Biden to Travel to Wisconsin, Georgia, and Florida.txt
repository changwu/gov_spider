
    On Wednesday, October 12, the First Lady will travel to Milwaukee, Wisconsin to speak with the Milwaukee Teachers' Education Association (MTEA) of the National Education Association (NEA) and visit students, parents, and educators at a Milwaukee Public Schools' "Homework Diner."
On Thursday, October 13, and Friday, October 14, as part of the White House's Joining Forces initiative, the First Lady will travel to Fort Benning, Georgia to visit service members and military families at U.S. Army Fort Benning and The Maneuver Center of Excellence.
On Saturday, October 15, as part of the Biden Administration's Cancer Moonshot and for National Mammography Day, the First Lady will travel to Broward County, Florida to host a cancer event with Representative Debbie Wasserman Schultz focused on breast cancer survivorship.
