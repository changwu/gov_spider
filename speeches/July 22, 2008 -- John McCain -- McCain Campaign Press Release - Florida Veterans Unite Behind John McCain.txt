
    TALLAHASSEE, FL -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced the leadership of Florida Veterans for John McCain, a statewide grassroots organization that will work to bolster John McCain's support within Florida's veterans' community.
"John McCain has the leadership, judgment and experience to keep Americans safe at home and abroad in these difficult times," said Chair of Florida Veterans for McCain USAF Colonel Bud Day (Ret.), a longtime friend and fellow Vietnam POW with McCain. "Florida's veterans support John McCain because they know he will put his country first and make the difficult and sometimes unpopular decisions needed to protect the homeland and our troops."
Today's announcement occurs in conjunction with Veterans for McCain events in Pensacola, Tampa, Jacksonville and throughout the country.
