
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to nominate seven individuals and designate one individual to serve in his administration:
The President intends to nominate the following individuals to be Members of the National Museum and Library Services Board (Library):
Beverly Allen of Georgia, for a term expiring December 6, 2008
Gail Daly of Texas, for a term expiring December 6, 2008
Donald Leslie of Wisconsin, for a term expiring December 6, 2006
Amy Owen of Utah, for a term expiring December 6, 2008
Sandra Pickett of Texas, for a term expiring December 6, 2005
Renee Swartz of New Jersey, for a term expiring December 6, 2007
Kim Wang of California, for a term expiring December 6, 2004
The President intends to designate Edward Brehm, of Minnesota, to be Chairperson of the Board of Directors of the African Development Foundation.
