
    Today Kasich for America announced Ohio Gov. John Kasich's new leadership team in Maryland, which includes six Republican Party leaders in the state.
Said Kasich, "I'm grateful to have the support of these leaders in Maryland. Their commitment to conservative values and our mission to return money, power and influence to the American people make them a vital and important asset to our team."

Kasich for America Maryland Leadership Team
Mary Beth Carozza, Maryland Delegate for District 38C in Wicomico and Worcester CountiesJake Shade, President of the Allegany County CommissionersLoretta Shields, Chairman of Howard County Central CommitteeNathan Volke, Chairman of Anne Arundel County Central CommitteeRob Willoughby, Former Chairman of Caroline County Central CommitteeErik Robey, Former Vice Chairman of the Maryland Republican Party
