
    President Donald J. Trump today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Montevideo, Uruguay to attend the Presidential Inauguration of Dr. Luis Lacalle Pou, President-elect of the Oriental Republic of Uruguay, on March 1, 2020.
The Honorable Andrew R. Wheeler, Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Kenneth S. George, United States Ambassador to the Oriental Republic of Uruguay
The Honorable Mauricio Claver-Carone, Deputy Assistant to the President and Senior Director for Western Hemisphere Affairs, National Security Council
The Honorable Michael G. Kozak, Acting Assistant Secretary, Bureau of Western Hemisphere Affairs, Department of State
