
    On Wednesday, October 23rd, the Vice President will travel to Marinette, WI. There, he will tour and deliver remarks at the Fincantieri Marinette Marine. Then, the Vice President will travel to Pleasant Prairie, WI and visit Uline Headquarters, a leading distributor of shipping, industrial and packaging materials. There, he will deliver remarks on the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement as part of an America First Policies series called "USMCA: A Better Deal for American Workers."
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
