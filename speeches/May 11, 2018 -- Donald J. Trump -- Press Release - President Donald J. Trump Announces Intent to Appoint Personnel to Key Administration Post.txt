
    President Donald J. Trump today announced his intent to appoint the following individual to a key position in his Administration:
Stephen A. Feinberg of New York, to be a Member, and upon appointment, Chair of the President's Intelligence Advisory Board.
Mr. Feinberg is the co-founder, Executive Chairman, and Co-Chief Executive Officer of Cerberus Capital Management and brings over 30 years of experience conducting organizational assessments, operational improvements, and complex reforms across foreign and domestic stakeholders to his position. Mr. Feinberg is a graduate of Princeton University and grew up in Spring Valley, New York.
