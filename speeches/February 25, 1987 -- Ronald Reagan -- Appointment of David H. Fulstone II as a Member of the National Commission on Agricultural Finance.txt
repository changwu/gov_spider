
    The President announced today his intention to appoint David H. Fulstone II to be a member of the National Commission on Agricultural Finance. This is a new position.
Mr. Fulstone is currently a rancher in Nevada. He is active in local agricultural, community, and political organizations. Mr. Fulstone is married, has one child, and resides in Yerington, NV. He was born December 23, 1950, in Yerington.
