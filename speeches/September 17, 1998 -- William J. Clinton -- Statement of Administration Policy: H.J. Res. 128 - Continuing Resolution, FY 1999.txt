
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)
(Sponsor: Livingston (R), Louisiana)
This Statement of Administration Policy provides the Administration's views on H.J. Res. 128, the Continuing Resolution, FY 1999. Your consideration of the Administration's views would be appreciated.
The Administration has no objection to the resolution and urges the House to adopt it in its current form.
The Administration urges the Congress to complete action on the pending FY 1999 appropriations bills as quickly as possible and send them to the President in acceptable form.
