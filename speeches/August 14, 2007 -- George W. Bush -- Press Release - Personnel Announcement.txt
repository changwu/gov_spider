
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint two individuals to serve in his Administration.
The President intends to appoint Anthony K. Dungy, of Indiana, to be a Member of the President's Council on Service and Civic Participation for a term of two years.
The President intends to appoint Anthony Carroll Wisniewski, of Maryland, to be a Member of the Board of Trustees of the Christopher Columbus Fellowship Foundation (Recommendation of the Minority Leader of the House of Representatives) for the remainder of a six-year term expiring 03/23/13.
