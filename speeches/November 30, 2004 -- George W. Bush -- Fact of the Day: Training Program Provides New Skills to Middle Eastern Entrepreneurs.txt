
    Business owners and executives from 15 Middle Eastern countries are gaining new skills to help increase economic opportunity in the region. Some 35 business leaders -- 40 percent of whom are women -- are learning business development, job creation, and management. The Middle East Entrepreneurship Training Program, a project of the U.S. Government's Middle East Partnership Initiative, is sponsoring this training program in Silicon Valley, California.
Source: U.S. Department of State
