
    The President today announced the appointment of two persons as members of the Board of Governors of the United Service Organizations, Inc. (USO). They are:
Maxine E. Flournoy, of Alice, Tex. Flournoy served in the Women's Airforce Service Pilots (WASP's) during World War II as a pilot. She has also served as a company pilot and, with her husband, operates an oil well drilling company.
Jeff Wald, of Los Angeles, president of .left Wald Television Productions and Wald-Nanas Associates, and a member of the board of governors of Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles.
