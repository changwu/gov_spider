
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the Inauguration of Her Excellency Michelle Bachelet, President-elect of the Republic of Chile on March 11, 2006.
The Honorable Condoleezza Rice, Secretary of State will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Craig A. Kelly, United States Ambassador to the Republic of Chile
The Honorable Karen P. Hughes, Under Secretary of State for Public Diplomacy and Public Affairs
