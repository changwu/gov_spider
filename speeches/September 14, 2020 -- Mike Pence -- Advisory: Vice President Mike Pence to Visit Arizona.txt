
    On Friday, September 18th, Vice President Mike Pence will travel to Phoenix, Arizona. There, the Vice President will participate in a Libre Initiative Roundtable for Hispanic Heritage Month focusing on the Trump administration's dedication to the Hispanic American community. During President Trump and Vice President Pence's administration, they have enacted pro-growth policies, which led to a record low unemployment for Hispanic Americans. Following, the Vice President will participate in a Veterans for Trump event highlighting the Trump administration's rebuilding of the military and unwavering support for the women and men who wear the uniform. Later that evening, the Vice President will return to Washington, D.C.
Additional details about the Vice President's trip are forthcoming.
