
    National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met with Foreign Minister Vivian Balakrishnan of Singapore at the White House on June 20, 2023. They reflected on the enduring strength of the U.S.-Singapore Strategic Partnership and reviewed opportunities to deepen cooperation, including through the U.S.-Singapore Climate Partnership. They discussed establishing a bilateral dialogue regarding critical and emerging technologies, and tasked their respective senior officials with expeditious follow-up. Mr. Sullivan and Dr. Balakrishnan also discussed a wide range of regional and global issues, including regional cybersecurity challenges.
