
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Maria C. Bechily, of Chicago, Ill., to be a member of the National Advisory Council on Women's Educational Programs.
Bechily, 31, is president of Ms. Executive Search, an executive search organization specializing in placing women and Hispanic executives. She was previously a counselor and placement specialist for the on-the-job training program of the Chicago Alliance of Business and Manpower Services.
