
    Following the State of the Union Address last week, First Lady Jill Biden, U.S. Secretary of Labor Marty Walsh, and U.S. Secretary of Energy Jennifer Granholm will travel to Valparaiso, Indiana on Friday, February 17th to highlight the Biden Administration's commitment to strengthening our economy and building pathways to good-paying jobs through career-connected learning.
At 12:45 PM, the First Lady, U.S. Secretary of Labor Marty Walsh, and U.S. Secretary of Energy Jennifer Granholm will arrive at Porter County Regional Airport in Valparaiso, IN. This arrival will be open press. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Wednesday, February 15th at 12:00 PM.
At 1:00 PM, the First Lady, U.S. Secretary of Labor Marty Walsh, and U.S. Secretary of Energy Jennifer Granholm will visit Ivy Tech Community College in Valparaiso, IN to highlight the Biden-Harris Administration's commitment to career-connected learning programs that build pathways for good-paying jobs. For interested media, RSVP HERE by Wednesday, February 15th at 12:00 PM.
