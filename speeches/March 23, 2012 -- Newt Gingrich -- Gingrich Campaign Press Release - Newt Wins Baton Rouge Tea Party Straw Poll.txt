
    
Yesterday, Newt overwhelmingly won the Baton Rouge Tea Party straw poll with 53%, his second major Tea Party straw poll victory in Louisiana.
Here are yesterday's full results:
Gingrich — 53%
Paul — 25%
Santorum — 20%
Romney — 1%
