
    President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint four individuals to serve in his administration:
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the Utah Reclamation Mitigation and Conservation Commission:
The President intends to appoint Brad T. Barber of Utah, for the remainder of a four-year term expiring July 10, 2006.
The President intends to appoint Kevin Kent Conway of Utah, for the remainder of a four-year term expiring November 6, 2005.
The President intends to appoint Dallin Willard Jensen of Utah, for the remainder of a four-year term expiring July 18, 2006.
The President intends to appoint Jody Lynn Williams of Utah, for a four-year term expiring July 10, 2006.
