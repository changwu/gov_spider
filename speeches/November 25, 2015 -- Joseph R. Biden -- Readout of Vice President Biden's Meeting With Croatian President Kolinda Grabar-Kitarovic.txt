
    Vice President Joe Biden met today with Croatian President Kolinda Grabar-Kitarovic. The Vice President thanked President Grabar-Kitarovic for hosting the Brdo-Brijuni summit of Southeast European leaders and for supporting Euro-Atlantic integration processes in the region. The two leaders also discussed the need to encourage reconciliation among the countries of Southeast Europe, as well as the need to advance energy security and coordinate migration policies in the region.
