
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report six new deferrals of budget authority for 1985 totaling $58,900,000 and four revised deferrals now totaling $110,566,481. The deferrals affect the Departments of Agriculture, Health and Human Services, Justice, and Labor.
The details of these deferrals are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
March 1, 1985.
