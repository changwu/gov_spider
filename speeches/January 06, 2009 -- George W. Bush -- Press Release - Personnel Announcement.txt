
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Accra, Ghana to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Professor John Evans Atta Mills, President of the Republic of Ghana, on January 7, 2009.
The Honorable Ronald A. Tschetter, Director of the Peace Corps, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Donald G. Teitelbaum, United States Ambassador to the Republic of Ghana
Rear Admiral R. Timothy Ziemer, Coordinator of United States Government Activities to Combat Malaria Globally
