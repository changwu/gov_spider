
    Pursuant to sections 2103(a) and 2105(a) of the Trade Act of 2002, I have notified the Congress of my intention to enter into a Free Trade Agreement with the Government of Chile.
Pursuant to section 2105(a)(1) of that Act, this notice shall be published in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush The White House, January 29, 2003. [Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 12:18 p.m., January 30, 2003]
