
    National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with Jean-Charles Ellermann-Kingombe, Permanent Under-Secretary of State at the Danish Prime Minister's Office. They condemned Russia's ongoing aggression against Ukraine and reaffirmed their commitment to continue providing Ukraine with assistance. They talked about ongoing work within the NATO Alliance, including preparations for the Vilnius Summit. They reaffirmed their intent to continue working together on addressing energy security, highlighting the benefits of the transition to clean energy. They also concurred on the importance of coordinating on shared economic priorities, such as sustainable supply chains and critical minerals.
