
    In honor of Asian Pacific American Heritage Month starting today, Hannah Castillo, Director of Coalitions for Donald J. Trump for President, Inc., issued the following statement.
"Asian Pacific American Heritage Month reminds us how much our nation is enriched by the history, culture, talents, and energies of Americans from Asia and the Pacific Islands. President Trump is dedicated to ensuring that our nation honors their diligence and patriotic devotion by fostering opportunity in all our communities for workers, families, and entrepreneurs to thrive in peace and freedom. We join the President in thanking Americans of Asian Pacific heritage this month for Keeping America Great!"
