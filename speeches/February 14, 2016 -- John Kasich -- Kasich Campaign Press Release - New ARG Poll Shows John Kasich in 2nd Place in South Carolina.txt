
    On the night of the Republican Primary Debate in Greenville, South Carolina, a new American Research Group poll shows that John Kasich is in second place among likely Republican primary voters living in South Carolina.
The poll was taken Feb. 12-13, 2016, and shows John Kasich in 2nd place with 15 percent of the vote, 1 point ahead of Marco Rubio, 3 points ahead of Ted Cruz, and 5 points ahead of Jeb Bush.
