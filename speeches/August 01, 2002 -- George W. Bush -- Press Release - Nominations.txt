
    President George W. Bush today announced his nomination of seven individuals to serve as Members of the Federal Judiciary.
The President has nominated Gregory L. Frost to be United States District Judge for the Southern District of Ohio.
The President has nominated Mark E. Fuller to be United States District Judge for the Middle District of Alabama.
The President has nominated Robert B. Kugler to be United States District Judge for the District of New Jersey.
The President has nominated Freda L. Wolfson to be United States District Judge for the District of New Jersey.
The President has nominated Jose L. Linares to be United States District Judge for the District of New Jersey.
The President has nominated Richard J. Holwell to be United States District Judge for the Southern District of New York.
The President has nominated Rosemary M. Collyer to be United States District Judge for the District of Columbia.
