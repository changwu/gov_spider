
    President Bush Announced His Intention to Nominate Four Individuals to Serve as Members of the Federal Judiciary
President George W. Bush today announced his intention to nominate of four individuals to serve as members of the federal judiciary.
The President intends to nominate Cormac J. Carney to be United States District Judge for the Central District of California.
The President intends to nominate John R. Adams to be United States District Judge for the Northern District of Ohio.
The President intends to nominate Thomas A. Varlan to be United States District Judge for the Eastern District of Tennessee.
The President intends to nominate J. Daniel Breen to be United States District Judge for the Western District of Tennessee.
