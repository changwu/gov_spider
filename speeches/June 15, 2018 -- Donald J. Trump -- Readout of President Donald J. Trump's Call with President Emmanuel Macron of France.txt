
    President Donald J. Trump spoke today with President Emmanuel Macron of France. The leaders discussed President Trump's summit in Singapore with North Korean leader Kim Jong Un. President Trump reaffirmed the United States goal of achieving a complete, verifiable, and irreversible denuclearization of North Korea. The leaders conferred on ensuring a political solution to the situation in Syria through the United Nations Geneva process. President Trump underscored his goal of negotiating a comprehensive deal with Iran that covers Iran's nuclear and ballistic missile programs and malign activities in the region. President Trump also called on the European Union to enter into negotiations to reduce trade barriers.
