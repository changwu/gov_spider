
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one new deferral of budget authority for 1985 totaling $16,004,810. The deferral affects an account in the United States Information Agency.
The details of this deferral are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
July 30, 1985.
