
    
  Rally at Sooner State's Capitol to feature legislator endorsements and a media availability

LAKE JACKSON, Texas — 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul will hold a major campaign rally in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.
The rally will take place on the plaza facing the Oklahoma State Capitol on Saturday, February 25th. In addition to a speech by the 12-term Congressman from Texas, the event will feature legislator endorsements and a media availability. On this visit, Carol Paul and granddaughter Linda will accompany Dr. Paul.
Details of the event are as follows. Time is Central.
Saturday, February 25, 2012
1:00 p.m.
Oklahoma City Rally
Capital Plaza North and South
2300 N. Lincoln Blvd.
Oklahoma City, OK 73105
