
    On Friday, April 7, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will join Secretary of Energy Jennifer Granholm in Oklahoma as part of the Biden-Harris Administration's Investing in America tour. The Second Gentleman and Secretary Granholm will tour Oklahoma State University in Stillwater and make an announcement on funding from the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law. Following this visit, Mr. Emhoff and Secretary Granholm will participate in a roundtable discussion on geothermal energy at the University of Oklahoma in Norman. They will be joined by representatives from Tribal Nations. The Investing in America tour highlights how the Biden-Harris Administration is delivering historic investments to communities across the nation. For interested media, RSVP HERE.
