
    The President today announced the appointment of two persons as members of the National Armed Forces Museum Advisory Board of the Smithsonian Institution. They are:
ANDREW J. GOODPASTER, Superintendent of the United States Military Academy;
THEODORE ROPP, a professor of history at Duke University who is currently a visiting professor at Singapore University and Australia National University. He has been a member of this Board since 1975 and is president and a trustee of the American Military Institute.
