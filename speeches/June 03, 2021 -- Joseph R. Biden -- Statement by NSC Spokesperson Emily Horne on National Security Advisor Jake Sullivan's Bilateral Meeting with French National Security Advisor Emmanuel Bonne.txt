
    Today, National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met in Washington with Emmanuel Bonne, National Security Advisor to the President of France, to exchange views on a range of shared global policy issues. They talked about the upcoming G7 leaders meeting and NATO Summit. Mr. Sullivan and Mr. Bonne agreed to continue coordinating on global access to COVID-19 vaccines and supporting negotiations to address the tax challenges arising from globalization. They discussed our work to strengthen and modernize NATO, as well as counter challenges associated with malicious cyber activity such as ransomware, and terrorism. In addition, they discussed Russia, China, Iran, and the Sahel.
