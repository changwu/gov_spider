On Sunday, May 21st, at 12:30 PM, as part of the First Lady's Joining Forces initiative, the First Lady will visit M.C. Perry High School, a Department of Defense Education Activity (DoDEA) school, at Marine Corps Air Station Iwakuni to participate in a celebration of the school's graduating seniors. The First Lady will deliver remarks and meet with U.S. military students and families in support of our nation's military-connected community serving abroad. This visit will be open press.
