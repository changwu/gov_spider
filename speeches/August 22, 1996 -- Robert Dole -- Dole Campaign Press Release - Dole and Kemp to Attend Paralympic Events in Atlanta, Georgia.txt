
    Republican presidential nominee Bob Dole, vice-presidential nominee Jack Kemp, and Joanne Kemp will visit the Paralympics in Atlanta, Georgia on Friday, August 23, 1996. They will arrive at 4:50pm EDT at Atlanta Hartsfield International Airport. They will then attend several pool coverage only events with Paralympic athletes. Media should arrive at the airport at 3:30pm EDT for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials with photo identification. All times are local.
