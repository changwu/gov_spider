
    President Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to observe the Presidential and Legislative Elections in Managua, Nicaragua on November 5, 2006.
The Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Paul Trivelli, United States Ambassador to the Republic of Nicaragua
The Honorable J. Bennett Johnston, Former United States Senator from Louisiana
The Honorable Bill Paxon, Former Member of the United States House of Representatives from New York
