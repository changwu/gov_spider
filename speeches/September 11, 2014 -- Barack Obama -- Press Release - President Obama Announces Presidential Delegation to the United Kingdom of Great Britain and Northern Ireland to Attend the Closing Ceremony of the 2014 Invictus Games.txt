
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland to attend the Closing Ceremony of the 2014 Invictus Games on September 14, 2014.
The delegation will attend athletic events, meet with U.S. athletes, and attend the Closing Ceremony.
Dr. Jill Biden will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Matthew W. Barzun, Ambassador of the United States of America to the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, Department of State
Dr. Jonathan Woodson, Assistant Secretary of Defense for Health Affairs
Lieutenant Commander Daniel Cnossen, United States Navy
Mrs. Lisa Battaglia, Spouse of the Senior Enlisted Advisor to the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff
Mrs. Jeanne Chandler, Spouse of the Sergeant Major of the Army
Mrs. Blaire Brush, Spouse of the Senior Enlisted Advisor for the National Guard Bureau
