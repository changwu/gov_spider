
    On Friday, September 18, 2009, the President signed into law:
H.R. 3325, which extends for one year the Social Security Administration's Work Incentives Planning and Assistance program and Protection and Advocacy for Beneficiaries of Social Security program; and
S.J.Res. 9, which appoints France A. Cordova as a citizen regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution.
