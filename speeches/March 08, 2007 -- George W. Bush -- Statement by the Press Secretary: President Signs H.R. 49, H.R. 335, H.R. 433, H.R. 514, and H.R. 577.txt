
    On Wednesday, March 07, 2007, the President signed into law:
H.R. 49, designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Gerald R. Ford, Jr. Post Office Building;
H.R. 335, designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Gale W. McGee Post Office;
H.R. 433, designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Scipio A. Jones Post Office Building;
H.R. 514, designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Sergeant Lea Robert Mills Brooksville Aviation Branch Post Office;
H.R. 577, designates a facility of the United States Postal Service as the Sergeant Henry Ybarra III Post Office Building.
