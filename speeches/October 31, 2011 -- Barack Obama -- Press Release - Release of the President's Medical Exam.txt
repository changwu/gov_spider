
    The following is a report from the President's second periodic physical examination performed at the White House Medical Unit with participation of consultants from the Walter Reed National Military Medical Center.
The physical examination was performed last week by Dr. Jeff Kuhlman, a Navy captain and the Physician to the President. Other specialists assisted in their areas of expertise.
Dr. Kuhlman recommends the President's next physical take place in December 2012.
