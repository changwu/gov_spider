
    Today, President Donald J. Trump released a report detailing his Administration's successful efforts to prevent, mitigate, and respond to mass atrocities. He has also launched a White House-led, interagency Atrocity Early Warning Task Force, which will coordinate the efforts of the United States Government to stop mass atrocities and enhance accountability for those who commit them. As President Trump has said, "We will never, ever be silent in the face of evil again . . . and we pledge: never again."
