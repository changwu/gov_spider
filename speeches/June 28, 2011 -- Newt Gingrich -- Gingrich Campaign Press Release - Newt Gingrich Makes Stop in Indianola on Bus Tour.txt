
    Approximately 50 people gathered inside the Pizza Ranch in Indianola June 25 to visit with presidential candidate Newt Gingrich as he made a stop on the Iowa Tea Party Bus Tour.
  The stop was part of the 18-day bus tour featuring Gingrich, Michele Bachmann, Herman Cain, Gary Johnson, Tim Pawlenty, Rick Santorum and other announced and potential presidential candidates.
"I think 2012 is the most important election since 1860 in defining our future as Americans," Gingrich said during the visit.
Gingrich spent nearly 45 minutes talking to the crowd and fielding questions.
He stated that job creation is a priority in his candidacy.
