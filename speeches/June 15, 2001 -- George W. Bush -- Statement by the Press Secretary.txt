
    President Bush will travel to Genoa, Italy to attend the G-7/8 Summit on July 20-22. While in Italy, the President will visit Rome to meet with President Ciampi and Prime Minister Berlusconi. He will also have an audience at Castelgandolfo with His Holiness Pope John Paul II. The President will begin his trip to Europe with a stop in the United Kingdom for discussions with Prime Minister Blair.
