
    On Wednesday, March 8th, the Vice President will travel to Miami Beach, Florida. In Miami Beach, the Vice President will deliver remarks at a democratic fundraiser. These remarks will be covered by an editorial pool. While there, the Vice President will join the Aspen Institute's Aspen Ideas: Climate 2023 and participate in a moderated conversation on the Biden-Harris Administration's investments and actions to combat the climate crisis and ongoing efforts to build a new clean energy economy that works for all. This moderated conversation will be pooled press and open to pre-credentialed media. Interested media should RSVP HERE for additional details.
