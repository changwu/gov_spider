
    Presidential Determination No. 94-48
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to section 2(b)(2) of the Migration and Refugee Assistance Act of 1962, as amended, 22 U.S.C. 2601(b)(2), I hereby designate persons fleeing from or repatriating to Cuba and Haiti as qualifying for assistance and determine that such assistance will contribute to the foreign policy interests of the United States.
You are authorized and directed to inform the appropriate committees of the Congress of this determination and the obligation of funds under this authority and to publish this determination in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, September 9, 1994.
