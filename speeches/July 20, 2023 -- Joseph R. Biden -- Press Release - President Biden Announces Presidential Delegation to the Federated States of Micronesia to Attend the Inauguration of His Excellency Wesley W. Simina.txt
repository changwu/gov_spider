
    President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Wesley W. Simina on Wednesday, July 26th, 2023, in Palikir, Federated States of Micronesia.
The Honorable Deb Haaland, Secretary of the United States Department of the Interior, will lead the delegation.
