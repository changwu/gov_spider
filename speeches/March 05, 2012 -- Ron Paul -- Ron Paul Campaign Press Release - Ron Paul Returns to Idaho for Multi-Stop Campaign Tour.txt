
    
  Visits Gem State ahead of first-ever caucus taking place March 6th—Super Tuesday

BOISE, Idaho — 2012 Republican Presidential candidate Ron Paul will return to Idaho for a series of campaign stops in the run-up to the Gem State's first-ever caucus taking place on March 6th — Super Tuesday.
The visit will take place on Monday, March 5th and Tuesday, March 6th.
Details of the 12-term Congressman from Texas' visit are as follows. Please note time zones.
