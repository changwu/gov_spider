
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Ankara, Turkey to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Recep Tayyip Erdogan, President-elect of the Republic of Turkey on August 28, 2014.
Mr. Jess L. Baily, Charge d'Affaires a.i. of the Embassy of the United States to the Republic of Turkey, will attend the Inauguration.
