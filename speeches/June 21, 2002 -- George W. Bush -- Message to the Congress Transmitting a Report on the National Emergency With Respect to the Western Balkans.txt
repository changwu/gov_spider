
    To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 401(c) of the National Emergencies Act, 50 U.S.C. 1641(c), and section 204(c) of the International Emergency Economic Powers Act, 50 U.S.C. 1703(c), I transmit herewith a 6-month report prepared by my Administration on the national emergency with respectto the Western Balkans that was declared in Executive Order 13219 of June 26, 2001.
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, June 21, 2002.
