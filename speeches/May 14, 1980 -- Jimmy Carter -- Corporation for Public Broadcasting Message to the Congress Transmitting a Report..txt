
    To the Congress of the United States:
I hereby transmit the "Annual Report of the Corporation for Public Broadcasting for FY 1979," in accordance with the Public Telecommunications Financing Act of 1978 (Public Law 95-567).
The Corporation's thorough report presents its major accomplishments during the past fiscal year in support of public radio and television broadcasting, technological change including satellite advances, and achievements in human resource development. The report notes the Corporation's vigorous response to the challenge provided by the Public Telecommunications Financing Act of 1978.
The Annual Report is being forwarding for the deliberations of the Congress.
JIMMY CARTER
The White House,
May 14, 1980.
