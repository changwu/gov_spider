
    By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 204 of Part II of the act entitled "AN ACT Making appropriations for the Legislative Branch of the Government for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1933, and for other purposes," approved June 30, 1932, Charles J. Harlow, plate printer in the Coast and Geodetic Survey of the Department of Commerce, who, during the current month, reaches the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, is hereby exempted from the provisions of that section until March 31, 1933.
HERBERT HOOVER
The White House,December 22, 1932.
