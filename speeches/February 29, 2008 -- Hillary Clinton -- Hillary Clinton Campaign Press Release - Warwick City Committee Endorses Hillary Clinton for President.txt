
    Warwick, RI: The Warwick Democratic City Committee today announced their endorsement of Hillary Clinton for president, citing her ability to bring real solutions to the problems facing America's middle class families and to provide universal healthcare.
"We believe in Hillary's vision for the future of our country and are proud to give her our endorsement for president," said Warwick Democratic City Committee Chairwoman Nancy Mayor. "Hillary Clinton's plan to restore America's middle class includes lowering taxes, providing quality health care to every American and making college accessible to all students. These are the issues that are important to the people of the city of Warwick."
Members of the committee have been working and will continue to work over the coming days to help deliver a Hillary Clinton victory in Rhode Island on March 4.
