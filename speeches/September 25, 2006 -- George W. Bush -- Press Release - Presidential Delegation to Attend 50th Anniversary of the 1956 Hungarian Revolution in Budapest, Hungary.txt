
    President Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend The 50th Anniversary of the 1956 Hungarian Revolution in Budapest, Hungary on October 23, 2006.
The Honorable George E. Pataki, Governor of New York will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable April H. Foley, U.S. Ambassador to Hungary
Mr. Peter K. Gogolak, former professional football player, New York Giants
Mr. Steven F. Udvar-Hazy, Chairman and CEO, International Lease Finance Corporation
