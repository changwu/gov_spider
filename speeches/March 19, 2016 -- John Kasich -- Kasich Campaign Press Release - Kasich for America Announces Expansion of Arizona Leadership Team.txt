
    Today Kasich for America announced that Former State Senator John Kaites, and Gordon and Lisa James will also serve on Governor Kasich's Arizona leadership team.
Kasich said, "We have a great team in Arizona. I am very appreciative of their help to share our positive message to strengthen our country and bring real solutions to the American people."
