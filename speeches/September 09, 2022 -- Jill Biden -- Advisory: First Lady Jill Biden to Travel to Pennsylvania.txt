
    On Sunday, September 11, First Lady Jill Biden and U.S. Secretary of the Interior Deb Haaland will attend and deliver remarks at the Flight 93 National Memorial Observance in Shanksville, Pennsylvania, to honor and remember the victims of the September 11th terror attack.
At 8:30 AM ET, the First Lady will arrive at John Murtha Johnstown-Cambria County Airport in Cambria County, PA. This arrival will be open press. For interested media, please RSVP HERE by Friday 9/9 at 6:00 PM ET.
At 10:30 AM ET, the First Lady and Secretary Haaland will deliver remarks at the Flight 93 National Memorial Observance and participate in a wreath-laying ceremony. The First Lady will also join members of the Association of Flight Attendants-CWA to honor and remember the crew members of Flight 93. This event will be open press and pooled for TV. For interested media, please RSVP HERE through the National Park Service by Saturday 9/10 at 9:00 AM ET.
