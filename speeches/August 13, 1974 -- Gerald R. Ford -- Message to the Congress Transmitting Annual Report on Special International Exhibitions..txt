
    To the Congress of the United States:
As required by law, I transmit to the Congress the Eleventh Annual Report on Special International Exhibitions conducted during fiscal year 1973 under the authority of the Mutual Educational and Cultural Exchange Act of 1961 (Public Law 87-256).
This report covers exhibitions presented abroad by the U.S. Information Agency at international fairs and under East-West Cultural Exchange agreements, as well as exhibitions and labor missions presented abroad by the Department of Labor.
GERALD R. FORD
The White House,
August 13, 1974.
