
    To the Congress of the United States:
I transmit herewith the Saint Lawrence Seaway Development Corporation's Annual Report for fiscal year 1992. This report has been prepared in accordance with section 10 of the Saint Lawrence Seaway Act of May 13, 1954 (33 U.S.C. 989(a)), and covers the period October 1, 1991, through September 30, 1992.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House,
September 21, 1993.
