
    On Friday, May 6, 2022, the President signed into law:
S. 400, the "William T. Coleman, Jr. and Norman Y. Mineta Department of Transportation Headquarters Act," which designates the headquarters building of the Department of Transportation located at 1200 New Jersey Avenue, SE, in Washington, DC, as the William T. Coleman, Jr. and Norman Y. Mineta Federal Building.
Thank you to Senators Wicker, Cantwell, Carper, Capito, Tim Scott, Booker, Warnock, Inhofe, Duckworth and many others, and Representatives DeFazio and Carson for their leadership.
