
    EXECUTIVE ORDER 8108
REVOCATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 6644 OF MARCH 14, 1934, WITHDRAWING PUBLIC LANDS
May 3, 1939
COLORADO
By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, c. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6644 of March 14, 1934, withdrawing public lands in Colorado pending a resurvey, and heretofore partially revoked, is hereby revoked as to the remainder of the lands affected thereby. This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the lands involved.
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
May 3, 1939.
Exec. Order No. 8108, 4 FR 1903, 1939 WL 3887 (Pres.)
