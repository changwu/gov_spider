
    Momentum Surging Across the Country
(Salt Lake City, UT) - The following is a statement from Clinton supporter State Representative Jackie Biskupski in response to Hillary's huge victory in Nevada, which was the first nomination contest in the west:
"Hillary's hard work earning caucus goers support and her focus on delivering real change and real solutions for working families helped her win a huge victory in Nevada," said State Rep. Biskupski. "Support for Senator Clinton is surging across Utah and the country because she has the momentum, the strength, and the experience to deliver the change we need."
Hillary Clinton has been talking to families across the country about the challenges they face and offering an aggressive economic stimulus plan to help deliver the relief and assistance they need.
