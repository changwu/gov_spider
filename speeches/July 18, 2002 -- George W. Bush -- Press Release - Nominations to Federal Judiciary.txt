
    President George W. Bush Today Announced the Nomination of the Following Individuals to Serve as Members of the Federal Judiciary
President George W. Bush today announced the nomination of the following individuals to serve as members of the federal judiciary:
William Smith, to be United States District Judge for the District of Rhode Island
S. James Otero, to be United States District Judge for the Central District of California
James Kinkeade, to be United States District Judge for the Northern District of Texas
Robert Junell, to be United States District Judge for the Western District of Texas
Robert G. Klausner, to be United States District Judge for the Central District of California
