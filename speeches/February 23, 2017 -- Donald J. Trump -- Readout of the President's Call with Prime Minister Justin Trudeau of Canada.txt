
    President Donald J. Trump spoke today with Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau to reiterate America's strong bilateral cooperation with Canada. President Trump underscored the importance of America's relationship with Canada for addressing the problems facing both countries, as well as the opportunities for both countries. To that end, President Trump emphasized the importance of working closely with Canada on cross-border issues, including implementation of his Administration's actions to protect America from terrorist attacks by foreign nationals and others. The two leaders noted their desire to continue building on the countries' deep partnership, which has been strengthened by their meeting on February 13.
