
    [Dated October 1, 1976. Released October 2, 1976]
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report 33 deferrals of funds totalling $761.7 million. The deferrals are primarily routine in nature and do not, for the most part, affect program levels. The details of each deferral are contained in the attached reports.
GERALD R. FORD
The White House,
October 1, 1976.
