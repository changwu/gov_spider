
    BURLINGTON, Vt. – U.S. Sen. Bernie Sanders on August 8 will travel to Seattle, Washington, where he will hold a rally at Alaska Airlines Arena at Hec Edmundson Pavilion. The Democratic Party presidential candidate will then head to Portland, Oregon, for a rally at Veterans Memorial Coliseum the following day.
Here is the itinerary:
Saturday, August 8
7 p.m. Seattle Rally, Alaska Airlines Arena at Hec Edmundson Pavilion, 3870 Montlake Blvd. NE, Seattle, Washington
Press registration can be found here.
Doors open at 6 p.m. The event is free and open to the public. Admission is first come, first served. Tickets are not required, but an RSVP is strongly encouraged.
For security reasons, please do not bring bags, and please limit personal items.
Attendees are encouraged to use public transportation or carpool. University of Washington parking is available for $15 in E1 or $10 in E1 for cars with 3 or more people. ADA parking will be in IMA, E17 and E12R.
Sunday, August 9
7 p.m. Portland Rally, Veterans Memorial Coliseum, 300 N. Winning Way, Portland, Oregon
*** This event has been moved to a bigger venue: Moda Center at Rose Quarter, 1 N. Center Court St., Portland
Press registration can be found here.
Doors open at 6 p.m. The event is free and open to the public. Admission is first come, first served. Tickets are not required, but an RSVP is strongly encouraged.
Public Transportation: MAX Station and Bus Station — Rose Quarter Transit Center
General Public Parking: Garden Garage, East Garage, West Garages on Winning Way
Bus and Group Drop-off Location: NE Wheeler Ave
Bus Staging/Parking: Benton Lot
