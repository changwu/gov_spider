
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Miller (D) California and 39 others)
The Administration supports House passage of H.R. 3684. However, the Administration will seek amendments in conference to incorporate the President's FY 1991 Budget proposal for the Council on Environmental Quality to establish awards for excellence in environmental education. The Administration will seek additional amendments to ensure that the National Environmental Education Foundation authorized by this bill is not a governmental entity, and to delete the requirement that EPA develop model environmental education curricula.
