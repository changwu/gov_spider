
    The President today announced four persons whom he will nominate to be members of the Board of Directors of the United States Railway Association. They are:
JAMES E. BURKE, 58, of Freehold, N.J., vice president of the Brotherhood of Railroad Trainmen;
ROBERT G. FLANNERY, 53, of San Francisco, president and chief executive officer of the Western Pacific Railroad Co.;
STANTON P. SENDER, 45, of Washington, D.C., transportation counsel of Sears, Roebuck and Co.;
NATHANIEL WELCH, 58, of Atlanta, executive vice president of the Georgia Freight Bureau.
