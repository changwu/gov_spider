
    Presidential Determination No. 96-40
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject: Assistance for Bosnia and Herzegovina
Pursuant to section 2 of Public Law 104-122 ("the Act"), I hereby determine and certify that the aggregate bilateral contributions pledged by non-United States donors for economic revitalization are at least equivalent to the U.S. bilateral contributions for economic revitalization made by the Act and in Public Law 104-107.
You are authorized and directed to transmit this determination to the appropriate committees of the Congress and to arrange for its publication in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, July 8, 1996.
