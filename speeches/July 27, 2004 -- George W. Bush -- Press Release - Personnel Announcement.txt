
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Warsaw, Poland to attend events in commemoration of the 60th Anniversary of the Warsaw Uprising.
The Honorable Colin L. Powell, U.S. Secretary of State, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are: The Honorable Victor Ashe, U.S. Ambassador to Poland The Honorable Edward Derwinski, Former Secretary of Veterans Affairs The Honorable Richard M. Daley, Mayor of the City of Chicago His Eminence Adam Cardinal Maida, Archbishop of Detroit The Honorable Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman of Clinique Laboratories, Inc., a subsidiary of
The Estee Lauder Companies Inc.
Mr. Chester Partyka, Former President of the Polish-American Congress of Ohio
