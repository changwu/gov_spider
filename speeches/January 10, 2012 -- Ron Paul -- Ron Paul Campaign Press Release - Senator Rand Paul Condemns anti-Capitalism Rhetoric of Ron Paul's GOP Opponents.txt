
    
  Recent attacks on Mitt Romney "go against the core of our political philosophy."

LAKE JACKSON, Texas - In response to comments against Mitt Romney made by Congressman Ron Paul's Republican rivals for the GOP nomination, the Ron Paul 2012 Presidential campaign released the following statement.
Below please find comments from U.S. Senator Rand Paul (R-KY):
"I am disturbed by recent developments in the Presidential race.
"Republicans are attacking Governor Romney for his successful private sector business career.
"These attacks, using the class warfare language of the far left and the demagoguery of Chicago politics, go against the core of our political philosophy.
"Republicans are proud of hard work and success. We encourage job creators. We applaud success in capitalism."
