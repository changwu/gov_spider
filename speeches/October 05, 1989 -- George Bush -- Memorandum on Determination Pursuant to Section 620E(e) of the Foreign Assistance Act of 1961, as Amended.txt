
    Presidential Determination No. 90-1
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to Section 620E(e) of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended, 22 U.S.C. 2375(e), I hereby certify that Pakistan does not possess a nuclear explosive device and that the proposed United States assistance program will reduce significantly the risk that Pakistan will possess a nuclear explosive device.
You are authorized and directed to publish this determination and certification in the Federal Register.
GEORGE BUSH
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, October 5, 1989.
