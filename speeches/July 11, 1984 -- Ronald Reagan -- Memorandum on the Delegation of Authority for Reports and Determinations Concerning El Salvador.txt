
    Memorandum for the Secretary of StateSubject: Delegation of Authority for Reports and Determinations Concerning El Salvador
By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and statutes of the United States of America, including Section 621 of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended, and Section 301 of Title 3 of the United States Code, I hereby delegate to you the functions conferred upon me by the Joint Resolution "Making an urgent supplemental appropriation for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1984, for the Department of Agriculture" (Public Law 98-332), insofar as they relate to El Salvador in an unnumbered paragraph entitled "Military Assistance" and to Section 108.
This memorandum shall be published in the Federal Register.
RONALD REAGAN
[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 1:52 p.m., July 13, 1984]
