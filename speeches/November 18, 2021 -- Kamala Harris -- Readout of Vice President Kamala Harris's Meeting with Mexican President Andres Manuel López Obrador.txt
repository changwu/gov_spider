
    Vice President Kamala Harris met today with Mexican President Andres Manuel López Obrador on the margins of the North American Leaders Summit. The two reviewed progress in the U.S.-Mexico bilateral relationship, including our shared economic and security priorities, working together on pandemic recovery, and cooperation on space. Vice President Harris and President López Obrador agreed to continue working together to address the root causes of migration from Central America and the need for a regional approach to migration in the Western Hemisphere.
