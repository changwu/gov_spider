
    PARKVILLE, MO -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Missouri Veterans for McCain will hold a press conference on Tuesday, July 22nd at 10:30 a.m. CDT. This press conference occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across the country.
