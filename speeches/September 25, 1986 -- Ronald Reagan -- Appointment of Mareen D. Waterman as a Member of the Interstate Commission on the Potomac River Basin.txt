
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Mareen D. Waterman to be a member of the Interstate Commission on the Potomac River Basin. He would succeed John M. Brennan.
Mr. Waterman is currently president and general manager of the Waterman Realty Co. in Chester, MD. Previously, he was owner/operator of the Grenadier Realty Co., in Maryland from 1962 to 1975. From 1958 to 1965, Mr. Waterman was vice president and general manager, Green Acres Homes, Inc., Annapolis, MD.
He attended Johns Hopkins University and Washington College. Mr. Waterman is married, has four children, and resides in Queenstown, MD. He was born July 22, 1933, in Agana, Guam.
