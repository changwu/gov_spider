
    The President today announced the appointment of two persons as members of the Board of Visitors to the United States Naval Academy. They are:
Anthony J. Celebrezze, Jr., secretary of state of Ohio, a former Ohio State senator, and a 1963 graduate of the Naval Academy; and Salvatore R. Gerbasi, an attorney in private practice in Nassau County, N.Y., and village justice of the Incorporated Village of Munsey Park.
