
    The Rudy Giuliani Presidential Committee today announced the Mayor's Delaware leadership team. The team will be chaired by Louis Freeh, former Director of the FBI, and co-chaired by Republican National Committeewoman Priscilla Rakestraw, businessman Frank Ursomarso, and Delaware Republican Party Secretary Catherine Murray. They join more than 30 other Delaware leaders supporting Rudy.
"Rudy's leadership team in Delaware demonstrates our strong support in the state," said Giuliani's Campaign Manager Michael DuHaime. "They bring incredible knowledge and experience to the campaign, which will help us continue building our grassroots support. The Mayor is proud to have them on board."
