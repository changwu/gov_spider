
    The Rudy Giuliani Presidential Committee today announced endorsements for Mayor Giuliani in Illinois' Kankakee, Will and DuPage Counties.
Mayor Giuliani's endorsements include several local leaders including Kankakee County State's Attorney Jamie Boyd, Kankakee County Sheriff Tim Bukowski, Kankakee County Republican Chairman Darrel Smith, Naperville Township Republican Chairman Fred Spitzzeri, DuPage Township Republican Chairman Bill Mayer, and Troy Township Republican Chairman George Muentnich.
"Rudy is proving to be the Republican candidate best positioned to win in Illinois and make the state competitive in the general election," said Giuliani's State Chairman and Illinois State Representative Tom Cross. "It is important to have local and grassroots support on the county level to turn out supporters and that is exactly what Rudy's campaign has been able to accomplish in counties throughout the state."
Mayor Giuliani has already announced overwhelming support from Republican leaders in Will, Kankakee, Lake and DuPage Counties including State Representative and Deputy Republican Leader Brent Hassert, State Representative Mark Beaubien, State Representative Ed Sullivan, Lake County Republican Chairman Dan Venturi, DuPage County State's Attorney Joe Birkett, DuPage County Sheriff John Zaruba, State Representatives Bob Biggins, State Representative Franco Coladipietro, State Representative Dennis Reboletti, Will County Recorder of Deeds Laurie McPhillips, and Will County Board Member Debbie Rozak.
