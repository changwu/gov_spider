
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Julius Berman to be a member of the Commission for the Preservation of America's Heritage Abroad for a term of 2 years. This is a new position.
Since 1960 Mr. Berman has been a partner with Kaye, Scholer, Fierman, Hays &
Handler, a law firm in New York, NY. He graduated from Yeshiva University (B.A., 1956) and New York University (LL.B., 1960). He was born on August 2, 1935, in Dukst, Poland. He is married, has three children, and currently resides in Forest Hills, NY.
