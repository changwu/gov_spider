President George W. Bush today announced his intention to nominate three individuals to serve in his administration.

Mr. Stallwood was the Town Administrator for the Town of Lincoln, Rhode Island from 1973 to 2001. He owned Stallwood Insurance Agency and Real Estate from 1964 to 1973, following four years of service in the United States Air Force. Stallwood has an Associate's degree from Johnson & Wales Junior College in Providence, Rhode Island.
