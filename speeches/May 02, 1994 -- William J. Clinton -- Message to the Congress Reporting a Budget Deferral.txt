
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one revised deferral of budget authority, totaling $7.3 million.
The deferral affects the Department of Health and Human Services. The details of the revised deferral is contained in the attached report.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, May 2, 1994.
