
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Vilnius, Lithuania to attend the Vilnius Energy Security Conference on October 10-11, 2007.
The Honorable Jeffrey Clay Sell, Deputy Secretary of Energy, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable John A. Cloud, United States Ambassador to the Republic of Lithuania;
The Honorable Karen Alderman Harbert, Assistant Secretary of Energy (International Affairs and Domestic Policy).
