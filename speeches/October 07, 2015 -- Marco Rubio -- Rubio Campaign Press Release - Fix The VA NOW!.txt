
    
The VA has failed our veterans, and is continuing to fail them every day. That's why Marco led the fight in the Senate last year to pass the VA Management Accountability Act. It was based on a pretty simple premise from the private sector — if someone isn't doing their job at the VA, they should be fired.
Nothing could be simpler. We need to make sure those directly responsible for the terrible performance of the VA can be held accountable.
