
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith propose rescission of $126.2 million appropriated for the Patrol Hydrofoil Missile program of the Department of Defense. In addition, I am reporting a deferral of $31.8 million for the Energy Research and Development Administration, Clinch River Breeder Reactor project, and revisions to two deferrals previously transmitted.
The details of the proposed rescission and the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
JIMMY CARTER
The White House,
May 18, 1977.
