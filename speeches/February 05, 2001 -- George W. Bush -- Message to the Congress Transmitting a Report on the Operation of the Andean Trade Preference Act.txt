
    To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 203(f) of the Andean Trade Preference Act (APTA) of 1991, as amended (19 U.S.C. 3201 et seq.), I transmit herewith the third report to the Congress on the Operation of the Andean Trade Preference Act.
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, February 5, 2001.
