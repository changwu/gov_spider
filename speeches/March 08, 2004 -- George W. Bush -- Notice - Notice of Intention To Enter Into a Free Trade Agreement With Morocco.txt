
    Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of the Trade Act of 2002, I have notified the Congress of my intention to enter into a free trade agreement with the Kingdom of Morocco.
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of that Act, this notice shall be published in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush
The White House,
March 8, 2004. [Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 8:45 a.m., March 9, 2004]
