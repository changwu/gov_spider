
    [Released October 18, 1931. Dated October 16, 1931]
My dear Mr. Peters:
Please present to the members of the German Evangelical Church of Brooklyn, my hearty congratulations on the ninetieth anniversary of the establishment of the church. I wish it all success in continuing its contribution to the spiritual advancement of the community.
Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Rev. Herbert H. Peters, Pastor, German Evangelical Church, 61 Schermerhorn Street, Brooklyn, N.Y.]
