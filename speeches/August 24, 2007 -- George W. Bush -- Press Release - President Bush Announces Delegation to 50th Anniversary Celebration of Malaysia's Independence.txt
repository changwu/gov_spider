
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the 50th Anniversary Celebration of Malaysia's Independence in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on August 31, 2007.
The Honorable Jeffrey Clay Sell, Deputy Secretary of Energy, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Christopher J. LaFleur, United States Ambassador to Malaysia
The Honorable Karan K. Bhatia, Deputy United States Trade Representative
The Honorable Talal Eid, Member of the United States Commission on International Religious Freedom
