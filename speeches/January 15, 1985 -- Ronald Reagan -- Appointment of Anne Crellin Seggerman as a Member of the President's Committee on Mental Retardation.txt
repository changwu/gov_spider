
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Anne Crellin Seggerman to be a member of the President's Committee on Mental Retardation for a term expiring May 11, 1987. This is a reappointment,
She is president and founder of the Fourth World Foundation, Inc., in Fairfield, CT. She is also chairman of the board and founder of the Huxley Institute for Biosocial Research, Fairfield County chapter. She is a member of the board of directors of the Easter Seal Rehabilitation Center in Fairfield, CT.
She is married, has six children, and resides in Fairfield, CT. She was born May 13, 1931, in Los Angeles, CA.
