
    Cook Will Serve As Co-Chair Of Missouri Campaign
The Clinton Campaign today announced the endorsement of former Missouri Secretary of State Rebecca "Bekki" Cook and named her a Co-Chair of Hillary's Missouri campaign.
"Hillary Clinton has a 35-year record fighting for change, and she has the strength and experience to hit the ground running on her first day in the White House," Cook said.
Cook served as Secretary of State from 1994 to 2001. Prior to her tenure as Secretary of State, Cook served on the Missouri State Board of Education from 1990 to 1994.
She currently practices law at Cook & Fort LLC in Cape Girardeau.
"I am honored that Bekki will help lead our efforts in Missouri," Clinton. "With her help, we'll bring our message of change across Missouri and the nation."
Cook joins Congressman Emanuel Cleaver II as a Missouri Co-Chair.
