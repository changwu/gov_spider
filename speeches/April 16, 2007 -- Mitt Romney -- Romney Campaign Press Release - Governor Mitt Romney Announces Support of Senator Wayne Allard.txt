"Governor Romney will bring a much needed breath of fresh air to Washington, something the country really desires."
Background On Senator Wayne Allard:
Serving His Eleventh Year In The United States Senate, Senator Allard Is A Conservative Leader In Washington. Throughout his career, Senator Allard has been committed to cutting taxes, reducing the deficit, returning power to state and local governments and assuring American security. He has been a leader in promoting traditional marriage and sponsored the Marriage Protection Amendment that would define marriage as a union between a man and a woman. He is a member of the Appropriations, Budget, Health, Education, Labor and Pensions, Banking, and Housing and Urban Affairs Committees. Senator Allard is also founder of the Senate Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucus. He is currently the Chairman of the Senate Space Caucus. He formerly served in the U.S. House of Representatives, the Colorado State Senate and is a trained veterinarian.
