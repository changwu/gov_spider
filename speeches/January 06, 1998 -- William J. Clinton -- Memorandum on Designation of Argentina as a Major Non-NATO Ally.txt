Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject: Designation of Argentina as a Major Non-NATO Ally
I hereby designate the Republic of Argentina a major non-NATO ally of the United States pursuant to section 517 of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended, for the purposes of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended, and the Arms Export Control Act.
You are authorized and directed to publish this determination in the Federal Register.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, January 6, 1998.
