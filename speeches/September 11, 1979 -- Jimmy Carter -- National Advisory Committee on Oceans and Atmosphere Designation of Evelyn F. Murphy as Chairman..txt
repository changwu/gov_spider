
    The President today announced that he has designated Evelyn F. Murphy to be Chairman of the National Advisory Committee on Oceans and Atmosphere (NACOA).
Murphy has been a member and Vice Chairman of NACOA since 1978. She replaces the late Donald L. McKernan as Chairman. Murphy is a fellow at the Institute of Politics of Harvard's Kennedy School of Government. She is a former secretary of environmental affairs of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.
