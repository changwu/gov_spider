
    The President today announced his intention to appoint the following individuals to be members of the Commission on Education of the Deaf. These are new positions.
Henry Klopping, of California. Dr. Klopping is superintendent, California School for the Deaf. He was born August 6, 1941, in Omaha, NE, and resides in Fremont, CA.
Gary W. Olsen, of Indiana. Mr. Olsen is executive director, National Association of the Deaf in Indianapolis. He was born May 7, 1941, in Grand Island, NE, and resides in Indianapolis, IN.
Sharon J. Speck, of Ohio. Mrs. Speck has been a staff nurse at Muskingum College Health Center in New Concord, OH, where she currently resides. She was born April 28, 1938.
