In-town pool report #6 - POTUS confirms Texas trip
POTUS commented on the increased death toll after the Uvalde, Texas shooting.
He confirmed FLOTUS preview of an upcoming trip to the grieving community: "I will be traveling to Texas in the coming days."
He also called on the Senate to confirm his ATF director nominee, Steve Dettelbach.



May 25, 202216:49

In-town pool report #7 - Remarks conclude
POTUS ended his remarks at 4:38 p.m.
He then made his way over to the signing desk and shook hands with Sens. Cory Booker and Dick Durbin, as well as Rep. Karen Bass.
He also greeted George Floyd's daughter, Gianna, saying "you're getting so big." Gianna sat down at the desk and he recounted how she once told him her daddy was going to change the world.
POTUS went on to chat with Rep. Sheila Jackson Lee, while VP spoke with Gianna.
POTUS is currently greeting other guests.
