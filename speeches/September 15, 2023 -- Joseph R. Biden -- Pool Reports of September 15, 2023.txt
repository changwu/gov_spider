In-town pool report #10: week ahead
Week Ahead 9/15 - 9/24
This evening, the President will travel to Wilmington, Delaware where he will remain until Sunday.
On Sunday, the President will return to the White House from Wilmington, Delaware.
In the evening, the President will depart the White House en route to New York, New York.
On Monday, the President will participate in two campaign receptions in New York City.
On Tuesday, The President will deliver remarks before the 78th Session of the United Nations General Assembly in New York, New York.
After his remarks, the President will participate in a bilateral meeting with United Nations Secretary-General António Guterres.
Following that, the President will host a meeting of the Central Asia 5 + 1.
In the evening, the President and the First Lady will host the Leader's Reception at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
On Wednesday, the President will participate in a bilateral meeting with Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu of Israel.
In the afternoon, The President will participate in a bilateral meeting with President Lula da Silva of Brazil.
The President will participate in a labor-focused event with President Lula da Silva of Brazil.
Later, the President will participate in two campaign receptions.
In the evening, the President will return to the White House.
On Thursday, the President will host a bilateral meeting with President Volodomyr Zelenskyy of Ukraine at the White House.
In the evening, the President will deliver remarks at the Congressional Hispanic Caucus Institute 46th Annual Gala.
On Saturday evening, the President will deliver remarks at the 2023 Phoenix Awards Dinner. The Vice President will participate and the Second Gentleman will attend.
