
    LAS VEGAS, NV — As momentum continues to build in Nevada around Senator Amy Klobuchar's candidacy, Amy For America released a Spanish language television and radio ad now airing in Nevada.
'Bienestar' started airing on cable in the Las Vegas and Reno media markets on February 15, the first day of Nevada's four-day early voting period. The radio version of the ad is airing in the Las Vegas market. The ads will run through the Nevada Caucuses on February 22.
'Bienestar' can be viewed here.

'Bienestar' English Transcript:
NARRATOR: We Nevada Democrats must elect a candidate who can actually defeat Donald
Trump. And that person is Amy Klobuchar. Amy will bring back to the White House the respect it deserves, will fight to give us better healthcare, better prices on our prescription medicines,
and the best education for our loved ones. Amy knows what's important: our well-being. This Saturday, we must vote for Amy Klobuchar. She is our best candidate.
KLOBUCHAR: I'm Amy Klobuchar and I approve this message.
