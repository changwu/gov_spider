
    The President today announced the appointment of two persons as members of the Board of Visitors to the U.S. Air Force Academy for terms expiring December 30, 1980. They are:
Bruce G. Sundlun, of Providence, R.I., president and chief executive officer of the Outlet Company in Providence, and retired colonel in the U.S. Air Force Reserve.
Sheila Widnall, of Lexington, Mass., associate professor of aeronautical engineering at Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
