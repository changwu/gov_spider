
    ALASKA
By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of March 12, 1914, 38 Stat. 305, 307, it is ordered (1) that Executive Order No. 1967-A of June 23, 1914, withdrawing certain lands in Alaska, be, and it is hereby, revoked as to the tract of land identified as Survey No. 2158, containing 26.16 acres, and (2) that the said tract be, and it is hereby, withdrawn and reserved for town-site entry under the act of March 3, 1891, 26 Stat. 1095, 1099.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,March 30, 1936.
