
    By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me By the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369,.37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5603, of April 20, 1931, withdrawing, together with other lands, public lands in T. 43 N., R. 84 W. of the sixth principal meridian, Wyoming, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked as to the said township.
This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of said township.
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
The White House,January 15, 1936.
