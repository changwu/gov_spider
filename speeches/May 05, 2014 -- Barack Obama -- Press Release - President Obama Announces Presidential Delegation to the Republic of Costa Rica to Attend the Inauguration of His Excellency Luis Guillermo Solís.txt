
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Luis Guillermo Solís, President-elect of the Republic of Costa Rica on May 8, 2014.
The Honorable Gina McCarthy, Administrator, Environmental Protection Agency, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
Mr. Gonzalo R. Gallegos, United States Chargé d'Affaires, a.i., to the Republic of Costa Rica, Department of State
Mr. Francisco Palmieri, Deputy Assistant Secretary of State for Central America and the Caribbean
