
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one new deferral of budget authority totaling $50,000,000 and one revision to a previously reported deferral, increasing the amount deferred by $1,498,389.
The deferrals affect the Department of Energy and the Department of the Treasury.
The details of each deferral are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
March 9, 1983.
