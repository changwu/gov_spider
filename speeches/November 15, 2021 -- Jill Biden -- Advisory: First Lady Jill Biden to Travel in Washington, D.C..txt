
    On Wednesday, November 17th, First Lady Jill Biden will travel in Washington, D.C. for two events. In the morning, the First Lady will deliver remarks to the members of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce Foundation's Hiring Our Heroes Veteran Employment Advisory Council at the U.S. Chamber of Commerce. This event will be pooled press.
In the afternoon, the First Lady will continue a nationwide effort urging parents and guardians to vaccinate kids ages 5-11. The First Lady, U.S. Surgeon General Dr. Vivek Murthy, Washington Mystics' Alysha Clark, and Washington Wizards' Thomas Bryant will visit a pediatric COVID-19 vaccination clinic at the Children's National THEARC. This event will be pooled press.
