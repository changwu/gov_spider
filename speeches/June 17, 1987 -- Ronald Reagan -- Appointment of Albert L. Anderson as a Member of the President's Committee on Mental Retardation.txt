
    The President today announced his intention to appoint Albert L. Anderson to be a member of the President's Committee on Mental Retardation for a term expiring May 11, 1990. This is a reappointment.
Since 1947 Dr. Anderson has been a dentist and pedodontics specialist in San Diego, CA. He was the founding member of the San Diego Stadium Authority and served as chairman from 1981 to 1982. Dr. Anderson has served as dental chief of staff at the Children's Hospital and the University Hospital in San Diego.
Dr. Anderson graduated from the University of the Redlands (B.S., 1944) and the University of Southern California (D.D.S., 1947). He was born January 17, 1923, in Sanger, CA. Dr. Anderson is married, has three children, and resides in San Diego, CA.
