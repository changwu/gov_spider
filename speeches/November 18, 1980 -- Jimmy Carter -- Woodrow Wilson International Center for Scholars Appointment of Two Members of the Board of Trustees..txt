
    The President today announced the appointment of two persons as members of the Board of Trustees of the Woodrow Wilson International Center for Scholars. They are:
JESSE H. OPPENHEIMER, of San Antonio, Tex., an attorney, member of the Chancellors Council of the University of Texas, and a national committee member of the Harvard University Center for Jewish Studies (reappointment);
KENNETH B. CLARK, of Hastings-on-Hudson, N.Y., president of a personnel consulting firm, distinguished professor of psychology emeritus of the City College of the City University of New York, and author of several books and articles.
