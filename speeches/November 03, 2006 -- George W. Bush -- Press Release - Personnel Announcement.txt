
    President George W. Bush announced today that he has named two individuals to serve in his Administration:
The President has named Craig M. Albright to be Special Assistant to the President for Legislative Affairs. Mr. Albright recently served as Deputy Assistant to the Vice President for Legislative Affairs. He received his bachelor's degree from Michigan State University.
The President has named Ross Kyle to be Special Assistant to the President for Cabinet Liaison. Mr. Kyle recently served as Acting Director of the Office of Cabinet Liaison. He attended the University of New Hampshire.
