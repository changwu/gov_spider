
    Momentum for Hillary Continues to Build on Eve of Democratic Presidential Debate
CLEVELAND, OH - On the eve of Ohio's Democratic Presidential debate, a dozen Cleveland area officials and community leaders endorsed Hillary, citing her ability to strengthen Ohio's economy and rebuild the middle class.
"In this crucial time in our country we need someone with a proven track record with the knowledge and the grit to help America push forward again - Hillary is that leader," said Lorain Mayor Anthony Krasienko.
"I am honored to have the support of these distinguished leaders from Cleveland, who share my views that we need to jump-start our economy, provide jobs with good wages, end the foreclosure crisis and ensure that all families have access to quality, affordable healthcare," said Clinton.
