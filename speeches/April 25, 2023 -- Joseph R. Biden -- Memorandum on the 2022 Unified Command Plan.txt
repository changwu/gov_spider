
    Memorandum for the Secretary of Defense 
Subject: 2022 Unified Command Plan
Pursuant to my authority as Commander in Chief, I hereby approve and direct the implementation of the 2022 Unified Command Plan.
Consistent with section 161(b)(2) of title 10, United States Code, and section 301 of title 3, United States Code, you are directed to notify the Congress on my behalf.
You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the Federal Register.
JOSEPH R. BIDEN, JR.
