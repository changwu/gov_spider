
    Charleston, WV - The Clinton Campaign will hold an open house at its headquarters in Charleston with Brigadier General Jack Yeager at 3 p.m. this Saturday, April 19, 2008. The headquarters is located at 191 Summers Street, Charleston, WV 25301. This event is free and open to the public.
WHAT: Open House at Clinton Campaign West Virginia Headquarters with Brigadier General Jack Yeager. General Yeager is the former Assistant Adjutant General of West Virginia and Commander of the Army National Guard. He also serves as national co-chair of Hillary Clinton for President. West Virginians interested in learning more about the campaign are encouraged to attend.
WHEN: 3 p.m. Saturday, April 19, 2008
WHERE: Clinton Campaign West Virginia Headquarters:
191 Summers Street
Charleston, WV
