
    The President today announced his intention to nominate A. C. Arterbery to be a member of the Board of Directors of the African Development Foundation for a term of 6 years. This is a new position.
He has been with TRW Systems and Energy for 22 years and presently serves as manager of information resources. Previously he was project manager of a fiber optics technology project. In recent years he helped establish and is now a board member of the International Technical Assistance Corp.
He graduated from California State University at Los Angeles and attended Missouri University Law School and the University of Southern California Graduate School of Business. He is married, has one child, and resides in Los Angeles, Calif. He was born August 4, 1930, in Bristow, Okla.
