
    On Monday, March 6, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will travel to Golden, Colorado to visit the National Renewable Energy Laboratory. During his visit, the Second Gentleman will tour the facility and meet with laboratory employees and college students. He will highlight the Biden-Harris Administration's investments in clean energy and commitment to increasing opportunities for women in STEM fields. This tour will be open to pre-credentialed media.
