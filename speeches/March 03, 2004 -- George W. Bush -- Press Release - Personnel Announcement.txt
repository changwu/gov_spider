
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the State Funeral of His Excellency Boris Trajkovski, President of Macedonia, Friday, March 5, 2004.
The Honorable Anthony Principi, Secretary of Veterans Affairs, will serve as head of the delegation.
