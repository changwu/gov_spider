
    U.S. Sen. Bernie Sanders will return to New Hampshire on Saturday for a house party in Manchester and will address the New Hampshire AFL-CIO Convention in North Conway. Sanders looks forward to discussing some of the most important economic issues facing the country and hearing from the people of New Hampshire.
This will be Sanders' tenth trip to New Hampshire since 2014.
Saturday, May 2
10 am    House party at the home of Elizabeth Ropp, 851 Beech St. in Manchester, New Hampshire
1:30 pm  Speech at the New Hampshire AFL- CIO Convention at the White Mountain Hotel and Resort, 2560 West Side Road, in North Conway, New Hampshire
