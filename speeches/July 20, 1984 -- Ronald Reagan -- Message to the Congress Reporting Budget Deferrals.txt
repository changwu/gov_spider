
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one new deferral of budget authority for $8,006,000 and six revised deferrals of budget authority which now total $68,528,548. The deferrals affect the Departments of Energy, and Health and Human Services.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN
The White House,
July 20, 1984.
