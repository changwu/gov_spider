
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Marjorie S. Holt to be a member of the General Advisory Committee of the United States Arms Control and Disarmament Agency. She would succeed Donald Rumsfeld.
Mrs. Holt served in the United States House of Representatives, 1972-1986.
Mrs. Holt graduated from Jacksonville University (B.A., 1945) and the University of Florida (J.D., 1949). She was born September 17, 1920, in Birmingham, AL. Mrs. Holt is married, has three children, and resides in Severna Park, MD.
