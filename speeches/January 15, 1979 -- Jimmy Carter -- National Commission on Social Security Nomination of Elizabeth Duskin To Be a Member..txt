
    The President today announced that he will nominate Elizabeth Duskin, of Chevy Chase, Md., to be a member of the National Commission on Social Security.
Duskin, 43, is director of research for the National Council of Senior Citizens. She has also served as an economic consultant to the Federal Council on Aging, where she prepared congressionally mandated studies. Her professional background has emphasized programs and policies affecting U.S. income distribution, particularly social and private insurance policies, income-conditioned programs, and health-related financing mechanisms.
