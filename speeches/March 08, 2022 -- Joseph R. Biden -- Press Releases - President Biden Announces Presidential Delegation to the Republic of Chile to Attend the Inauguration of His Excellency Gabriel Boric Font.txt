
    President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Valparaíso, Chile to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Gabriel Boric Font as President of the Republic of Chile on March 11, 2022.
The Honorable Isabella Casillas Guzman, Administrator of the U.S. Small Business Administration, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
Mr. Richard Glenn, Chargé d'Affaires, a.i., U.S. Embassy Santiago
The Honorable Brian A. Nichols, Assistant Secretary for Western Hemisphere Affairs, U.S. Department of State
Ms. Isabel Allende, Award-winning Chilean-American Author and Chilean National Literature Prize Recipient
