
    APEC Summit
Q. Mr. President, are you disappointed at the lack of consensus on free trade at APEC?
The President. I might have more to say about that before we go. Don't be too discouraged.
2000 Presidential Election
Q. How about the Florida court ruling, sir?
The President. I'm over here, so I'm not sure what it means. It's obviously going to have to be interpreted now, since the two sides have a different reading on it.
Q. How about the secretary of state denying the hand recount?
The President. I'm over here doing this work; I don't think I should get involved in that. The American people deserve a full and fair count, and I hope the process will produce it. And they're over there debating it in the appropriate way. I shouldn't be involved in that.
Q. Should Gore and Bush meet?
The President. What?

The President. I don't think I should be involved in that.
