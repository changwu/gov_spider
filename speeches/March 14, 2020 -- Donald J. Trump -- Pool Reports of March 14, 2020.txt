Pool Report #11: Johnson readout
Judd Deere passes along the following statement:
"Today, President Donald J. Trump spoke with Prime Minister Boris Johnson of the United Kingdom. The President discussed actions taken to stop the COVID-19 pandemic, including today's announcement of restrictions, with limited exception, on the travel of foreign nationals who have recently been in the United Kingdom to the United States. The two leaders also discussed next week's videoconference on the COVID-19 pandemic with G7 leaders. President Trump and Prime Minister Johnson also agreed to continue close cooperation to counter threats from the Iranian regime and its proxies in Iraq."Francesca Chambers
