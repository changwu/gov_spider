
    3 local leaders endorse Chris Christie
Dan Tuohy
Union Leader
N.J. Gov. Chris Christie has won endorsements from three notable local government leaders.
Concord City Councilor Dan St. Hilaire, Rockingham County Commission Chairman Tom Tombarello, and Rockingham County Commissioner Kevin Coyle.
St. Hilaire is a former New Hampshire Executive Councilor and former Merrimack County Attorney who served as an alternate delegate for Sen. John McCain, R-Ariz., at the 2008 Republican National Convention.
