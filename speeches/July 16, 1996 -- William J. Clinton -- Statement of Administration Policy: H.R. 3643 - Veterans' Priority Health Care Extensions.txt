
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICYThis Statement Has Been Coordinated by OMB with the Appropriate Agencies
(House)(Hutchinson (R) AR and 16 cosponsors)
The Administration supports House passage of H.R. 3643, but will work in the Senate to modify provisions that would curtail the Department of Veterans Affairs' authority to provide priority health care to veterans with disorders that may be associated with exposure to Agent Orange and ionizing radiation.
