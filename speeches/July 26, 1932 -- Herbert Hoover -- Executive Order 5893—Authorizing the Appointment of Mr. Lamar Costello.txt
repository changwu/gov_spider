
    Mr. Lamar Costello may be appointed to the position of Supervisor of Detentions at $3,000 per annum in the Immigration Service at Ellis Island, N. Y., without regard to the requirements of the civil-service rules. On account of his military training and other qualifications, Mr. Costello is particularly fitted for this position.
This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Labor.
HERBERT HOOVER
The White House,July 26, 1932.
