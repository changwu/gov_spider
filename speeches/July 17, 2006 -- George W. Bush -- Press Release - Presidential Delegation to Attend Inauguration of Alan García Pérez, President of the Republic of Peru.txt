
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Lima, Peru to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Alan García Pérez, President of the Republic of Peru on July 28, 2006.
The Honorable Carlos M. Gutierrez, Secretary of Commerce, will lead the delegation.
Accompanying member of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable James Curtis Struble, United States Ambassador to the Republic of Peru
