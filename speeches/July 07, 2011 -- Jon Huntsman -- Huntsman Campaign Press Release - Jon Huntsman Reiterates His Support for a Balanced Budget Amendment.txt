
    This morning, Jon reiterated his support for a balanced budget amendment on Tampa Bay's WFLA Radio, and talked about the tough choices that must be made to rein in government spending. 
Jon has been very clear that one of his top priorities as President would be passing a balanced budget amendment, to force the government to only spend what it takes in.
"We're going to have to fight for a balanced budget amendment," Jon recently said. "It keeps everybody honest. It's the best safeguard imaginable."
