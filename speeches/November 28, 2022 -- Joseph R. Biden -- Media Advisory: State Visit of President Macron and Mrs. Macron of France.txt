
    President Joe Biden and First Lady Jill Biden will host President Emmanuel Macron and Mrs. Brigitte Macron of France for a State Visit to the United States, which will include a state dinner, on December 1, 2022. This will be the first State Visit of the Biden-Harris Administration. It will underscore the deep and enduring relationship between the United States and France, our oldest ally, that is founded on our shared democratic values, economic ties, and defense and security cooperation. The leaders will discuss our continued close partnership on shared global challenges and areas of bilateral interest.
The President, the First Lady, the Vice President, and the Second Gentleman will greet President Emmanuel Macron and Mrs. Brigitte Macron of France for the Official Arrival Ceremony. This event will be open press. [RSVP here by Wednesday, November 30th at 9:00 AM EST]
The President will hold a bilateral meeting with President Emmanuel Macron of France. This event will be covered by the in-house restricted pool.
The President will host a joint press conference with President Emmanuel Macron of France. This event will be open to pre-credentialed media. [RSVP here by Wednesday, November 30th at 9:00 AM EST]
The President and the First Lady will greet President Emmanuel Macron and Mrs. Brigitte Macron of France on arrival for the State Dinner. This event will be pooled press.
The President and the First Lady will participate in a photo opportunity with President Emmanuel Macron and Mrs. Brigitte Macron of France at the Grand Staircase on the occasion of the State Dinner. This event will be pooled press.
The President and the First Lady will host President Emmanuel Macron and Mrs. Brigitte Macron of France for a State Dinner. The Vice President and the Second Gentleman will attend. This event will be pooled press.
