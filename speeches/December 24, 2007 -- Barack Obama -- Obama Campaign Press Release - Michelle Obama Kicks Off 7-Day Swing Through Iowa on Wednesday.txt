
    Des Moines, IA -- On Wednesday, December 26th, Michelle Obama will return to Iowa for a 7-day swing through the state. Michelle kicks off her tour Wednesday with stops in Newton, Winterset, and Adel. Thursday, Michelle will continue her trip with events in Osceola, Creston, Mt. Ayr, Bedford, and Red Oak. Michelle will speak with Iowans about Barack Obama's ability to unite our country and bring about change we can believe in.
