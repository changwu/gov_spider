
    On Friday, October 8, 2021, the President signed into law:
S. 189, the "Veterans' Compensation Cost-of-Living Adjustment Act of 2021," which provides for a cost-of-living adjustment (COLA) for veterans' disability compensation and dependency and indemnity compensation beneficiaries equal to the Social Security COLA.
Thank you to Senators Thune, Schatz, Blumenthal, Sinema, Hassan, Tester, Hirono, Murray, Brown, and Sanders and Representatives Luria, Nehls, Takano, and Bost for their leadership.
