
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(Senate)(Pell (D) RI and 18 others)
S. 373 moves in the direction of administration priorities in certain program areas, particularly in the Chapter 1 program for educationally disadvantaged children and in bilingual education. The bill contains several objectionable provisions, however, and fails to include some of the administration's recommendations.
