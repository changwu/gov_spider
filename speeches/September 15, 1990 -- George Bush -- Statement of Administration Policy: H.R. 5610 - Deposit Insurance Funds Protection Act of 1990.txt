
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Gonzalez (D) Texas arid Wylie (R) Ohio)
The Administration supports legislation that would provide immediately more flexibility' to the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) to raise assessments on commercial banks to replenish the Bank Insurance Fund. The Administration will send a legislative proposal to Congress in the next few days.
The Administration supports House passage of H.R. 5610, but will seek amendments in the Senate to conform to the Administration proposal which will provide more flexibility to the FDIC in order to strengthen the Barik Insurance Fund.
