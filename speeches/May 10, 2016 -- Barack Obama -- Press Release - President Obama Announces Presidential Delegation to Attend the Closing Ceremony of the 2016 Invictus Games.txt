
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Attend the Closing Ceremony of the 2016 Invictus Games in Orlando, Florida.
Dr. Jill Biden will lead the delegation as part of the 5th Anniversary of Joining Forces. Vice President Joe Biden will join the delegation to attend the games.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
Mrs. Betty Easley, Caregiver Fellow, Elizabeth Dole Foundation
Mr. Noah Galloway, Iraq War Veteran and founder of the No Excuses Charitable Fund
Mr. William McNulty, Co-Founder and CEO of Team Rubicon Global, Ltd.
Mr. Brad Snyder, retired U.S. Navy Lieutenant and 2012 Paralympic Gold Medalist, Swimming
