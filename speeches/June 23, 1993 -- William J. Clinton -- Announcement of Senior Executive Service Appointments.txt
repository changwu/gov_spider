
    The President today announced the appointment of a total of 26 Senior Executive Service officials at Departments and Agencies across the Government.
"We are continuing to move forward with the process of filling all of the positions in the Federal Government," said the President. "I continue to be pleased with the excellence of our appointees, the work that is being done across the Government, and the diversity of the administration we are putting together."
The appointees, who do not need to be confirmed by the Senate, are:
