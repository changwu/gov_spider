
    The President today announced the appointment of Calvin W. Carter and Richard H. Jenrette as members of the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation for terms expiring February 28, 1982.
Carter, 43, of Tampa, Fla., is president and chief executive officer of the O. H. Carter Co., an insurance company.
Jenrette, 48, of New York City, is also designated Chairman of the Council. He is chairman of the board and chief executive officer of Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette, Inc. He is a trustee of the National Trust for Historic Preservation.
