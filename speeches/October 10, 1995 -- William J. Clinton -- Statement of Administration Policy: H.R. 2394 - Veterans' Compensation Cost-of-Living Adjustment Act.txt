
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Rep. Everett (R) AL and two others)
The Administration strongly supports the cost-of-living adjustment (COLA) for the beneficiaries of veterans' disability compensation and Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC) contained in H.R. 2394.
Pay-As-You-Go Scoring 
H.R. 2394 is subject to the pay-as-you-go requirement of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1990. Estimates of the savings from this bill are currently under development.
