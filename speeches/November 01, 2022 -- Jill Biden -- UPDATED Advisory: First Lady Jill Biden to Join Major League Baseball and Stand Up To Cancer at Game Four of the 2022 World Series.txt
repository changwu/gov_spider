
    On Wednesday, November 2nd, as part of the Biden Administration's Cancer Moonshot, First Lady Jill Biden will attend Game Four of the 2022 World Series at Citizens Bank Park in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Dr. Biden will join players, umpires, coaches, and fans in Major League Baseball's annual Game Four tradition of honoring those affected by cancer as part of the league's support and partnership with Stand Up To Cancer.
The First Lady's visit will be covered by a traveling pool and media previously credentialed with Major League Baseball.
