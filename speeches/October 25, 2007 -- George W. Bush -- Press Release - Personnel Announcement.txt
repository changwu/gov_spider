
    President George W. Bush today named one individual to serve in his Administration.
The President has named Brian V. McCormack to be Deputy Assistant to the President for Strategic Initiatives and External Affairs. Mr. McCormack recently served as Special Assistant to the President and Deputy Director of the Office of Public Liaison. Earlier in his career, he served as Special Assistant to the Under Secretary for Acquisition, Technology and Logistics at the Department of Defense and Special Assistant to Ambassador Paul Bremer. He also previously served as Personal Aide to the Vice President. Mr. McCormack received his bachelor's degree from Boise State University.
