
    First Lady Jill Biden will join President Joe Biden for the G7 Leaders' Summit in Hiroshima, Japan from May 19-21, 2023. The G7 Leaders' Summit will highlight a range of the most pressing global issues, including the G7's unwavering support for Ukraine, addressing the dual food and climate crises, securing inclusive and resilient economic growth, and continuing to lead a clean energy transition at home and for our partners around the world.
