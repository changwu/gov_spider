
    On Tuesday, August 1, 2006, the President signed into law:
H.R. 2977, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Paul Kasten Post Office Building;
H.R. 3440, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Dr. Jose Celso Barbosa Post Office Building;
H.R. 3549, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the William F. Clinger, Jr. Post Office Building;
H.R. 3934, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Gerard A. Fiorenza Post Office Building;
H.R. 4101, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the Lieutenant Michael P. Murphy Post Office Building; and
H.R. 4108, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service as the State Senator Verda Welcome and Dr. Henry Welcome Post Office Building.
