
    On Wednesday, May 17th, First Lady Jill Biden will travel to Bethel, Alaska to highlight the Biden-Harris Administration's historic investments to expand broadband connectivity in Native American communities, including Alaska Native communities in Alaska's Yukon-Kuskokwim Delta. These investments were made possible by the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law and are part of President Biden's Investing in America agenda. Dr. Biden's visit will be the first visit to Bethel, Alaska by a First Lady of the United States.
