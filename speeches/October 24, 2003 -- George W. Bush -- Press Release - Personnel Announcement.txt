
    President George W. Bush has designated a Presidential Delegation to attend the commemoration ceremony of the 20th Anniversary of the restoration of democracy to Grenada, in St. George's, Grenada, October 24-26, 2003.
Head of Delegation The Honorable Otto Reich, the President's Special Envoy for Western Hemisphere Initiatives
Members of the Delegation Ms. Marcia Bernicat, Charge d'Affaires ad Interim, United States Embassy Barbados
Admiral Joseph Metcalf III, Vice Admiral, U.S. Navy Retired
The Honorable Langhorne Anthony Motley, Former Assistant Secretary of State for Western Hemisphere Affairs
Mr. Ken Tomlinson, Chairman, U.S. Broadcasting Board of Governors
