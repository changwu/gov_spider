
    President Donald J. Trump today announced the following White House appointments:
Captain Keith B. Davids will serve as Special Assistant to the President and Deputy Director, White House Military Office. Mr. Davids previously served as Deputy Commander, Naval Special Warfare Command.
Zachary D. Fuentes of Florida will serve as Special Assistant to the President and Assistant to the Chief of Staff. Mr. Fuentes previously served as Military Assistant to the Secretary of Homeland Security.
Kirstjen M. Nielsen of Florida will serve as Assistant to the President and Principal Deputy Chief of Staff. Ms. Nielsen formerly served as Chief of Staff of the Department of Homeland Security.
Robert L. "Bobby" Peede will serve as Deputy Assistant to the President and Director of Presidential Advance. Mr. Peede previously served as Deputy Assistant to the Vice President and Director of Vice Presidential Advance.
Daniel P. Walsh will serve as Deputy Assistant to the President and Director, White House Military Office. Mr. Walsh previously served as Deputy Director, White House Military Office.
