
    The President today announced that he will reappoint Jerome M. Rosow, of New York, as Chairman of the Advisory Committee on Federal Pay. Rosow has served as Chairman of the Committee since 1972.
Rosow, 58, is president and founder of the Work in America Institute. He was Assistant Secretary of Labor for Policy, Evaluation and Research from 1969 to 1971. He is the author of numerous publications and articles on work and workers and has served as an adviser and consultant to numerous Government commissions and panels.
