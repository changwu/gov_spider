
    On Tuesday, June 30th, Vice President Pence will travel to Arizona. There, the Vice President will give remarks at a Faith in America event in Tuscon. Following, the Vice President will meet with Governor Ducey in Yuma on the efforts the state is taking to combat COVID-19. Later that evening, the Vice President will return to Washington, D.C.
