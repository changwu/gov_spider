
    
  Will host caucus-goer meet and greet in Honolulu

HONOLULU, Hawaii — The Ron Paul 2012 Presidential Campaign announced today that Congressman Paul's son, Ronnie Paul, will host a 'Meet and Greet' in Honolulu on Saturday, March 10th. This is the first event announced as part of Mr. Paul's multiday trip to the Aloha State on behalf of his father. Ron Paul 2012 National Campaign Manager John Tate will also be joining Mr. Paul. Details of other events will be released at a later date.
Details of the Honolulu 'Meet and Greet' are as follows. The event is free and open to the public and press. Both Mr. Paul and Mr. Tate will be available for media interviews after the event.
Saturday, March 10, 2012
1:00 p.m.
Meet and Greet
Naupaka I & II
Hilton Alana Waikiki Hotel
