
    PORTLAND, OR -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that former Congressman Denny Smith, Greg Wooldridge and Jake McMichael will participate in the announcement of Oregon Veterans for John McCain on Tuesday, July 22nd at 11:00 a.m. PDT. This event occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across the country.
