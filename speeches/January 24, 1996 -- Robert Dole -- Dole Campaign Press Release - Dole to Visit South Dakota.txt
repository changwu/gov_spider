
    GOP Presidential frontrunner Senator Bob Dole will visit South Dakota on Thursday, January 25, 1996. He will attend a Pennington County community meeting in Rapid City from 11:30am to 12:15pm MST. Senator Dole will address a Joint Session Of the South Dakota State Legislature from 3:00pm to 3:25pm CST and the South Dakota Newspaper Association in Pierre from 3:40pm to 4:30pm CST. Media attending should arrive thirty minutes early for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials.
