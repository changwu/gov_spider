
    DES MOINES, Iowa — U.S. Sen. Bernie Sanders on December 29 returns to Iowa for a three-day swing through the state. The trip kicks off with a town meeting in Muscatine and includes stops in Davenport, Burlington, Keokuk, Ottumwa and Knoxville. Sanders will discuss a wide range of issues including getting big money out of politics, combating climate change and making college affordable.
