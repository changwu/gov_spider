
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
SENT 10/3/94(House)(Oberstar (D) Minnesota and 25 others)
The Administration supports House passage of H.R. 4495.
The Administration is currently reviewing the international legal implications of H.R. 4495's application to flights by foreign aircraft outside of U.S. airspace. Based on the results of that review, the Administration may propose an amendment in the Senate.
