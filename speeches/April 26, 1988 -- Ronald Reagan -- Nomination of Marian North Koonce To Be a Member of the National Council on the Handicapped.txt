
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Marian North Koonce to be a member of the National Council on the Handicapped for a term expiring September 23, 1990. This is a reappointment.
From 1945 to 1980, Mrs. Koonce was an independent operator for Real Estate Management in Santa Barbara, CA.
Mrs. Koonce attended the University of Texas. She was born August 11, 1924, in Yoakum, TX. She is married, has six children, and resides in Santa Barbara, CA.
