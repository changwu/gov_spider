
    The President today announced his intent to nominate Sally Shelton-Colby as the Assistant Administrator of the Bureau for Global Programs, Field Support and Research at the Agency for International Development, U.S. International Development Cooperation Agency.
"I am pleased to nominate Sally Shelton-Colby to the position," the President said, adding, "Her foreign affairs experience and educational background will be a great asset to the Agency for International Development."
