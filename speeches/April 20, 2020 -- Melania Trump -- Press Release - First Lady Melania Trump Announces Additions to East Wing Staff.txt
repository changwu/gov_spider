
    Today, First Lady Melania Trump is announcing the additions of Marcia Lee Kelly and Emma Doyle to the East Wing Staff.
Marcia Lee Kelly will be joining the East Wing as Senior Advisor to the First Lady. Kelly returns to the White House as a special government employee. Previously, Kelly served as the Director of White House Management and Administration and Director of the Office of Administration.
Emma Doyle will be joining the First Lady's Office as Deputy Chief of Staff for Policy. Doyle previously served in the West Wing as the Principal Deputy Chief of Staff.
