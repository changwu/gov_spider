
    Elizabeth Dole, wife of presumptive Republican Presidential nominee Bob Dole, will give the keynote address for the North Carolina GOP State Convention on Friday, June 14, 1996. Bob Dole will also give brief remarks. Media attending the event should arrive an hour early and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials with photo identification.
WHO: Elizabeth Dole and Bob Dole
WHAT: North Carolina GOP State Convention
WHERE: M.C. Benton Jr. Convention and Visitor's Center
301 West 5th Street
Winston-Salem, NC
WHEN: Friday, June 14, 1996 from 6,-00pm to 8:00pm (Event Runs)
News Media wishing to travel with Dole's aircraft charter should contact Steve Duchesne, Press Travel Director, at (202) 414-8069 or by fax at (202)-414-8074.
