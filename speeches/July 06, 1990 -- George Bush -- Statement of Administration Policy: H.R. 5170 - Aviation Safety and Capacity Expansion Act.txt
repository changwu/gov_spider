
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House Rules)(Oberstar (D) MN and 3 others)
The Administration supports House passage of H.R. 5170.
The Administration will seek an amendment in the Senate to ensure adequate coverage of the Federal Government's maximum liability under multi-year contracts. Upon signing such a contract, the Federal Aviation Administration should be required to obligate the first year's contract cost plus the cost of a termination for convenience clause.
