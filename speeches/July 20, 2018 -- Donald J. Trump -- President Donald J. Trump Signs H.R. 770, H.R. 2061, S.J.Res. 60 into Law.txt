
    On Friday, July 20, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 770, the "American Innovation $1 Coin Act," which requires the Department of the Treasury to mint coins in recognition of American innovation and significant innovation and pioneering efforts of individuals or groups from each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, and the United States territories;
H.R. 2061, the "North Korean Human Rights Reauthorization Act of 2017," which reauthorizes and amends the North Korean Human Rights Act of 2004; and
S.J.Res. 60, which reappoints Barbara M. Barrett as a citizen regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution.
