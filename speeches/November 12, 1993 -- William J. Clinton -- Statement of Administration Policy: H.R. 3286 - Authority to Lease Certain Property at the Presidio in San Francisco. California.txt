
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICY
(House)(Pelosi (D) California)
The Administration supports H.R. 3286.
Pay-As-You-Go Scoring
H.R. 3286 affects receipts and direct spending; therefore, it is subject to the pay-as-you-go (PAYGO) requirement of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act (OBRA) of 1990. OMB's preliminary PAYGO estimate is zero.
