
    The President today announced the appointment of Patricia Mack Bryan to be Associate Counsel to the President.
Since June 1987 Ms. Bryan has served in the Office of the Counsel to the President. Prior to her position in the White House, she was Acting Deputy Assistant Attorney General and Special Assistant to the Assistant Attorney General, Office of Legal Policy, Department of Justice, 1983-1987, and an associate with the firm of Jones, Day, Reavis & Pogue, 1980-1983.
Ms. Bryan graduated from Gettysburg College (B.A., 1977) and Harvard Law School (J.D., 1980). She was born July 7, 1955, in Philadelphia, PA.
