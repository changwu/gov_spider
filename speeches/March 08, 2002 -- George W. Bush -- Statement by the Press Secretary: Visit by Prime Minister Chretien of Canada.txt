
    President Bush will meet with Prime Minister Chretien of Canada at the White House on March 14 to review key bilateral issues and planning for the June 2002 G-8 meeting in Kananaskis, Canada.
The President and the Prime Minister will meet in the Oval Office, and continue their discussions over a working dinner in the White House. The President looks forward to the opportunity to review progress in the campaign against terrorism and regional issues of interest to both countries.
