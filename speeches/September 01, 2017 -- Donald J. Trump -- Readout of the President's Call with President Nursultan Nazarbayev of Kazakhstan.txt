
    President Donald J. Trump spoke today with President Nursultan Nazarbayev of Kazakhstan to discuss the two countries' 25-year relationship. President Trump and President Nazarbayev also discussed Kazakhstan's support for President Trump's recently announced South Asia strategy. President Trump expressed appreciation for Kazakhstan's regional and global leadership, including its upcoming tenure as Chair of the United Nations Security Council in January, and congratulated President Nazarbayev on hosting the Astana Expo 2017.
