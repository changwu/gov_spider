
    Senate Majority Leader Bob Dole will visit Iowa on Saturday, February 10, 1996. He will attend events in Indianola, Atlantic and Le Mars. He will be joined by Governor Terry Branstad at the Indianola and Atlantic events and Senator Chuck Grassley at all of the events on Saturday. Media attending should arrive thirty minutes early for set-up and will be asked to provide local or national media credentials.
