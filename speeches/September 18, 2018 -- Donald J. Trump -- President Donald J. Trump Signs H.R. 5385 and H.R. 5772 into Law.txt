
    On Tuesday, September 18, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 5385, the "Dr. Benjy Frances Brooks Children's Hospital GME Support Reauthorization Act of 2018," which extends and reauthorizes appropriations through fiscal year (FY) 2023 for payments to children's hospitals associated with operating approved graduate medical residency training programs; and
H.R. 5772, which designates the J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Courthouse in Amarillo, Texas, as the J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Mary Lou Robinson United States Courthouse.
