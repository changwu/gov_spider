
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICYThis Statement Has Been Coordinated by OMB with the Appropriate Agencies
(House)(Saxton (R) New Jersey and 129 cosponsors)
The Administration strongly supports House passage of H.R. 1965. However, the Administration will seek amendments in the Senate to conform the authorization levels to the President's Budget, which allows for new States and additional estuarine research reserves to receive Federal assistance.
