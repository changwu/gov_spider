
    Des Moines, IA -- U.S. Senator Barack Obama will travel across Iowa on the "Stand for Change" Tour on Wednesday, December 26 through Caucus Day, Thursday, January 3rd. Obama will ask Iowans across the state to "Stand for Change" by caucusing for him on January 3rd and taking the first step towards bringing to Washington, D.C. the kind of change we can believe in.
