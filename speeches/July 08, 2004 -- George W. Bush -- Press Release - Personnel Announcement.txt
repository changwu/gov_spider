
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to attend the funeral services of His Excellency Thomas Klestil, the former President of the Republic of Austria, on July 10, 2004.
The Honorable Arnold Schwarzenegger, Governor of California, will lead the delegation.
The Honorable W.L. Lyons Brown, U.S. Ambassador to Austria and Mrs. Brown will also attend the services.
