
    [Dated June 19, 1975. Released June 20, 1975]
Dear Dean:
I hereby authorize The President Ford Committee to solicit and receive contributions and to incur expenses and to make expenditures to further my nomination for the Office of President of the United States.
Best personal regards,
GERALD R. FORD
[Honorable Dean Burch, Chairman, The President Ford Committee, 1200 18th Street, NW., Washington, D.C. 20036]
