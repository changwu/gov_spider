
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICYThis Statement Has Been Coordinated by OMB with the Appropriate Agencies
(House)(Bliley (R) VA and 241 cosponsors)
The Administration strongly supports the enactment of H.R. 1627 as reported. This bipartisan bill meets the Administration's goals of a strengthened health-based food safety standard; enhanced protection for infants and children; and common-sense reform of regulatory requirements.
