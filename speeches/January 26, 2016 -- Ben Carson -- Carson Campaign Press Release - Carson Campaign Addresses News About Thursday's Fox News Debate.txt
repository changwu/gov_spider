
    Alexandria, Va. - January 26, 2016 - "Dr. Carson is focused on getting his message to the voters and is looking forward to Thursday's debate," said Jason Osborne, Senior Communications Strategist for the Ben Carson for President 2016 campaign. "Dr. Carson is grateful to Fox News for providing this forum as a service to the people of Iowa just prior to their Caucus. Regardless of who is on the stage, Dr. Carson will be speaking directly to the voters and sharing his plans to 'Heal, Inspire and Revive' America."
