
    ALBUQUERQUE, NM -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that it will be launching the New Mexico Veterans for McCain Coalition in Las Cruces on Tuesday, July 22nd at 12:00 p.m. MDT. This announcement occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across Nevada and the country. Congresswoman Heather Wilson (R-NM) and Congressman Steve Pearce (R-NM), both Air Force veterans, will serve as Honorary Members to the Coalition.

LAS CRUCES, NEW MEXICO

WHAT: Lunch and Remarks at American Legion Post 10
