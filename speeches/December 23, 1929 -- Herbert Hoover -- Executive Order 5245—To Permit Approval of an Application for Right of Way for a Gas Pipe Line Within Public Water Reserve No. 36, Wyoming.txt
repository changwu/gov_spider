
    Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Interior, Executive order of August 2, 1916, creating Public Water Reserve No. 36, in Wyoming, is hereby modified to the extent of authorizing him to approve an application filed by the Uinta Pipe Line Company, pursuant to the provisions of the act of Congress approved February 25, 1920 (41 Stat. 437), for right of way for a pipe line to convey gas crossing certain lands within said reserve.
HERBERT HOOVER
The White House,December 23, 1929.
