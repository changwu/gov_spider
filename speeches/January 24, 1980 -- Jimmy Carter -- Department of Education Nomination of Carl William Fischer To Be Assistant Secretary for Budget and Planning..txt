
    The President today announced that he will nominate Carl William Fischer, of College Park, Md., to be Assistant Secretary of Education for Budget and Planning, a new position. Fischer has been Deputy Assistant Secretary of Energy for Planning and Evaluation since 1979.
He was born August 4, 1931, in Pittsburgh, Pa. He received a B.A. from Muskingum College in 1953 and an M.P.A. from Harvard University in 1964.
