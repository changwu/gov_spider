
    The President named his choices today for four positions on the Board of Directors of the Overseas Private Investment Corporation, U.S. International Development Cooperation Agency, announcing that he has nominated Gordon Giffin to serve on that Board and that he intends to nominate John Chrystal, George J. Kourpias, and Lottie Shackelford.
"These nominations will strengthen this important foreign assistance Agency," said the President. "I look to these four individuals to provide leadership in helping American businesses compete more effectively overseas."
