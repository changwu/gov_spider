
    Charleston, WV - Elaine Harris, a prominent West Virginia labor leader released the following statement responding to tonight's presidential debate in Philadelphia.
"In tonight's debate, it was clear that Hillary Clinton is the best choice for West Virginia. Clinton is a proven leader who is ready to take on the tough challenges we face. She has the strength and experience to deliver solutions that will turn our economy around, create good jobs, rebuild the middle class, and help working families get ahead. We need a President who understands the challenges West Virginians face and will work hard to strengthen our communities and create opportunities for all Americans."
