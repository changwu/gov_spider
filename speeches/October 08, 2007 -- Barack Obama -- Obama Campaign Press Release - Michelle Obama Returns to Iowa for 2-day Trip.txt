
    Des Moines, IA -0 The Obama for America Campaign today announced that Michelle Obama will return to Iowa for events in Waterloo, Iowa Falls, Hampton, Eagle Grove, Fort Dodge, Rockwell City, Jefferson, and Boone. This week marks Michelle's 10th visit to Iowa since her husband announced his candidacy. Over her two-day trip to Iowa, Michelle will meet with local activists and community members and discuss Senator Obama's commitment to challenging the conventional thinking in Washington, D.C. and transforming our country.
