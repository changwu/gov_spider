
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Henry R. Folsom to be a Commissioner of the Postal Rate Commission for the term expiring October 14, 1988. This is a reappointment.
Mr. Folsom is currently serving as Commissioner, Postal Rate Commission. Previously he was consultant to the Water Resources Agency of New Castle County, Del., and also was adjunct professor in urban affairs and public policy, University of Delaware. He was with E.I. du Pont de Nemours & Co. in 1937-1973, where he served in a number of positions including construction engineer, field project manager, and senior process engineer.
He graduated from the University of Delaware (B.S., 1936). He is married and resides in Hockessin, Del. He was born July 19, 1913.
