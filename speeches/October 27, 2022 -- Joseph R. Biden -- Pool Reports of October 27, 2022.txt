October 27, 202215:06

Travel pool report #5 -tour
At Onondaga Community College, Biden is participating in a tour of Micron Pavilion, led by Micron President and CEO Sanjay Mehrotra, and President of Central & Northern New York Trades Council, Greg Lancette.
Gov. Hochul, Majority Leader Schumer, and Sen. Gillibrand joined the tour.
The tour started 2:52pm.
Biden was introduced to an Air Force veteran, who went to Syracuse and works at Micron.
"Me too," Biden said about Syracuse.
Biden looked at a replica of the future Micron semiconductor facility.
"It's good it's coming home because we invented the suckers," he said, which Gillibrand laughed at.
Biden added, "Pretty good, man" and said that the facility will bring "Incredibly good salaries."
The group was then brought over to look at fabrication equipment.
Pool was ushered out of the room at 3:01pm. We are holding in the room where Biden will deliver his remarks.
Also should mention, Schumer is wearing orange socks that are presumably Syracuse socks. He showed them to Biden on the tarmac.
