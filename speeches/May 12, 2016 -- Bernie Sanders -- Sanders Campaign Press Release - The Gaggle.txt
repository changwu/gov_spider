
    Bernie Sanders campaigned across South Dakota on Thursday. He began the day at Mt. Rushmore, drove to the Pine Ridge reservation and spoke at an outdoor rally of some 3,500 supporters at a park in Rapid City before heading to a second rally in Sioux Falls.
After the Rapid City rally, He talked about what he saw at the Pine Ridge reservation. He answered questions about Donald Trump's trip to Capitol Hill. And he spoke about his quest for delegates in the contest with Hillary Clinton for the Democratic Party nomination for president.
