
    The President today announced the appointment of three persons as members of the Advisory Committee on the Arts (John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts). They are:
RALPH HORNBLOWER III, a Washington, D.C., attorney who has studied voice and has sung with the Washington Opera;
G. JOSEPH MINNETT, a Washington, D.C., attorney and former member of the Civil Aeronautics Board;
ANN K. REGAN, of Billings, Mont., a Montana State senator and junior high school teacher.
