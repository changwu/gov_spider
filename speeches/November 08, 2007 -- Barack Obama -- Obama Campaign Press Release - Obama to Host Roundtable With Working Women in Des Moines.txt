
    Des Moines, IA -- On Friday, November 9, Senator Barack Obama will host a roundtable discussion with local women about his plan to reclaim the American Dream. Obama's plan, unveiled this week in Bettendorf, includes guaranteeing paid sick days, expanding the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA), supporting paid FMLA, and doubling funding for after-school programs.
