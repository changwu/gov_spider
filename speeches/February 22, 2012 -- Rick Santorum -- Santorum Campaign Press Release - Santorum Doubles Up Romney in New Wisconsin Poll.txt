
    Verona, PA - In a newly released Wisconsin Republican Presidential poll, Rick Santorum received nearly double the support of former Massachusetts Governor Mitt Romney, continuing his trend of leading Romney in the general election must-win battleground "Rust Belt" states.

Gingrich:           12%
