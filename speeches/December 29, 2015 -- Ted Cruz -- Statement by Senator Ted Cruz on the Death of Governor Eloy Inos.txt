
    HOUSTON, Texas — Today, Presidential candidate Ted Cruz released the following statement offering prayers and condolences on the passing of Govenor Inos.
"Governor Eloy Songao Inos was a faithful public servant and important leader in the Northern Mariana Islands. Heidi and I send our condolences to his family and to our fellow Americans in the Marianas. Our prayers are with them as they mourn their loss."
