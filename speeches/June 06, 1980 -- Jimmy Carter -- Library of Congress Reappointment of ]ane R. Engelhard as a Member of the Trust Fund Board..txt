
    The President today announced the reappointment of Jane R. Engelhard, of Far Hills, N.J., as a member of the Library of Congress Trust Fund Board.
Engelhard is chairman of the board of Engelhard Hanovia, Inc., director of Engelhard Minerals and Chemicals Corp., and was executive vice president of Holbrook Microfilming Co., which microfilmed records of the Library of Congress during World War II.
