
    The following clauses of Schedule A are revoked: XIV-1, XVI-1, XVII-1, XX-1, XZXI-1, XXII-1; and subdivision I is amended by the addition of clause 17 as follows:
17. One private secretary or confidential clerk to the head of each independent establishment, to the assistant to the head of each independent establishment, and to each member of commissions or boards; when such heads, assistants, or members are appointed by the President.
HERBERT HOOVER
The White House,March 31, 1931.
