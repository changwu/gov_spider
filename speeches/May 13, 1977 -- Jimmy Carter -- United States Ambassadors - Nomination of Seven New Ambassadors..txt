
    The President today announced six persons who will be nominated to be Ambassadors. They are:
DONALD C. BERGUS, to be Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Sudan;
LAWRENCE S. EAGLEBURGER, to be Ambassador to the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia;
ARTHUR A. HARTMAN, to be Ambassador to France;
JAMES G. LOWENSTEIN, to be Ambassador to Luxembourg;
ROBERT H. MILLER, to be Ambassador to Malaysia; and
HARRY W. SHLAUDEMAN, to be Ambassador to Peru.
The President also announced that he will nominate Philip H. Alston, Jr., now Ambassador to Australia, to serve concurrently and without additional compensation as Ambassador to the Republic of Nauru.
