
    Today Kasich for America announced a major statewide TV and digital ad buy in Ohio.
"Rise," which will begin airing statewide on Tuesday, praises the tenacious and innovative spirit of Ohioans who worked with Gov. Kasich to pull the economy out of a deep hole and give people hope again. The ad challenges Ohioans to rally behind John Kasich again in order to change America like they've changed Ohio.
