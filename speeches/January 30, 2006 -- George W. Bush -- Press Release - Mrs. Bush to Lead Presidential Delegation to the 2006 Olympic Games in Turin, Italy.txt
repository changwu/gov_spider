
    President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the 2006 Olympic Games in Turin, Italy.
Mrs. Laura Bush will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation are:
The Honorable Ronald P. Spogli, U.S. Ambassador to ItalyMiss Barbara BushMr. Roland Betts, Founder and Chairman of Chelsea Piers, L.P.Mr. Brad Freeman, Founder Freeman Spogli & Co.Ms. Dorothy Hamill, Olympic Gold Medalist and Member of the U.S. Olympic Hall of FameDr. Eric Heiden, Olympic Gold Medalist and Member of the U.S. Olympic Hall of FameMs. Kerri Strug, Olympic Gold MedalistDr. Debi Thomas, Olympic Bronze MedalistMr. Herschel Walker, Former Olympian, Professional Football Player & Heisman Trophy Winner
