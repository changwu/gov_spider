
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Audrey A. Kaslow of California to be a Commissioner of the U.S. Parole Commission for the term of 6 years.
Ms. Kaslow has been employed by the Los Angeles County Probation Department since 1950 and is now a probation director.
A native of Miami, Ariz., Ms. Kaslow holds a bachelor's degree from the University of California and a master's degree from the University of Southern California.
She has served as a special representative on the California Fair Employment Practices Commission.
