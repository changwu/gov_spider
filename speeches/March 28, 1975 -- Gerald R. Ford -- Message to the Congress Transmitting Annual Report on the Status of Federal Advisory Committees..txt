
    To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the provisions of section 6(c) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the report on the status of advisory committees in 1974 is herewith forwarded.
This is the third annual report. It is organized to improve public access to information concerning specific advisory committees and the Federal agencies to whom they provide advice.
GERALD R. FORD
The White House,
March 28, 1975.
