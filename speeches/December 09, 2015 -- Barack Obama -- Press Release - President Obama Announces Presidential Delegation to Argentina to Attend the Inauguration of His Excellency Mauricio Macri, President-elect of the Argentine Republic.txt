
    President Barack Obama today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Argentina to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Mauricio Macri, President-elect of the Argentine Republic, on December 10, 2015.
The Honorable Anthony Foxx, Secretary of Transportation, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Noah Mamet, U.S. Ambassador to Argentina, Department of State
The Honorable Roberta S. Jacobson, Assistant Secretary of State for Western Hemisphere Affairs, Department of State
