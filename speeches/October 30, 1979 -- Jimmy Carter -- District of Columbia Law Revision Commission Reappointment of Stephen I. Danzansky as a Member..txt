
    The President today announced that he has reappointed Stephen I. Danzansky as a member of the District of Columbia Law Revision Commission for a 4-year term.
Danzansky, 40, is a Washington attorney who has served on the board of directors of Self Determination for D.C. Metropolitan Commission and serves as special counsel to the Citizen Coalition for Planning and Zoning Reform of the District of Columbia.
