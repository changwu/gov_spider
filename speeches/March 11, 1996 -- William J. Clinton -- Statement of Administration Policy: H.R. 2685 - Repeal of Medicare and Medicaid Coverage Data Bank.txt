
    STATEMENT OF ADMINISTRATION POLICYThis Statement Has Been Coordinated by OMB with the Appropriate Agencies
(House)(Thomas (R) CA, Bilirakis (R) FL and Dickey (R) AR)
The Administration supports enactment of H.R. 2685.
Pay-As-You-Go Scoring
H.R. 2685 affects programs that are subject to the pay-as-you-go requirement of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1990. OMB's preliminary scoring estimate of the bill is zero.
