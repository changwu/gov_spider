
    On Friday, October 28, Second Gentleman Douglas Emhoff will travel to Houma, Louisiana to attend the Keel Laying Ceremony for the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's ship Discoverer. The Discoverer is a new oceanographic research vessel which incorporates the latest clean energy technologies and will support a wide variety of missions, ranging from general oceanographic research and exploration to studies of marine life, climate, and ocean ecosystems. Mr. Emhoff is the sponsor of the ship which will launch in 2026. For interested media, please RSVP HERE by Thursday, October 27th at 12:00 PM ET.
