In-town pool report #1 - Gathering 
Morning! Welcome to Sunday.
Pool was called to gather at 11.50am (for a noon final gather) at the White House ahead of the president's return from Camp David. Marine One is expected to land at Fort Lesley J. McNair, near Nationals Park, at 1.15pm before motorcading back to 1600 Pennsylvania Ave.
Feel free to reach out with any questions.



November 14, 202113:34

In-town pool #2 - Arrival at Fort McNair 
M1 touched down at Ft. McNair at 1.24pm.
POTUS traveled with, according to the White House:
*Annie Tomasini, Assistant to the President & Director of Oval Office Operations
*Yohannes Abraham, Deputy Assistant to the President & Chief of Staff and Executive Secretary for the NSC*Stephen Goepfert, Special Assistant to the President & Personal Aide to the President
POTUS, wearing a light blue shirt and dark suit jacket without a tie, did not respond to shouted questions about his thoughts on Trump ally Steve Bannon's indictment, whether his climate deal was aggressive enough, and the status of paid family leave in the reconciliation bill.
He loaded into his waiting motorcade at 1.24pm.
Pool in now rolling back to the White House.
