
    On Thursday, September 5th, Vice President Joe Biden will travel to Orlando, Florida, for an event at the University of Central Florida to discuss the importance of ensuring that every American has the opportunity to attain a quality education. The Vice President will highlight the plan that President Obama laid out last week to make college more affordable, tackle rising costs, and improve the value of higher education for students and their families as part of his commitment to offering a better bargain for the middle class.
Additional details about the Vice President's event are forthcoming.
