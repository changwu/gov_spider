
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Thomas A. Dine as Assistant Administrator for Europe and the Newly Independent States and Jill B. Buckley as Assistant Administrator for Legislation and Public Affairs at the Agency for International Development, U.S. International Development Cooperation Agency.
"Thomas Dine and Jill Buckley are each experienced in their fields, and I am confident they will work hard to pursue AID's important goals," the President said.
