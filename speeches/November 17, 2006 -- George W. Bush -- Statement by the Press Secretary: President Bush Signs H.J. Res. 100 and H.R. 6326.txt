
    On Friday, November 17, 2006 the President signed into law:
H.J. Res. 100, which provides FY 2007 appropriations for continuing projects and activities of the Federal Government through Friday, December 8, 2006; and
H.R. 6326, which authorizes the Department of Agriculture to fulfill certain commodity orders for FY 2007 placed prior to November 14, 2006, under the Nutrition Services Incentive Program.
