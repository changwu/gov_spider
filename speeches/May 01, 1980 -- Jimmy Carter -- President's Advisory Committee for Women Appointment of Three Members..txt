
    The President today announced the appointment of three persons to be members of the President's Advisory Committee for Women. They are:
CATHERINE. CONROY, of Milwaukee, Wis., a member and international representative of the Communication Workers of America and an executive board member of the Milwaukee County Labor Council (AFL-CIO);
ANTOINETTE L. LEONE, of Hartford, Conn., an attorney and a member of the Hartford City Council;
ELENA MARTINEZ, of Chicago, a psychiatric social worker and the administrator of the Association of Latinoamericana de Evanston, in Evanston, Ill.
