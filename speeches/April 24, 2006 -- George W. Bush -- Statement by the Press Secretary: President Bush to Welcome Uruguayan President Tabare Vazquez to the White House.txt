
    President Bush will welcome Uruguayan President Tabare Vazquez to the White House on May 4, 2006. The meeting will provide the two leaders with an opportunity to share views on topics of mutual interest. The President looks forward to discussing with President Vazquez a full range of bilateral and international issues, including Uruguay's important contributions to global peacekeeping efforts, especially in Haiti, and our shared goals of free trade and the rule of law in the hemisphere.
