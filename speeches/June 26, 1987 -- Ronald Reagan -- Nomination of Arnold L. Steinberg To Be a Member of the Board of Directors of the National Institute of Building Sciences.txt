
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Arnold L. Steinberg to be a member of the Board of Directors of the National Institute of Building Sciences for a term expiring September 7, 1988. He would succeed Van Norden Logan.
Since 1979 Mr. Steinberg has been president of Arnold Steinberg & Associates, Inc., in Sherman Oaks, CA. Prior to this he served as a management consultant for political campaigns.
Mr. Steinberg graduated from George Washington University (B.A., 1969) and Pepperdine University (M.B.A., 1975). He was born October 23, 1947, in Los Angeles, CA. Mr. Steinberg currently resides in Sherman Oaks, CA.
