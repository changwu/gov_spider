
    On Monday, July 30, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 6042, which delays for one year the reduction of Federal medical assistance percentage for Medicaid personal visits furnished without an electronic visit verification system; and
S. 2692, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4558 Broadway in New York, New York, as the Stanley Michels Post Office Building.
