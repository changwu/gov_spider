
    The President today announced the reappointment of five persons as members of the National Advisory Council on the Education of Disadvantaged Children. They are:
CLEO HOLT, a public school reading teacher in Mt. Vernon, Ill.;
ANNETTE DROZ FUENTES, a classroom teacher in the Bronx, N.Y., public schools and a specialist in bilingual education;
ROBERT H. KOFF, dean of the School of Education, State University of New York at Albany;
LUCILLE L. SANTOS, deputy assistant superintendent of special programs with the San Antonio (Texas) Independent School District;
RICHARD ST. GERMAINE, tribal chairman and director of education of the Lac Courte Oreilles Tribe in Hayward, Wis.
