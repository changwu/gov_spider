
    On Friday, September 21, 2018, the President signed into law:
H.R. 5895, the "Energy and Water, Legislative Branch, and Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriations Act, 2019," which provides full-year funding through September 30, 2019, for projects and activities of the Federal Government included in the following appropriations bills: Energy and Water Development and Related Agencies Appropriations bill (Division A); Legislative Branch Appropriations bill Division B); and Military Construction, Veterans Affairs, and Related Agencies Appropriations bill (Division C).
