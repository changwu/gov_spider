
    On Friday, November 30, 2007, the President signed into law:
H.R. 2089, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Louisiana Armed Services Veterans Post Office;
H.R. 2276, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Corporal Christopher E. Esckelson Post Office Building;
H.R. 3297, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Nate DeTample Post Office Building;
H.R. 3307, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Dennis P. Collins Post Office Building;
H.R. 3308, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Lance Corporal David K. Fribley Post Office;
H.R. 3325, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Corporal Stephen R. Bixler Post Office;
H.R. 3382, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Philip A. Baddour, Sr. Post Office;
H.R. 3446, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Michael W. Schragg Post Office Building;
H.R. 3518, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Charles H. Hendrix Post Office Building;
H.R. 3530, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Chief Warrant Officer Aaron Weaver Post Office Building; and
H.R. 2572, which designates a facility of the United States Postal Service, as the Wallace S. Hartsfield Post Office Building.
