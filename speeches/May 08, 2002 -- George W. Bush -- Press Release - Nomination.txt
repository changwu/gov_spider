
    President Bush Today Announced His Nomination of Anthony Dichio to be United States Marshal for the District of Massachusetts
President George W. Bush today announced his nomination of Anthony Dichio to be United States Marshal for the District of Massachusetts. Mr. Dichio has served as a Trooper with the Massachusetts State Police since 1980, and he has been a member of the Executive Protection Unit since 1991. Mr. Dichio earned an associate's degree from Bunker Hill Community College and a bachelor's degree from Curry College.
