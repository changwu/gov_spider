
    The President today announced his intention to nominate Harold T. Duryee to be Federal Insurance Administrator, Federal Emergency Management Agency. He would succeed Jeffrey S. Bragg.
Since 1984 Mr. Duryee has been Executive Deputy to the Federal Insurance Administrator at the Federal Emergency Management Agency. Previously he was deputy administrator, Ohio Bureau of Workers' Compensation, in Columbus from 1977 to 1984.
Mr. Duryee graduated from Kenyon College (A.B., 1951). He is married and resides in Washington, DC. Mr. Duryee was born February 11, 1930, in Willoughby, OH.
